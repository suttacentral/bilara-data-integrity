1.3.1.6 Saṁyojaniyasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Yogakkhemivagga
Saṁyojaniyasutta
109.
598“Saṁyojaniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi saṁyojanañca. Taṁ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyā dhammā, katamañca saṁyojanaṁ? Cakkhuṁ, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyo dhammo. Yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha saṁyojanaṁ…pe…  jivhā saṁyojaniyo dhammo…pe…  mano saṁyojaniyo dhammo. Yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha saṁyojanaṁ. Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyā dhammā, idaṁ saṁyojanan”ti.
599Chaṭṭhaṁ.
