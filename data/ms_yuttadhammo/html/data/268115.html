2.2.7 Macchariyasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Satipaṭṭhānavagga
Macchariyasutta
69.
454“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, macchariyāni. Katamāni pañca? Āvāsamacchariyaṁ, kulamacchariyaṁ, lābhamacchariyaṁ, vaṇṇamacchariyaṁ, dhammamacchariyaṁ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca macchariyāni.
455Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ macchariyānaṁ pahānāya…pe…  ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.
456Sattamaṁ.
