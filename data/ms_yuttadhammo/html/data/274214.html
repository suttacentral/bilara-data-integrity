4.1.6 Rūpasaññāsutta
Okkantasaṁyutta
Cakkhuvagga
Rūpasaññāsutta
307.
1053Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Rūpasaññā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddasaññā…  gandhasaññā…  rasasaññā…  phoṭṭhabbasaññā…  dhammasaññā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī…pe…  sambodhiparāyano’”ti.
1054Chaṭṭhaṁ.
