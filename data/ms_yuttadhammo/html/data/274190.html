3.1.17 Hoticanacahotitathāgatosutta
Diṭṭhisaṁyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Hoticanacahotitathāgatosutta
222.
969Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati— ‘hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe… niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
970Sattarasamaṁ.
