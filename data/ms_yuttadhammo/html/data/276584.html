1.7.6.3 Pattanillehakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
Pattanillehakasikkhāpada
629.
1950Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pattanillehakaṁ bhuñjanti…pe… .
1951“Na pattanillehakaṁ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (53:198)
1952Na pattanillehakaṁ bhuñjitabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca pattanillehakaṁ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1953Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, parittake sese ekato saṅkaḍḍhitvā nillehitvā bhuñjati, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1954Tatiyasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
