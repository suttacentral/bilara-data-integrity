2307Kilesaṁ akusalañca nokilesaṁ akusalañca dhammaṁ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
3.
2308Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (sabbattha nava), avigate nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
4.
2309Kilesaṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchaṁ paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccaṁ paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
2310Nokilesaṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
2311Kilesaṁ akusalañca nokilesaṁ akusalañca dhammaṁ paṭicca kileso akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate khandhe ca vicikicchañca paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato moho, uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca uddhaccañca paṭicca uddhaccasahagato moho. (1) (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
5.
2312Nahetuyā tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ, sahajātavārādi vitthāretabbo.)

2313Kileso akusalo dhammo kilesassa akusalassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
