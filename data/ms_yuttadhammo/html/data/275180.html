1.2.2
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Adhikaraṇavagga
12.
32“Dvemāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni dve? Paṭisaṅkhānabalañca bhāvanābalañca. Katamañca, bhikkhave, paṭisaṅkhānabalaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco iti paṭisañcikkhati— ‘kāyaduccaritassa kho pāpako vipāko diṭṭhe ceva dhamme abhisamparāyañca, vacīduccaritassa pāpako vipāko diṭṭhe ceva dhamme abhisamparāyañca, manoduccaritassa pāpako vipāko diṭṭhe ceva dhamme abhisamparāyañcā’ti. So iti paṭisaṅkhāya kāyaduccaritaṁ pahāya kāyasucaritaṁ bhāveti, vacīduccaritaṁ pahāya vacīsucaritaṁ bhāveti, manoduccaritaṁ pahāya manosucaritaṁ bhāveti, suddhaṁ attānaṁ pariharati. Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, paṭisaṅkhānabalaṁ.
33Katamañca, bhikkhave, bhāvanābalaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu satisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti vivekanissitaṁ virāganissitaṁ nirodhanissitaṁ vosaggapariṇāmiṁ, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti… vīriyasambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti… pītisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti… passaddhisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti… samādhisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti vivekanissitaṁ virāganissitaṁ nirodhanissitaṁ vosaggapariṇāmiṁ. Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, bhāvanābalaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve balānī”ti.
