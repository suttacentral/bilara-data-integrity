2.4.6 Yonisomanasikārasutta
Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta
Nīvaraṇavagga
Yonisomanasikārasutta
217.
511“Yoniso ca kho, bhikkhave, manasikaroto anuppanno ceva satisambojjhaṅgo uppajjati, uppanno ca satisambojjhaṅgo bhāvanāpāripūriṁ gacchati…pe…  anuppanno ceva upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo uppajjati, uppanno ca upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo bhāvanāpāripūriṁ gacchatī”ti.
512Chaṭṭhaṁ.
