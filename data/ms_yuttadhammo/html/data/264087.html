871Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
