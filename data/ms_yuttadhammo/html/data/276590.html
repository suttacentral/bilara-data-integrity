1.7.6.9 Satthapāṇisikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
Satthapāṇisikkhāpada
636.
1990Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū satthapāṇissa dhammaṁ desenti…pe….
1991“Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṁ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (59:204)
1992Satthaṁ nāma ekatodhāraṁ ubhatodhāraṁ paharaṇaṁ.
1993Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṁ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1994Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1995Navamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
