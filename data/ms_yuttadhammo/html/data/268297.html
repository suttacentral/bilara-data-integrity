4.3.2 Ariyadhammasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Ariyadhammasutta
179.
1324“Ariyadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anariyadhammañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, anariyo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anariyo dhammo.
1325Katamo ca, bhikkhave, ariyo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyo dhammo”ti.
1326Dutiyaṁ.
