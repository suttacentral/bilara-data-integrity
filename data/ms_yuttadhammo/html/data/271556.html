Anulomapaccanīka
11.
40๐Puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṁ vattabbe.
41Ājānāhi niggahaṁ. Hañci puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe— “sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṁ tattha vadesi— “vattabbe kho— ‘puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe— ‘sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.
42No ce pana vattabbe— “sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe— “puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṁ tattha vadesi— “vattabbe kho— ‘puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe— ‘sabbattha puggalo upalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe….
43Tatiyo niggaho.
