3.4.4 Atthasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Atthasutta
137.
1112“Atthañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anatthañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, anattho? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anattho. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, attho? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, attho”ti.
1113Catutthaṁ.
