4.1.10 Puggalasutta
Anamataggasaṁyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Puggalasutta
133.
853Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ. Bhagavā etadavoca— 
854“Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro…pe…  ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, kappaṁ sandhāvato saṁsarato siyā evaṁ mahā aṭṭhikaṅkalo aṭṭhipuñjo aṭṭhirāsi yathāyaṁ vepullo pabbato, sace saṁhārako assa, sambhatañca na vinasseyya. Taṁ kissa hetu? Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro…pe…  alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.
855Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṁ vatvāna sugato athāparaṁ etadavoca satthā— 
856
“Ekassekena kappena,
puggalassaṭṭhisañcayo;
Siyā pabbatasamo rāsi,
iti vuttaṁ mahesinā.
857
So kho panāyaṁ akkhāto,
vepullo pabbato mahā;
Uttaro gijjhakūṭassa,
magadhānaṁ giribbaje.
858
Yato ca ariyasaccāni,
sammappaññāya passati;
Dukkhaṁ dukkhasamuppādaṁ,
dukkhassa ca atikkamaṁ;
Ariyaṁ caṭṭhaṅgikaṁ maggaṁ,
dukkhūpasamagāminaṁ.
859
Sa sattakkhattuṁparamaṁ,
sandhāvitvāna puggalo;
Dukkhassantakaro hoti,
sabbasaṁyojanakkhayā”ti.
860Dasamaṁ.
861Paṭhamo vaggo.
862Tassuddānaṁ
863
Tiṇakaṭṭhañca pathavī,
assu khīrañca pabbataṁ;
Sāsapā sāvakā gaṅgā,
daṇḍo ca puggalena cāti.
