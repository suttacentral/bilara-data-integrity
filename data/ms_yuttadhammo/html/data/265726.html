4721Niyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca niyyāniko dhammo uppajjati napurejātapaccayā— arūpe niyyānikaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ…pe…. Niyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca aniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati napurejātapaccayā— niyyānike khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (2)
