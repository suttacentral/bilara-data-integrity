25 Vitakkattika, Kusalattika
Vitakkattika, Kusalattika
42.
116Savitakkasavicāraṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasavitakkasavicāro nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
117Avitakkavicāramattaṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naavitakkavicāramatto nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pannarasa, adhipatiyā pannarasa…pe…  avigate pannarasa.
118Savitakkasavicāraṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasavitakkasavicāro naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
