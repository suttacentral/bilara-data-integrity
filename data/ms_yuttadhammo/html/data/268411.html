2.1.9 Dutiyajananakuhanasutta
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dutiyajananakuhanasutta
36.
220Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ—
221“Nayidaṁ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ vussati janakuhanatthaṁ, na janalapanatthaṁ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṁsatthaṁ, na ‘iti maṁ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṁ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ vussati abhiññatthañceva pariññatthañcā”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati—
222
“Abhiññatthaṁ pariññatthaṁ,
brahmacariyaṁ anītihaṁ;
Adesayi so bhagavā,
nibbānogadhagāminaṁ.
223
Esa maggo mahattehi,
anuyāto mahesibhi;
Ye ye taṁ paṭipajjanti,
yathā buddhena desitaṁ;
Dukkhassantaṁ karissanti,
satthusāsanakārino”ti.
224Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
225Navamaṁ.
