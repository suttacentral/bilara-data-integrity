56.
3964Saṁkiliṭṭho dhammo saṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā saṁkiliṭṭhā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ saṁkiliṭṭhānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3965Saṁkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  saṁkiliṭṭhā khandhā vuṭṭhānassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)

3967Asaṁkiliṭṭho dhammo saṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  āvajjanā saṁkiliṭṭhānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)
