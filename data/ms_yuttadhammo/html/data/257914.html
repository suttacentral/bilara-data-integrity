Hetuduka, Parittattika

177Nahetuṁ naparittaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nanahetuṁ naparittaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
