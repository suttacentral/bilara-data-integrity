2.4.5 Anabhiratijātaka
Dukanipāta
Asadisavagga
Anabhiratijātaka
69.
450
“Yathodake āvile appasanne,
Na passati sippikasambukañca;
Sakkharaṁ vālukaṁ macchagumbaṁ,
Evaṁ āvilamhi citte;
Na passati attadatthaṁ paratthaṁ.
70.
451
Yathodake acche vippasanne,
So passati sippikasambukañca;
Sakkharaṁ vālukaṁ macchagumbaṁ,
Evaṁ anāvilamhi citte;
So passati attadatthaṁ paratthan”ti.
452Anabhiratijātakaṁ pañcamaṁ.
