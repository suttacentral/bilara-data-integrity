2.3.2.10.10 Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
1209.
1905Katame dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho— ime dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
1210.
1906Katame dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Cittañca, sabbañca rūpaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu— ime dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
