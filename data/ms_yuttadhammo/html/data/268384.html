1.1.9 Lobhapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Lobhapariññāsutta
9.
43Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ— 
44“Lobhaṁ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṁ aparijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ avirājayaṁ appajahaṁ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Lobhañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṁ parijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ virājayaṁ pajahaṁ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati— 
45
“Yena lobhena luddhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ;
Taṁ lobhaṁ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṁ lokaṁ kudācanan”ti.
46Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
47Navamaṁ.
