10.1 Suttantabhājanīya
Bojjhaṅgavibhaṅga
Suttantabhājanīya
466.
1555Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
467.
1556Tattha katamo satisambojjhaṅgo? Idha bhikkhu satimā hoti paramena satinepakkena samannāgato, cirakatampi cirabhāsitampi saritā hoti anussaritā—  ayaṁ vuccati “satisambojjhaṅgo”. (1)
1557So tathā sato viharanto taṁ dhammaṁ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaṁsamāpajjati—  ayaṁ vuccati “dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo”. (2)
1558Tassa taṁ dhammaṁ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaṁsamāpajjato āraddhaṁ hoti vīriyaṁ asallīnaṁ—  ayaṁ vuccati “vīriyasambojjhaṅgo”. (3)
1559Āraddhavīriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā—  ayaṁ vuccati “pītisambojjhaṅgo”. (4)
1560Pītimanassa kāyopi passambhati, cittampi passambhati—  ayaṁ vuccati “passaddhisambojjhaṅgo”. (5)
1561Passaddhakāyassa sukhino cittaṁ samādhiyati—  ayaṁ vuccati “samādhisambojjhaṅgo”. (6)
1562So tathā samāhitaṁ cittaṁ sādhukaṁ ajjhupekkhitā hoti—  ayaṁ vuccati “upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo”. (7)
468.
1563Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
469.
1564Tattha katamo satisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi ajjhattaṁ dhammesu sati, atthi bahiddhā dhammesu sati. Yadapi ajjhattaṁ dhammesu sati tadapi satisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi bahiddhā dhammesu sati tadapi satisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (1)
1565Tattha katamo dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo? Atthi ajjhattaṁ dhammesu pavicayo, atthi bahiddhā dhammesu pavicayo. Yadapi ajjhattaṁ dhammesu pavicayo tadapi dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi bahiddhā dhammesu pavicayo tadapi dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (2)
1566Tattha katamo vīriyasambojjhaṅgo? Atthi kāyikaṁ vīriyaṁ, atthi cetasikaṁ vīriyaṁ. Yadapi kāyikaṁ vīriyaṁ tadapi vīriyasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi cetasikaṁ vīriyaṁ tadapi vīriyasambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (3)
1567Tattha katamo pītisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi savitakkasavicārā pīti, atthi avitakkaavicārā pīti. Yadapi savitakkasavicārā pīti tadapi pītisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi avitakkaavicārā pīti tadapi pītisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (4)
1568Tattha katamo passaddhisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi kāyapassaddhi, atthi cittapassaddhi. Yadapi kāyapassaddhi tadapi passaddhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi cittapassaddhi tadapi passaddhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (5)
1569Tattha katamo samādhisambojjhaṅgo? Atthi savitakko savicāro samādhi, atthi avitakko avicāro samādhi. Yadapi savitakko savicāro samādhi tadapi samādhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi avitakko avicāro samādhi tadapi samādhisambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (6)
1570Tattha katamo upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo? Atthi ajjhattaṁ dhammesu upekkhā, atthi bahiddhā dhammesu upekkhā. Yadapi ajjhattaṁ dhammesu upekkhā tadapi upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. Yadapi bahiddhā dhammesu upekkhā tadapi upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṁvattati. (7)
470.
1571Satta bojjhaṅgā—  satisambojjhaṅgo, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo, vīriyasambojjhaṅgo, pītisambojjhaṅgo, passaddhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhisambojjhaṅgo, upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo.
471.
1572Tattha katamo satisambojjhaṅgo? Idha bhikkhu satisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti vivekanissitaṁ virāganissitaṁ nirodhanissitaṁ vossaggapariṇāmiṁ, dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti…pe…  vīriyasambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti…  pītisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti…  passaddhisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti…  samādhisambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṁ bhāveti vivekanissitaṁ virāganissitaṁ nirodhanissitaṁ vossaggapariṇāmiṁ.
1573Suttantabhājanīyaṁ.
