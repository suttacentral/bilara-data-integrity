2.4.2.10.4 Cittasampayuttaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasampayuttaduka
1531.
2242Katame dhammā cittasampayuttā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho— ime dhammā cittasampayuttā.
1532.
2243Katame dhammā cittavippayuttā? Rūpañca, nibbānañca— ime dhammā cittavippayuttā. Cittaṁ na vattabbaṁ— cittena sampayuttantipi, cittena vippayuttantipi.
