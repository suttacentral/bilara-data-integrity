106.
3786Upādāno ceva upādānasampayutto ca dhammo upādānassa ceva upādānasampayuttassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo… tīṇi. (3)
3787Upādānasampayutto ceva no ca upādāno dhammo upādānasampayuttassa ceva no ca upādānassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. (Tīṇipi, tīsupi dvepi adhipati kātabbā, ghaṭanādhipatipi tīṇi.)
