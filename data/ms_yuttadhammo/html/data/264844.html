133.
852Lokiyaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokiyaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, vatthuṁ paṭicca khandhā, ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ, kaṭattārūpaṁ, upādārūpaṁ. (1)
134.
853Lokuttaraṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokuttaraṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
854Lokuttaraṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokuttare khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (2)
855Lokuttaraṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo ca lokuttaro ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā—  lokuttaraṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
856Lokiyañca lokuttarañca dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lokuttare khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) (1)
