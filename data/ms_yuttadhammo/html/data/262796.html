2886Asaṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesiko dhammo asaṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesikassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  asaṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesikā indriyā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  cakkhundriyaṁ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṁ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. Rūpajīvitindriyaṁ—  kaṭattārūpānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo.
