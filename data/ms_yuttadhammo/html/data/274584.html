316“Tayome, bhikkhave, bhavā. Katame tayo? Kāmabhavo, rūpabhavo, arūpabhavo— ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo bhavā. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṁ bhavānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe… ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
