2.2.9 Khayadhammasutta
Rādhasaṁyutta
Dutiyamāravagga
Khayadhammasutta
178.
864Sāvatthinidānaṁ. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca—  “‘khayadhammo, khayadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, khayadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṁ kho, rādha, khayadhammo, vedanā khayadhammo, saññā khayadhammo, saṅkhārā khayadhammo, viññāṇaṁ khayadhammo. Evaṁ passaṁ…pe…  nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
865Navamaṁ.
