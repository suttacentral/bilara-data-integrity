Sanidassanattika, Āsavaduka
101.
249Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho noāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho noāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe… anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho noāsavo ca naanidassanasappaṭigho noāsavo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe… avigate cha.

Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ noāsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanasappaṭigho nanoāsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.
