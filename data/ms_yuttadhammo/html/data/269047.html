2.1.4 Uragajātaka
Dukanipāta
Daḷhavagga
Uragajātaka
7.
349
“Idhūragānaṁ pavaro paviṭṭho,
Selassa vaṇṇena pamokkhamicchaṁ;
Brahmañca vaṇṇaṁ apacāyamāno,
Bubhukkhito no vitarāmi bhottuṁ”.
8.
350
“So brahmagutto cirameva jīva,
Dibyā ca te pātubhavantu bhakkhā;
Yo brahmavaṇṇaṁ apacāyamāno,
Bubhukkhito no vitarāsi bhottun”ti.
351Uragajātakaṁ catutthaṁ.
