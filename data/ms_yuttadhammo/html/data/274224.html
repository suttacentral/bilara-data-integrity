5.1.6 Saññāsutta
Uppādasaṁyutta
Uppādavagga
Saññāsutta
317.
1079Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe…  yo dhammasaññāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe…  yo dhammasaññāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
1080Chaṭṭhaṁ.
