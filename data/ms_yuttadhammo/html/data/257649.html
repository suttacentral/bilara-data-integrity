34 Āsavasampayuttaduka, Kusalattika
Āsavasampayuttaduka, Kusalattika
43.
125Āsavavippayuttaṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṁ…pe… avigate ekaṁ.
126Āsavasampayuttaṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
127Āsavavippayuttaṁ abyākataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā naāsavasampayutto naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
