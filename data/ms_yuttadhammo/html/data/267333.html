20.9 Kiṁkaṇikapupphiyattheraapadāna
Tamālapupphiyavagga
Kiṁkaṇikapupphiyattheraapadāna
47.
2728
“Sumaṅgaloti nāmena,
sayambhū aparājito;
Pavanā nikkhamitvāna,
nagaraṁ pāvisī jino.
48.
2729
Piṇḍacāraṁ caritvāna,
nikkhamma nagarā muni;
Katakiccova sambuddho,
so vasī vanamantare.
49.
2730
Kiṁkaṇipupphaṁ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṁ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
sayambhussa mahesino.
50.
2731
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṁ pupphamabhiropayiṁ;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
51.
2732
Chaḷāsītimhito kappe,
apilāsisanāmako;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
52.
2733
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe…
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
2734Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā kiṁkaṇikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2735Kiṁkaṇikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṁ navamaṁ.
