27.3 Aḍḍhacandiyattheraapadāna
Padumukkhipavagga
Aḍḍhacandiyattheraapadāna
10.
3171
“Tissassa kho bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Aḍḍhacandaṁ mayā dinnaṁ,
dharaṇīruhapādape.
11.
3172
Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṁ candamabhiropayiṁ;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
12.
3173
Pañcavīse ito kappe,
devalo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
13.
3174
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe…
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
3175Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā aḍḍhacandiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3176Aḍḍhacandiyattherassāpadānaṁ tatiyaṁ.
