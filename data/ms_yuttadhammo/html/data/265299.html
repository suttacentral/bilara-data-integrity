Nakamma
244.
2921Cittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca cittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— cittasahabhū khandhe paṭicca cittasahabhū cetanā.
2922Nocittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— bāhiraṁ… āhārasamuṭṭhānaṁ… utusamuṭṭhānaṁ…pe….
2923Nocittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca cittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— cittaṁ paṭicca sampayuttakā cetanā.
2924Cittasahabhuñca nocittasahabhuñca dhammaṁ paṭicca cittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— cittasahabhū khandhe ca cittañca paṭicca sampayuttakā cetanā.
