4.4.6 Āsevitabbadhammasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyamaggavagga
Āsevitabbadhammasutta
194.
1370“Āsevitabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi nāsevitabbañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, nāsevitabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, nāsevitabbo dhammo.
1371Katamo ca, bhikkhave, āsevitabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, āsevitabbo dhammo”ti.
1372Chaṭṭhaṁ.
