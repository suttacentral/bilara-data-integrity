48 Sanidassanattika, Hetusampayuttaduka
Sanidassanattika, Hetusampayuttaduka
91.
227Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetusampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetusampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetusampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetusampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetusampayutto ca naanidassanasappaṭigho nahetusampayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
228Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetuvippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetuvippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
