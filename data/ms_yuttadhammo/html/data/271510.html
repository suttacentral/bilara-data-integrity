2.4.2.12.7 Kilesakilesasampayuttaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesakilesasampayuttaduka
1577.
2288Katame dhammā kilesā ceva kilesasampayuttā ca? Yattha dve tayo kilesā ekato uppajjanti—  ime dhammā kilesā ceva kilesasampayuttā ca.
1578.
2289Katame dhammā kilesasampayuttā ceva no ca kilesā? Ṭhapetvā kilese avasesaṁ akusalaṁ—  ime dhammā kilesasampayuttā ceva no ca kilesā. Kilesavippayuttā dhammā na vattabbā—  kilesā ceva kilesasampayuttā cātipi, kilesasampayuttā ceva no ca kilesātipi.
