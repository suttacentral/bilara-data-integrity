3.4.7 Sāvajjasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Sāvajjasutta
140.
1118“Sāvajjañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi anavajjañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāvajjo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāvajjo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anavajjo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anavajjo dhammo”ti.
1119Sattamaṁ.
