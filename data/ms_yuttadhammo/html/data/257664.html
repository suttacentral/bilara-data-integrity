Ajjhattikaduka, Kusalattika
58.
164Ajjhattikaṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhattiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bāhiraṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhattiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Ajjhattikaṁ kusalañca bāhiraṁ kusalañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhattiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
