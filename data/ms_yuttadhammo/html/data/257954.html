12 Kusalattika, Pariyāpannaduka
Kusalattika, Pariyāpannaduka
32.
73Nakusalaṁ napariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naakusalaṁ napariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naabyākataṁ napariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato pariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe… vipāke tīṇi…pe… avigate pañca.
74Nakusalaṁ naapariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā kusalo apariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nakusalaṁ naapariyāpannaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā abyākato apariyāpanno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… dve. (Akusalamūle dve, dukamūle dve.) Hetuyā cha… vipāke tīṇi…pe… avigate cha.
