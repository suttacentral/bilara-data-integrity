2.4.1.22 Sanidassanattika
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Tikaatthuddhāra
Sanidassanattika
1438.
2144Katame dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighā? Rūpāyatanaṁ— ime dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighā.
1439.
2145Katame dhammā anidassanasappaṭighā? Cakkhāyatanaṁ…pe… phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṁ— ime dhammā anidassanasappaṭighā.
1440.
2146Katame dhammā anidassanaappaṭighā? Catūsu bhūmīsu kusalaṁ, akusalaṁ, catūsu bhūmīsu vipāko, tīsu bhūmīsu kiriyābyākataṁ, yañca rūpaṁ anidassanaṁ appaṭighaṁ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṁ, nibbānañca— ime dhammā anidassanaappaṭighā.
2147Tikaṁ.
