1.28 Hetuduka, Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
52.
150Hetuṁ cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha nava.)
151Nahetuṁ nocittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu nocittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṁ. Sabbattha vitthāro.)
