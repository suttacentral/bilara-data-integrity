2760Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā tīṇi, naanantare tīṇi, nasamanantare tīṇi, naaññamaññe ekaṁ, naupanissaye tīṇi (sabbattha tīṇi), nasampayutte ekaṁ, navippayutte ekaṁ, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.
