3.2.19 Rūpīattāsutta
Diṭṭhisaṁyutta
Dutiyagamanavagga
Rūpīattāsutta
242.
990Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe… 
991“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti. Vedanāya sati…pe…  saññāya sati…  saṅkhāresu sati…  viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti.
992Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti? “Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…  vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “No hetaṁ, bhante”. “Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Vedanā…pe…  “no hetaṁ, bhante”. “Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.
993Ekūnavīsatimaṁ.
