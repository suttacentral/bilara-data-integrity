459» Yassa kāyasaṅkhāro na nirujjhati tassa vacīsaṅkhāro na nirujjhitthāti? Nirujjhittha.

462« Yassa vā pana cittasaṅkhāro na nirujjhittha tassa kāyasaṅkhāro na nirujjhatīti? Natthi.
