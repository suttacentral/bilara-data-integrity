2.1.5 Paṭhamasīlasutta
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Paṭhamasīlasutta
32.
198Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ— 
199“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye. Katamehi dvīhi? Pāpakena ca sīlena, pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati— 
200
“Pāpakena ca sīlena,
pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;
Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,
yo samannāgato naro;
Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,
nirayaṁ sopapajjatī”ti.
201Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
202Pañcamaṁ.
