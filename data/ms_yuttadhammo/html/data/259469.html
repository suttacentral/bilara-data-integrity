12.7.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesakilesasampayuttaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
97.
4051Kilesañceva kilesasampayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca kileso ceva kilesasampayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  lobhaṁ paṭicca moho diṭṭhi thinaṁ uddhaccaṁ ahirikaṁ anottappaṁ. (Kilesasaṁkiliṭṭhadukasadisaṁ ninnānākaraṇaṁ, sabbe vārā.)
4052Kilesakilesasampayuttadukaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
