2.6 Chakkapuggalapaññatti
Niddesa
Chakkapuggalapaññatti
202.
512Tatra yvāyaṁ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṁ saccāni abhisambujjhati, tattha ca sabbaññutaṁ pāpuṇāti balesu ca vasībhāvaṁ, sammāsambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (1)
513Tatra yvāyaṁ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṁ saccāni abhisambujjhati, na ca tattha sabbaññutaṁ pāpuṇāti na ca balesu vasībhāvaṁ, paccekasambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (2)
514Tatra yvāyaṁ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṁ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, sāvakapāramiñca pāpuṇāti, sāriputtamoggallānā tena daṭṭhabbā. (3)
515Tatra yvāyaṁ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṁ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, na ca sāvakapāramiṁ pāpuṇāti, avasesā arahantā tena daṭṭhabbā. (4)
516Tatra yvāyaṁ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṁ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, anāgāmī hoti anāgantā itthattaṁ, anāgāmī tena daṭṭhabbo. (5)
517Tatra yvāyaṁ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṁ saccāni anabhisambujjhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme dukkhassantakaro hoti, āgantā itthattaṁ, sotāpannasakadāgāmino tena daṭṭhabbā. (6)
518Chakkaniddeso.
