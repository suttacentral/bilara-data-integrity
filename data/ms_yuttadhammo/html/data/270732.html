2.3.1.13 Parittārammaṇattika
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Tikanikkhepa
Parittārammaṇattika
1029.
1694Katame dhammā parittārammaṇā? Paritte dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā— ime dhammā parittārammaṇā.
1030.
1695Katame dhammā mahaggatārammaṇā? Mahaggate dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā— ime dhammā mahaggatārammaṇā.
1031.
1696Katame dhammā appamāṇārammaṇā? Appamāṇe dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā— ime dhammā appamāṇārammaṇā.
