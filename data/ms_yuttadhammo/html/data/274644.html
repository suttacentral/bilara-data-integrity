2.5.3 Mārasutta
Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta
Cakkavattivagga
Mārasutta
224.
536“Mārasenappamaddanaṁ vo, bhikkhave, maggaṁ desessāmi; taṁ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo? Yadidaṁ—  satta bojjhaṅgā. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—  ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo”ti.
537Tatiyaṁ.
