Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, Sārammaṇaduka
44.
127Hetuṁ sārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
128Nahetuṁ sārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
129Hetuṁ sārammaṇañca nahetuṁ sārammaṇañca dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu sārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
130Hetuyā nava…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
131Nahetuṁ anārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha ekaṁ.)
