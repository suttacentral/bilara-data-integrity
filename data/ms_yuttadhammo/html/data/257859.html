91.

215Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṁ.
92.
216Araṇo sanidassano dhammo naaraṇassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. Araṇo sanidassano dhammo nasaraṇassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
