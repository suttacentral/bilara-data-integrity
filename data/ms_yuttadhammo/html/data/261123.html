Paṭiccādivāra
Hetu
103.
1842Ganthañceva ganthaniyañca dhammaṁ paṭicca gantho ceva ganthaniyo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  sīlabbataparāmāsaṁ kāyaganthaṁ paṭicca abhijjhākāyagantho, abhijjhākāyaganthaṁ paṭicca sīlabbataparāmāso kāyagantho, idaṁsaccābhinivesakāyaganthaṁ paṭicca abhijjhākāyagantho, abhijjhākāyaganthaṁ paṭicca idaṁsaccābhinivesakāyagantho. (1)
1843Ganthañceva ganthaniyañca dhammaṁ paṭicca ganthaniyo ceva no ca gantho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  ganthe paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ. (2)
1844Ganthañceva ganthaniyañca dhammaṁ paṭicca gantho ceva ganthaniyo ca ganthaniyo ceva no ca gantho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (1)
