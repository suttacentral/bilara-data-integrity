2.5.8 Attahitasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Asuravagga
Attahitasutta
98.
655“Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmiṁ. Katame cattāro? Attahitāya paṭipanno no parahitāya, parahitāya paṭipanno no attahitāya, nevattahitāya paṭipanno no parahitāya, attahitāya ceva paṭipanno parahitāya ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.
656Aṭṭhamaṁ.
