9.9 Codakenaupaṭṭhāpetabbadhamma
Pātimokkhaṭṭhapanakkhandhaka
Codakenaupaṭṭhāpetabbadhamma
400.
1935“Codakena, bhante, bhikkhunā paraṁ codetukāmena kati dhamme ajjhattaṁ upaṭṭhāpetvā paro codetabbo”ti? “Codakenupāli, bhikkhunā paraṁ codetukāmena pañca dhamme ajjhattaṁ upaṭṭhāpetvā paro codetabbo— kālena vakkhāmi, no akālena; bhūtena vakkhāmi, no abhūtena; saṇhena vakkhāmi, no pharusena; atthasaṁhitena vakkhāmi, no anatthasaṁhitena; mettacitto vakkhāmi, no dosantaroti. Codakenupāli, bhikkhunā paraṁ codetukāmena ime pañca dhamme ajjhattaṁ upaṭṭhāpetvā paro codetabbo”ti.
