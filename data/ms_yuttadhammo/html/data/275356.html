4.1.36--40
Peyyāla
Kodhapeyyāla
188.
283“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge. Katamehi dvīhi? Akkodhena ca anupanāhena ca…  amakkhena ca apaḷāsena ca…  anissāya ca amacchariyena ca…  amāyāya ca asāṭheyyena ca…  hiriyā ca ottappena ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge”.
