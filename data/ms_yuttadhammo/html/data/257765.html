60 Sanidassanattika, Āsavasampayuttaduka
Sanidassanattika, Āsavasampayuttaduka
103.
253Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe… anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavasampayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe… avigate cha.
254Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
