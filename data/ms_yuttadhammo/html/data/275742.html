5.2.10 Dussīlasutta
Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka
Parisāvagga
Dussīlasutta
220.
1394“Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye. Katamehi catūhi? Assaddho hoti, dussīlo hoti, kusīto hoti, duppañño hoti— imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye.
1395Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge. Katamehi catūhi? Saddho hoti, sīlavā hoti, āraddhavīriyo hoti, paññavā hoti— imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge”ti.
1396Dasamaṁ.
1397Parisasobhaṇavaggo dutiyo.
1398Tassuddānaṁ
1399
Parisā diṭṭhi akataññutā,

Ahirikaṁ duppaññena cāti.
