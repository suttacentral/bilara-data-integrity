30 Parittattika, Kusalattika
Parittattika, Kusalattika
39.
124Namahaggataṁ nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā mahaggato kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava…pe… avigate nava.
125Namahaggataṁ naakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā paritto akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
126Naparittaṁ naabyākataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe… avigate cha.
