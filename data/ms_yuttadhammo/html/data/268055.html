1.1.9 Puggalasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Sambodhivagga
Puggalasutta
9.
80“Navayime, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmiṁ. Katame nava? Arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, sotāpanno, sotāpattiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, puthujjano—  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.
81Navamaṁ.
