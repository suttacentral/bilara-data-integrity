1.1.6 Dutiyaājānīyasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Āhuneyyavagga
Dutiyaājānīyasutta
6.
22“Chahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato rañño bhadro assājānīyo rājāraho hoti rājabhoggo, rañño aṅgantveva saṅkhaṁ gacchati. Katamehi chahi? Idha, bhikkhave, rañño bhadro assājānīyo khamo hoti rūpānaṁ, khamo saddānaṁ, khamo gandhānaṁ, khamo rasānaṁ, khamo phoṭṭhabbānaṁ, balasampanno ca hoti. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, chahi aṅgehi samannāgato rañño bhadro assājānīyo rājāraho hoti rājabhoggo, rañño aṅgantveva saṅkhaṁ gacchati.
23Evamevaṁ kho, bhikkhave, chahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu āhuneyyo…pe…  anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassa. Katamehi chahi? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu khamo hoti rūpānaṁ…pe…  khamo dhammānaṁ. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, chahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu āhuneyyo hoti…pe…  anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassā”ti.
24Chaṭṭhaṁ.
