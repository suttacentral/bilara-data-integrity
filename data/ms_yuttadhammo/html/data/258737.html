Vedanāttika
Paṭiccādivāra
10.
42Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (3)
43Nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (3)
44Naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naadukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
