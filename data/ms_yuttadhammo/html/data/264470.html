315.
1026Adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya magga atthi avigatanti satta. Adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya indriya magga sampayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. Adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya magga vippayutta atthi vigatanti tīṇi. [Avipāka—  3]
1027Adhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya magga atthi avigatanti ekaṁ. Adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya vipāka indriya magga sampayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṁ. Adhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya magga vippayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṁ. [Savipāka—  3]
