1.7.3.1 Khambhakatasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Khambhakatasikkhāpada
596.
1785Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū khambhakatā antaraghare gacchanti…pe….
1786“Na khambhakato antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (21:166)
1787Na khambhakatena antaraghare gantabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā khambhaṁ katvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1788Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1789Paṭhamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
