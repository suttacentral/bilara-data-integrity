697Kusalaṁ dhammaṁ saṁsaṭṭho kusalo dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā…  adhipatipaccayā…  (adhipati paṭisandhikkhaṇe natthi.) Anantarapaccayā…  samanantarapaccayā…  sahajātapaccayā…  aññamaññapaccayā…  nissayapaccayā…  upanissayapaccayā. (Sabbāni padāni hetumūlakasadisāni.)
