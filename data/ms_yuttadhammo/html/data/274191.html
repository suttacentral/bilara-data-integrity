3.1.18 Nevahotinanahotitathāgatosutta
Diṭṭhisaṁyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Nevahotinanahotitathāgatosutta
223.
971Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati— ‘neva hoti, na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe…
972“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati— ‘neva hoti, na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti…pe….
973“Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti? “Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya— ‘neva hoti, na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “No hetaṁ, bhante”. “Yampidaṁ diṭṭhaṁ sutaṁ mutaṁ viññātaṁ pattaṁ pariyesitaṁ anuvicaritaṁ manasā tampi niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti? “Aniccaṁ, bhante”. “Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti? “Dukkhaṁ, bhante”. “Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya— ‘neva hoti, na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “No hetaṁ, bhante”.
974“Yato kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa imesu ca ṭhānesu kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhepissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhasamudayepissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhanirodhepissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāyapissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako sotāpanno avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti. Aṭṭhārasamaṁ.
975Sotāpattivaggo.
Aṭṭhārasaveyyākaraṇaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
976Tassuddānaṁ
977
Vātaṁ etaṁ mama,
so attā no ca me siyā;
Natthi karoto hetu ca,
mahādiṭṭhena aṭṭhamaṁ.
978
Sassato loko ca,
Asassato ca antavā ca;
Anantavā ca taṁ jīvaṁ taṁ sarīranti,
Aññaṁ jīvaṁ aññaṁ sarīranti ca.
979
Hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇāti,
Na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇāti;
Neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇāti.
