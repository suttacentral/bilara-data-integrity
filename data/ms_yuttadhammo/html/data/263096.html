11.
1897Parittaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— paritte khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe paritte khandhe paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṁ, khandhe paṭicca vatthu…pe… ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ…pe… bāhiraṁ… āhārasamuṭṭhānaṁ… utusamuṭṭhānaṁ… asaññasattānaṁ ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ…pe…. (1)
1898Mahaggataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— mahaggate khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe mahaggate khandhe paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṁ. (1)
1899Appamāṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— appamāṇe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (1)
1900Parittañca appamāṇañca dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— appamāṇe khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (1)
1901Parittañca mahaggatañca dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— mahaggate khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe mahaggate khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṁ. (1)
