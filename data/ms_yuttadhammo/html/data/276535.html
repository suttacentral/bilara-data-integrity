1.7.1.4 Dutiyasuppaṭicchannasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Dutiyasuppaṭicchannasikkhāpada
579.
1700Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kāyaṁ vivaritvā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe… .
1701“Suppaṭicchanno antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (4:149)
1702Suppaṭicchannena antaraghare nisīditabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca kāyaṁ vivaritvā antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1703Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, vāsūpagatassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1704Catutthasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
