1.1.10 Macchariyasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Dhanavagga
Macchariyasutta
10.
53“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, saṁyojanāni. Katamāni satta? Anunayasaṁyojanaṁ, paṭighasaṁyojanaṁ, diṭṭhisaṁyojanaṁ, vicikicchāsaṁyojanaṁ, mānasaṁyojanaṁ, issāsaṁyojanaṁ, macchariyasaṁyojanaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta saṁyojanānī”ti.
54Dasamaṁ.
55Dhanavaggo paṭhamo.
56Tassuddānaṁ
57
Dve piyāni balaṁ dhanaṁ,
Saṁkhittañceva vitthataṁ;
Uggaṁ saṁyojanañceva,
Pahānaṁ macchariyena cāti.
