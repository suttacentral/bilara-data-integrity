1.4.10 Dutiyabrahmacariyasutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Dutiyabrahmacariyasutta
40.
141Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Brahmacariyañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmacariyatthañca. Taṁ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṁ— sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyattho? Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyattho”ti.
142Dasamaṁ.
143Paṭipattivaggo catuttho.
144Tassuddānaṁ
145
Paṭipatti paṭipanno ca,
viraddhañca pāraṅgamā;
Sāmaññena ca dve vuttā,
brahmaññā apare duve;
Brahmacariyena dve vuttā,
vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
