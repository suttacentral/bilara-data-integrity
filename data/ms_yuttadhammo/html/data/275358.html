Peyyāla
Kodhapeyyāla
190.
285“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato idhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sugatiṁ saggaṁ lokaṁ upapajjati. Katamehi dvīhi? Akkodhena ca anupanāhena ca… amakkhena ca apaḷāsena ca… anissāya ca amacchariyena ca… amāyāya ca asāṭheyyena ca… hiriyā ca ottappena ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato idhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sugatiṁ saggaṁ lokaṁ upapajjati”.
286Kodhapeyyālaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
