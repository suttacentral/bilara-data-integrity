2.1.5 Daṭṭhabbasutta
Vedanāsaṁyutta
Sagāthāvagga
Daṭṭhabbasutta
253.
1114“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā. Sukhā, bhikkhave, vedanā dukkhato daṭṭhabbā, dukkhā vedanā sallato daṭṭhabbā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā aniccato daṭṭhabbā. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno sukhā vedanā dukkhato diṭṭhā hoti, dukkhā vedanā sallato diṭṭhā hoti, adukkhamasukhā vedanā aniccato diṭṭhā hoti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘bhikkhu sammaddaso acchecchi taṇhaṁ, vivattayi saṁyojanaṁ, sammā mānābhisamayā antamakāsi dukkhassā’ti.
1115
Yo sukhaṁ dukkhato adda,
dukkhamaddakkhi sallato;
Adukkhamasukhaṁ santaṁ,
addakkhi naṁ aniccato.
1116
Sa ve sammaddaso bhikkhu,
parijānāti vedanā;
So vedanā pariññāya,
diṭṭhe dhamme anāsavo;
Kāyassa bhedā dhammaṭṭho,
saṅkhyaṁ nopeti vedagū”ti.
1117Pañcamaṁ.
