4.2.10 Āsavakkhayasutta
Indriyasaṁyutta
Mudutaravagga
Āsavakkhayasutta
490.
1056“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṁ…pe…  paññindriyaṁ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ indriyānaṁ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā bhikkhu āsavānaṁ khayā anāsavaṁ cetovimuttiṁ paññāvimuttiṁ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṁ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharatī”ti.
1057Dasamaṁ.
1058Mudutaravaggo dutiyo.
1059Tassuddānaṁ
1060
Paṭilābho tayo saṁkhittā,
vitthārā apare tayo;
Paṭipanno ca sampanno,
dasamaṁ āsavakkhayanti.
