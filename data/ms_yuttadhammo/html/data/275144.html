16.3.14
Ekadhammapāḷi
Tatiyavagga
321.
410“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, appamattakampi muttaṁ duggandhaṁ hoti…  appamattakopi kheḷo duggandho hoti…  appamattakopi pubbo duggandho hoti…  appamattakampi lohitaṁ duggandhaṁ hoti; evamevaṁ kho ahaṁ, bhikkhave, appamattakampi bhavaṁ na vaṇṇemi, antamaso accharāsaṅghātamattampi”.
411Vaggo tatiyo.
