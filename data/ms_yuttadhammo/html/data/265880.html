351.
740Hetu nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo hetussa nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
741Hetu nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo hetussa nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
742Hetu nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo hetussa nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati…  nava…pe… .
743Nahetu nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo nahetussa nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
