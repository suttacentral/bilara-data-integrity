2.3.2.10.11 Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
1211.
1907Katame dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
1212.
1908Katame dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Cittañca, sabbañca rūpaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
