9.10 Niyassakammakathā
Campeyyakkhandhaka
Niyassakammakathā
412.
1846Idha pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhu bālo hoti abyatto āpattibahulo anapadāno, gihisaṁsaṭṭho viharati ananulomikehi gihisaṁsaggehi. Tatra ce bhikkhūnaṁ evaṁ hoti—  ‘ayaṁ kho, āvuso, bhikkhu bālo abyatto āpattibahulo anapadāno, gihisaṁsaṭṭho viharati ananulomikehi gihisaṁsaggehi. Handassa mayaṁ niyassakammaṁ karomā’ti. Te tassa niyassakammaṁ karonti—  adhammena vaggā. So tamhā āvāsā aññaṁ āvāsaṁ gacchati. Tatthapi bhikkhūnaṁ evaṁ hoti—  ‘ayaṁ kho, āvuso, bhikkhu saṁghena niyassakammakato adhammena vaggehi. Handassa mayaṁ niyassakammaṁ karomā’ti. Te tassa niyassakammaṁ karonti—  adhammena samaggā…pe…  dhammena vaggā…  dhammapatirūpakena vaggā…  dhammapatirūpakena samaggā…pe… .
1847Yathā heṭṭhā, tathā cakkaṁ kātabbaṁ.
