6.1.6 Asanisutta
Lābhasakkārasaṁyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Asanisutta
162.
1033Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe… adhigamāya. Kaṁ, bhikkhave, asanivicakkaṁ āgacchatu, sekhaṁ appattamānasaṁ lābhasakkārasiloko anupāpuṇātu.
1034Asanivicakkanti kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasilokassetaṁ adhivacanaṁ. Evaṁ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe… evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1035Chaṭṭhaṁ.
