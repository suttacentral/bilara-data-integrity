3.5.3 Visamasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Maṅgalavagga
Visamasutta
149.
1069“Tīhi, bhikkhave…pe…  visamena kāyakammena, visamena vacīkammena, visamena manokammena—  imehi kho…pe…  evaṁ niraye.
1070Tīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi…pe…  samena kāyakammena, samena vacīkammena, samena manokammena—  imehi kho…pe…  evaṁ sagge”ti.
1071Tatiyaṁ.
