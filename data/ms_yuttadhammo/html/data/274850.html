7.3.4 Suddhikasutta
Iddhipādasaṁyutta
Ayoguḷavagga
Suddhikasutta
836.
1481“Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chandasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṁ iddhipādaṁ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi…pe…  cittasamādhi…  vīmaṁsāsamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṁ iddhipādaṁ bhāveti—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā”ti.
1482Catutthaṁ.
