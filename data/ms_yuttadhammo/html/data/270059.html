16.2.9 Navakaniddesa
Ñāṇavibhaṅga
Niddesa
Navakaniddesa
808.
2553Tattha katamā navasu anupubbavihārasamāpattīsu paññā? Paṭhamajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, dutiyajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, tatiyajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, catutthajjhānasamāpattiyā paññā, ākāsānañcāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, viññāṇañcāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, ākiñcaññāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, nevasaññānāsaññāyatanasamāpattiyā paññā, saññāvedayitanirodhasamāpattiyā vuṭṭhitassa paccavekkhaṇāñāṇaṁ— imā navasu anupubbavihārasamāpattīsu paññā. Evaṁ navavidhena ñāṇavatthu.
2554Navakaṁ.
