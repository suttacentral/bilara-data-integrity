11.5.6 Suddhakasutta
Sotāpattisaṁyutta
Sagāthakapuññābhisandavagga
Suddhakasutta
1042.
2074“Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo.
2075Katamehi catūhi? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako buddhe aveccappasādena samannāgato hoti— itipi so bhagavā…pe… satthā devamanussānaṁ buddho bhagavāti. Dhamme…pe… saṁghe…pe… ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi…pe… samādhisaṁvattanikehi. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo”ti.
2076Chaṭṭhaṁ.
