30.
1509Kusalaṁ kilesavippayuttaṁ saṁkilesikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kusalo kilesavippayutto saṁkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Kusalaṁ kilesavippayuttaṁ saṁkilesikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato kilesavippayutto saṁkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Kusalaṁ kilesavippayuttaṁ saṁkilesikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kusalo kilesavippayutto saṁkilesiko ca abyākato kilesavippayutto saṁkilesiko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
1510Abyākataṁ kilesavippayuttaṁ saṁkilesikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato kilesavippayutto saṁkilesiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
