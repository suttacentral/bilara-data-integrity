1627Noganthaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nogantho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— noganthe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (Yāva asaññasattā.) (1)
1628Ganthañca noganthañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nogantho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— ganthe ca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
1629Naadhipatipaccayā… nava, naanantarapaccayā… tīṇi, nasamanantarapaccayā… tīṇi, naaññamaññapaccayā… tīṇi, naupanissayapaccayā… tīṇi.
