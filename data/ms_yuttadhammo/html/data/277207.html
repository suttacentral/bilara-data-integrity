1.3.3.5 Soṇasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Gahapativagga
Soṇasutta
128.
739Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Atha kho soṇo gahapatiputto yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ nisīdi. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho soṇo gahapatiputto bhagavantaṁ etadavoca— “ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo yena midhekacce sattā diṭṭheva dhamme no parinibbāyanti? Ko pana, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo yena midhekacce sattā diṭṭheva dhamme parinibbāyantī”ti? (Yathā purimasuttantaṁ, evaṁ vitthāretabbaṁ.) Ayaṁ kho, soṇa, hetu, ayaṁ paccayo yena midhekacce sattā diṭṭheva dhamme parinibbāyantīti.
740Pañcamaṁ.
