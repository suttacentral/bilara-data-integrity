312Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
313Ahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  ahetukā indriyā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṁ…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  cakkhundriyaṁ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṁ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. Rūpajīvitindriyaṁ—  kaṭattārūpānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
314Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
315Ahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo—  ahetukāni jhānaṅgāni sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṁ…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… . (1)
316Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa maggapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
