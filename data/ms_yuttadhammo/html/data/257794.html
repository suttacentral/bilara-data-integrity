335Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ narūpāvacaraṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nanarūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
