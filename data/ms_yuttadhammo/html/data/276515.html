1.5.8.6 Amūlakasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Sahadhammikavagga
Amūlakasikkhāpada
459.
1367Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhikkhuṁ amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁsenti.
1368Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe… te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū bhikkhuṁ amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁsessantī”ti…pe… “saccaṁ kira tumhe, bhikkhave, bhikkhuṁ amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁsethā”ti? “Saccaṁ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe… kathañhi nāma tumhe, moghapurisā, bhikkhuṁ amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁsessatha. Netaṁ, moghapurisā, appasannānaṁ vā pasādāya…pe… evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṁ sikkhāpadaṁ uddiseyyātha—
460.
1369“Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuṁ amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁseyya, pācittiyan”ti. (76:125)
461.
1370Yo panāti yo yādiso…pe… bhikkhūti…pe… ayaṁ imasmiṁ atthe adhippeto bhikkhūti.
1371Bhikkhunti aññaṁ bhikkhuṁ.
1372Amūlakaṁ nāma adiṭṭhaṁ assutaṁ aparisaṅkitaṁ.
1373Saṁghādisesenāti terasannaṁ aññatarena.
1374Anuddhaṁseyyāti codeti vā codāpeti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa.
462.
1375Upasampanne upasampannasaññī amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Upasampanne vematiko amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁseti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Upasampanne anupasampannasaññī amūlakena saṁghādisesena anuddhaṁseti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
1376Ācāravipattiyā vā diṭṭhivipattiyā vā anuddhaṁseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampannaṁ anuddhaṁseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne upasampannasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne vematiko, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Anupasampanne anupasampannasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
463.
1377Anāpatti— tathāsaññī codeti, vā codāpeti vā, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1378Amūlakasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.
