Nahetuduka
75.
1863Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe pañca, adhipatiyā nava, anantare aṭṭha, samanantare aṭṭha, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe tīṇi, nissaye terasa, upanissaye aṭṭha, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, āsevane dve, kamme aṭṭha, vipāke āhāre indriye jhāne magge satta, sampayutte tīṇi, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā terasa, natthiyā aṭṭha, vigate aṭṭha, avigate terasa. (Evaṁ gaṇetabbaṁ.)
1864Paccanīyānulomaṁ.
