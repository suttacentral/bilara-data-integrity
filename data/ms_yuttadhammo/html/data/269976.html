Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga

Aññamaññacatukka
246.
982Avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayāpi avijjā; saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṁ, viññāṇapaccayāpi saṅkhāro; viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṁ, nāmapaccayāpi viññāṇaṁ; nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṁ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayāpi nāmaṁ; chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayāpi chaṭṭhāyatanaṁ; phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayāpi phasso; vedanāpaccayā taṇhā, taṇhāpaccayāpi vedanā; taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ, upādānapaccayāpi taṇhā; upādānapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti. (1:13)
