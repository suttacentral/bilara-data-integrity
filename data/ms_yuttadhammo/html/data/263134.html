77.
2101Paritto dhammo parittassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā parittā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ parittānaṁ khandhānaṁ…pe…  anulomaṁ gotrabhussa, anulomaṁ vodānassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2102Paritto dhammo mahaggatassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  paṭhamassa jhānassa parikammaṁ tasseva āsevanapaccayena paccayo…pe…  nevasaññānāsaññāyatanassa parikammaṁ tasseva āsevanapaccayena paccayo. Dibbassa cakkhussa parikammaṁ…pe…  anāgataṁsañāṇassa parikammaṁ anāgataṁsañāṇassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (2)
2103Paritto dhammo appamāṇassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṁ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (3)
2104Mahaggato dhammo mahaggatassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā mahaggatā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ mahaggatānaṁ khandhānaṁ…pe…  āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
