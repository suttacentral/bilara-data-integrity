“Devo ca vassati, devo ca gaḷagaḷāyati,
Ekako cāhaṁ bherave bile viharāmi;
Tassa mayhaṁ ekakassa bherave bile viharato,
Natthi bhayaṁ vā chambhitattaṁ vā lomahaṁso vā.
