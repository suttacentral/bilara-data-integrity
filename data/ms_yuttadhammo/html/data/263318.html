43.
2841Ajjhatto dhammo ajjhattassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  ajjhattā āhārā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṁ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  ajjhatto kabaḷīkāro āhāro ajjhattassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2842Ajjhatto dhammo bahiddhā dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  ajjhatto kabaḷīkāro āhāro bahiddhā kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
2843Bahiddhā dhammo bahiddhā dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo (pavattipaṭisandhi) bahiddhā kabaḷīkāro āhāro bahiddhā kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2844Bahiddhā dhammo ajjhattassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  bahiddhā kabaḷīkāro āhāro ajjhattassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
