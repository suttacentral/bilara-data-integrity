2.3.2.10.1 Sārammaṇaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Sārammaṇaduka
1191.
1887Katame dhammā sārammaṇā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā sārammaṇā.
1192.
1888Katame dhammā anārammaṇā? Sabbañca rūpaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā anārammaṇā.
