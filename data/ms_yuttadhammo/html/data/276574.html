1.7.5.3 Sakabaḷasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Kabaḷavagga
Sakabaḷasikkhāpada
619.
1899Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sakabaḷena mukhena byāharanti…pe… .
1900“Na sakabaḷena mukhena byāharissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (43:188)
1901Na sakabaḷena mukhena byāharitabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca sakabaḷena mukhena byāharati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1902Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1903Tatiyasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
