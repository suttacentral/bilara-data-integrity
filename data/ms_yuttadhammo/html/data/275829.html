1.3.9 Caṅkamasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Pañcaṅgikavagga
Caṅkamasutta
29.
123“Pañcime, bhikkhave, caṅkame ānisaṁsā. Katame pañca? Addhānakkhamo hoti, padhānakkhamo hoti, appābādho hoti, asitaṁ pītaṁ khāyitaṁ sāyitaṁ sammā pariṇāmaṁ gacchati, caṅkamādhigato samādhi ciraṭṭhitiko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca caṅkame ānisaṁsā”ti.
124Navamaṁ.
