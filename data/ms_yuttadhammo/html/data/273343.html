1.5.1 Adhammakammadvādasaka
Kammakkhandhaka
Āpattiyāadassaneukkhepanīyakamma
Adhammakammadvādasaka
48.
220Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā adassane, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Asammukhā kataṁ hoti, appaṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā adassane, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
221Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā adassane, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Anāpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, desitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti…pe…  acodetvā kataṁ hoti, asāretvā kataṁ hoti, āpattiṁ anāropetvā kataṁ hoti…pe…  asammukhā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  appaṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  anāpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  desitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  acodetvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  asāretvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  āpattiṁ anāropetvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā adassane, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
222Āpattiyā adassane ukkhepanīyakamme
adhammakammadvādasakaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
