Hetusahetukadukādi, Kusalattika
36.
104Nahetuñceva naahetukañca nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu ceva naahetuko ca nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuñceva naahetukañca nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nahetuñceva naahetukañca nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu ceva naahetuko ca nakusalo ca naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu nakusalo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
105Naahetukañceva nana ca hetuṁ nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naahetuko ceva nana ca hetu nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi.
106Nahetuñceva naahetukañca nakusalañca naahetukañceva nana ca hetuṁ nakusalañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu ceva naahetuko ca nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
107Hetuyā nava…pe… avigate nava.
