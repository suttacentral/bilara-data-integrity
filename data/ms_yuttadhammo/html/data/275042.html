12.7.2 Paccantasutta
Saccasaṁyutta
Paṭhamaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga
Paccantasutta
1132.
2437Atha kho bhagavā parittaṁ nakhasikhāyaṁ paṁsuṁ āropetvā bhikkhū āmantesi— “taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, katamaṁ nu kho bahutaraṁ— yo vāyaṁ mayā paritto nakhasikhāyaṁ paṁsu āropito, ayaṁ vā mahāpathavī”ti? “Etadeva, bhante, bahutaraṁ, yadidaṁ— mahāpathavī; appamattakāyaṁ bhagavatā paritto nakhasikhāyaṁ paṁsu āropito. Saṅkhampi na upeti, upanidhampi na upeti, kalabhāgampi na upeti mahāpathaviṁ upanidhāya bhagavatā paritto nakhasikhāyaṁ paṁsu āropito”ti.
