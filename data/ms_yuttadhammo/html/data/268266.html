3.4.5 Dhammasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Dhammasutta
138.
1114“Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo”ti.
1115Pañcamaṁ.
