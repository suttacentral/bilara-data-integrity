1.3.5.3 Dukkhanibbānasappāyasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Navapurāṇavagga
Dukkhanibbānasappāyasutta
148.
814“Nibbānasappāyaṁ vo, bhikkhave, paṭipadaṁ desessāmi. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamā ca sā, bhikkhave, nibbānasappāyā paṭipadā? Idha, bhikkhave, cakkhuṁ dukkhanti passati, rūpā dukkhāti passati, cakkhuviññāṇaṁ dukkhanti passati, cakkhusamphasso dukkhoti passati, yampidaṁ cakkhusamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi dukkhanti passati…pe… jivhā dukkhāti passati…pe… mano dukkhoti passati, dhammā dukkhāti passati, manoviññāṇaṁ dukkhanti passati, manosamphasso dukkhoti passati, yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi dukkhanti passati. Ayaṁ kho sā, bhikkhave, nibbānasappāyā paṭipadā”ti.
815Tatiyaṁ.
