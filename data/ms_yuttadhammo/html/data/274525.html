1.4.9 Paṭhamabrahmacariyasutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Paṭhamabrahmacariyasutta
39.
139Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Brahmacariyañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmacariyaphalāni ca. Taṁ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṁ— sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ. Katamāni ca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaphalāni? Sotāpattiphalaṁ, sakadāgāmiphalaṁ, anāgāmiphalaṁ, arahattaphalaṁ— imāni vuccanti, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaphalānī”ti.
140Navamaṁ.
