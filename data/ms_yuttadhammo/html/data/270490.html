40.1.8.37 Phalapīṭhānisaṁsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṁsakathā
Phalapīṭhānisaṁsa
187.
4484
Phalapīṭhe jine datvā,
saṁghe gaṇavaruttame;
Dvānisaṁse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
188.
4485
Soṇṇamaye maṇimaye,
dantasāramaye bahū;
Pallaṅkaseṭṭhe vindāmi,
phalapīṭhassidaṁ phalaṁ.
