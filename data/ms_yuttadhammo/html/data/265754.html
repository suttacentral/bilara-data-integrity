354.
4818Saraṇo dhammo saraṇassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā saraṇā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ saraṇānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. Saraṇo dhammo araṇassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  saraṇā khandhā vuṭṭhānassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)
