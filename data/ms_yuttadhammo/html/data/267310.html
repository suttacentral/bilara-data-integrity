18.6 Maggadattikattheraapadāna
Kumudavagga
Maggadattikattheraapadāna
26.
2545
“Anomadassī bhagavā,
dvipadindo narāsabho;
Diṭṭhadhammasukhatthāya,
abbhokāsamhi caṅkami.
27.
2546
Uddhate pāde pupphāni,
sobhaṁ muddhani tiṭṭhare;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvā pupphamokiriṁ.
28.
2547
Vīsakappasahassamhi,
ito pañca janā ahuṁ;
Pupphacchadaniyā nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.
29.
2548
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
2549Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā maggadattiko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2550Maggadattikattherassāpadānaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.
