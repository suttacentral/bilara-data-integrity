36.
95Hetu dhammo hetussa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…pe… . (Hetumūlake tīṇi.)
96Nahetu dhammo nahetussa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  nahetū indriyā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  cakkhundriyaṁ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṁ kāyaviññāṇassa…pe… . Rūpajīvitindriyaṁ—  kaṭattārūpānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Evaṁ indriyapaccayā vitthāretabbā. Nava.)
