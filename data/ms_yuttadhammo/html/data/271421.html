2.3.2.12.6 Kilesasaṁkiliṭṭhaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesasaṁkiliṭṭhaduka
1255.
1954Katame dhammā kilesā ceva saṁkiliṭṭhā ca? Teva kilesā kilesā ceva saṁkiliṭṭhā ca.
1256.
1955Katame dhammā saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā? Tehi dhammehi ye dhammā saṁkiliṭṭhā, te dhamme ṭhapetvā vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā.
