6.1.7 Diddhasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṁyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Diddhasutta
163.
1036Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe… adhigamāya. Kaṁ, bhikkhave, diddhagatena visallena sallena vijjhatu, sekhaṁ appattamānasaṁ lābhasakkārasiloko anupāpuṇātu.
1037Sallanti kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasilokassetaṁ adhivacanaṁ. Evaṁ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe… evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1038Sattamaṁ.
