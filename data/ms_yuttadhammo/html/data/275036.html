12.6.6 Dutiyamahāpathavīsutta
Saccasaṁyutta
Abhisamayavagga
Dutiyamahāpathavīsutta
1126.
2419“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, mahāpathavī parikkhayaṁ pariyādānaṁ gaccheyya ṭhapetvā satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā. Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, katamaṁ nu kho bahutaraṁ—  yaṁ vā mahāpathaviyā parikkhīṇaṁ pariyādinnaṁ, yā vā satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā avasiṭṭhā”ti? “Etadeva, bhante, bahutaraṁ mahāpathaviyā yadidaṁ parikkhīṇaṁ pariyādinnaṁ; appamattikā satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā avasiṭṭhā. Saṅkhampi na upenti, upanidhampi na upenti, kalabhāgampi na upenti mahāpathaviyā parikkhīṇaṁ pariyādinnaṁ upanidhāya satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā avasiṭṭhā”ti. “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa…pe…  yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2420Chaṭṭhaṁ.
