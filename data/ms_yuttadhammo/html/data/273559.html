9.4.5 Dhammānupassanāsaccapabba
Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta
Dhammānupassanā
Dhammānupassanāsaccapabba
386.
1034Puna caparaṁ, bhikkhave, bhikkhu dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati catūsu ariyasaccesu. Kathañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhu dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati catūsu ariyasaccesu? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ‘idaṁ dukkhan’ti yathābhūtaṁ pajānāti, ‘ayaṁ dukkhasamudayo’ti yathābhūtaṁ pajānāti, ‘ayaṁ dukkhanirodho’ti yathābhūtaṁ pajānāti, ‘ayaṁ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yathābhūtaṁ pajānāti.
Mahāsatipaṭṭhānasutta
Dhammānupassanā
Dhammānupassanāsaccapabba
1035Paṭhamabhāṇavāro niṭṭhito.
