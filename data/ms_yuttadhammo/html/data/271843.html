1.32 Lakkhaṇāhatavatthu
Mahākhandhaka
Lakkhaṇāhatavatthu
95.
433Tena kho pana samayena aññataro puriso lakkhaṇāhato katadaṇḍakammo bhikkhūsu pabbajito hoti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma samaṇā sakyaputtiyā lakkhaṇāhataṁ katadaṇḍakammaṁ pabbājessantī”ti. Bhagavato etamatthaṁ ārocesuṁ. “Na, bhikkhave, lakkhaṇāhato katadaṇḍakammo pabbājetabbo. Yo pabbājeyya, āpatti dukkaṭassā”ti.
