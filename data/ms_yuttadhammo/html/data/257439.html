28 Ganthasampayuttaduka
Ganthasampayuttaduka
42.
133Naganthasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthasampayutto ca naganthavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
134Naganthavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthasampayutto ca naganthavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
135Naganthasampayuttañca naganthavippayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttañca naganthavippayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthavippayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naganthasampayuttañca naganthavippayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naganthasampayutto ca naganthavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
136Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  vipāke ekaṁ…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
