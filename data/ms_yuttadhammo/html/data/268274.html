3.5.2 Kaṇhamaggasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyavagga
Kaṇhamaggasutta
146.
1131“Kaṇhamaggañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi sukkamaggañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, kaṇhamaggo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, kaṇhamaggo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukkamaggo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukkamaggo”ti.
1132Dutiyaṁ.
