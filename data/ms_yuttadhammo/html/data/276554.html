1.7.3.3 Oguṇṭhitasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Oguṇṭhitasikkhāpada
598.
1795Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sasīsaṁ pārupitvā antaraghare gacchanti…pe….
1796“Na oguṇṭhito antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (23:168)
1797Na oguṇṭhitena antaraghare gantabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca sasīsaṁ pārupitvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1798Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1799Tatiyasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
