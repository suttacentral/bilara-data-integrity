2.4.7 Abhabbasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sītivagga
Abhabbasutta
91.
701“Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo uppādetuṁ. Katame cha? Sakkāyadiṭṭhiṁ, vicikicchaṁ, sīlabbataparāmāsaṁ, apāyagamanīyaṁ rāgaṁ, apāyagamanīyaṁ dosaṁ, apāyagamanīyaṁ mohaṁ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme abhabbo diṭṭhisampanno puggalo uppādetun”ti.
702Sattamaṁ.
