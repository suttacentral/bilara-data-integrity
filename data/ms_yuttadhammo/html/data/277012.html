1.2.3.3 Dutiyasamudayasutta
Khandhasaṁyutta
Majjhimapaṇṇāsaka
Khajjanīyavagga
Dutiyasamudayasutta
75.
383Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Sutavā, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako rūpassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṁ pajānāti. Vedanāya…  saññāya…  saṅkhārānaṁ…  viññāṇassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṁ pajānātī”ti.
384Tatiyaṁ.
