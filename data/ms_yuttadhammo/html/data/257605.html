Bhāvanāyapahātabbaduka
36.
117Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca nanabhāvanāya pahātabbo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
118Nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
119Bhāvanāya pahātabbañca nabhāvanāya pahātabbañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)
