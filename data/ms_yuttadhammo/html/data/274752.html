4.1.9 Paṭhamavibhaṅgasutta
Indriyasaṁyutta
Suddhikavagga
Paṭhamavibhaṅgasutta
479.
1018“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṁ…pe… paññindriyaṁ. Katamañca, bhikkhave, saddhindriyaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako saddho hoti, saddahati tathāgatassa bodhiṁ— ‘itipi so bhagavā arahaṁ sammāsambuddho vijjācaraṇasampanno sugato lokavidū anuttaro purisadammasārathi satthā devamanussānaṁ buddho bhagavā’ti— idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, saddhindriyaṁ.
1019Katamañca, bhikkhave, vīriyindriyaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako āraddhavīriyo viharati akusalānaṁ dhammānaṁ pahānāya, kusalānaṁ dhammānaṁ upasampadāya, thāmavā daḷhaparakkamo anikkhittadhuro kusalesu dhammesu— idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, vīriyindriyaṁ.
1020Katamañca, bhikkhave, satindriyaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako satimā hoti paramena satinepakkena samannāgato cirakatampi cirabhāsitampi saritā anussaritā— idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, satindriyaṁ.
1021Katamañca, bhikkhave, samādhindriyaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako vossaggārammaṇaṁ karitvā labhati samādhiṁ, labhati cittassa ekaggataṁ— idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, samādhindriyaṁ.
1022Katamañca, bhikkhave, paññindriyaṁ? Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako paññavā hoti udayatthagāminiyā paññāya samannāgato ariyāya nibbedhikāya, sammā dukkhakkhayagāminiyā— idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, paññindriyaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.
1023Navamaṁ.
