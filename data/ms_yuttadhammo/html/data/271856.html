1.45 Apalāḷanavatthu
Mahākhandhaka
Apalāḷanavatthu
480Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū therānaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ sāmaṇere apalāḷenti. Therā sāmaṁ dantakaṭṭhampi mukhodakampi gaṇhantā kilamanti. Bhagavato etamatthaṁ ārocesuṁ. “Na, bhikkhave, aññassa parisā apalāḷetabbā. Yo apalāḷeyya, āpatti dukkaṭassā”ti.
