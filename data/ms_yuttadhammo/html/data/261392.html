68.
3989Saṁkiliṭṭho dhammo saṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3990Saṁkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3991Saṁkiliṭṭho dhammo saṁkiliṭṭhassa ca asaṁkiliṭṭhassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
69.
3992Asaṁkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3993Asaṁkiliṭṭho dhammo saṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3994Saṁkiliṭṭho ca asaṁkiliṭṭho ca dhammā saṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa sahajātaṁ…  purejātaṁ. (1)
3995Saṁkiliṭṭho ca asaṁkiliṭṭho ca dhammā asaṁkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa sahajātaṁ…  pacchājātaṁ…  āhāraṁ…  indriyaṁ. (2)
