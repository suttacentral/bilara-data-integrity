3.4.6 Sāsavasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Sāsavasutta
139.
1116“Sāsavañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi anāsavañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo”ti.
1117Chaṭṭhaṁ.
