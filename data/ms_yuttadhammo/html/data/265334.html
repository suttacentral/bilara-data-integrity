3062Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo (navapi pañhā cittasahabhūdukasadisā)…  samanantarapaccayena paccayo…  nava…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  nava (paṭiccasadisā)…  aññamaññapaccayena paccayo…  nava (paṭiccasadisā)…  nissayapaccayena paccayo…  nava (paccayasadisā)…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo.
