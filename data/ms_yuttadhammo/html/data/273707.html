2.2.2 Veṇḍusutta
Devaputtasaṁyutta
Anāthapiṇḍikavagga
Veṇḍusutta
93.
433Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho veṇḍu devaputto bhagavato santike imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi—
434
“Sukhitāva te manujā,
sugataṁ payirupāsiya;
Yuñjaṁ gotamasāsane,
appamattā nu sikkhare”ti.
435
“Ye me pavutte siṭṭhipade, (veṇḍūti bhagavā)
Anusikkhanti jhāyino;
Kāle te appamajjantā,
Na maccuvasagā siyun”ti.
