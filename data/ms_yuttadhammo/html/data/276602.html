1.7.7.11 Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Pacchatogamanasikkhāpada
649.
2062Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū pacchato gacchantā purato gacchantassa dhammaṁ desenti…pe… .
2063“Na pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṁ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (71:216)
2064Na pacchato gacchantena purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṁ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2065Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2066Ekādasamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
