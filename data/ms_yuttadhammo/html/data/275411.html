1.5.8 Asaṅkhatalakkhaṇasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Cūḷavagga
Asaṅkhatalakkhaṇasutta
48.
290“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatassa asaṅkhatalakkhaṇāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Na uppādo paññāyati, na vayo paññāyati, na ṭhitassa aññathattaṁ paññāyati. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi asaṅkhatassa asaṅkhatalakkhaṇānī”ti.
291Aṭṭhamaṁ.
