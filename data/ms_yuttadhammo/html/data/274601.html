1.14.10 Uddhambhāgiyasutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Oghavagga
Uddhambhāgiyasutta
181.
353“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṁ, avijjā—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ uddhambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṁ bhāveti vivekanissitaṁ…pe…  sammāsamādhiṁ bhāveti vivekanissitaṁ virāganissitaṁ nirodhanissitaṁ vossaggapariṇāmiṁ. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ uddhambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabboti.
354Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṁ, avijjā—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ uddhambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṁ bhāveti…pe…  sammāsamādhiṁ bhāveti rāgavinayapariyosānaṁ dosavinayapariyosānaṁ mohavinayapariyosānaṁ…  amatogadhaṁ amataparāyanaṁ amatapariyosānaṁ…  nibbānaninnaṁ nibbānapoṇaṁ nibbānapabbhāraṁ. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ uddhambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
355Dasamaṁ.
356Oghavaggo aṭṭhamo.
357Tassuddānaṁ
358
Ogho yogo upādānaṁ,
ganthaṁ anusayena ca;
Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇaṁ,
khandhā oruddhambhāgiyāti.
359Vagguddānaṁ
360
Avijjāvaggo paṭhamo,
dutiyaṁ vihāraṁ vuccati;
Micchattaṁ tatiyo vaggo,
catutthaṁ paṭipanneneva.
361
Titthiyaṁ pañcamo vaggo,
chaṭṭho sūriyena ca;
Bahukate sattamo vaggo,
uppādo aṭṭhamena ca.
362
Divasavaggo navamo,
Dasamo appamādena ca;
Ekādasabalavaggo,
Dvādasa esanā pāḷiyaṁ;
Oghavaggo bhavati terasāti.
363Maggasaṁyuttaṁ paṭhamaṁ.
