1.4.1.2 Bāhiranandikkhayasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Nandikkhayavagga
Bāhiranandikkhayasutta
157.
863“Anicceyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu rūpe aniccāti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṁ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandirāgakkhayā cittaṁ suvimuttanti vuccati. Anicceyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sadde…  gandhe…  rase…  phoṭṭhabbe…  dhamme aniccāti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṁ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandirāgakkhayā cittaṁ suvimuttanti vuccatī”ti.
864Dutiyaṁ.
