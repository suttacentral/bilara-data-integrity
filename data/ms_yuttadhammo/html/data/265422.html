436.
3422Upādinnaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  vatthuṁ paccayā anupādinnā khandhā. (1)
3423Anupādinnaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  anupādinnaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paccayā tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ…pe…  mahābhūte paccayā cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ upādārūpaṁ. (1)
3424Upādinnañca anupādinnañca dhammaṁ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  anupādinnaṁ ekaṁ khandhañca vatthuñca paccayā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe ca…pe… . (1) (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
