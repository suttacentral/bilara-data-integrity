2.1.9 Paṭhamapuggalasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Gotamīvagga
Paṭhamapuggalasutta
59.
610“Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassa? Katame aṭṭha? Sotāpanno, sotāpattiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno, arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha puggalā āhuneyyā…pe… anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassāti.
611
Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṁgho ujubhūto,
paññāsīlasamāhito.
612
Yajamānānaṁ manussānaṁ,
Puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṁ;
Karotaṁ opadhikaṁ puññaṁ,
Saṁghe dinnaṁ mahapphalan”ti.
613Navamaṁ.
