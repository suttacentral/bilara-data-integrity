1.4.1 Anupubbavihārasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Mahāvagga
Anupubbavihārasutta
32.
246“Navayime, bhikkhave, anupubbavihārā. Katame nava? Paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ, dutiyaṁ jhānaṁ, tatiyaṁ jhānaṁ, catutthaṁ jhānaṁ, ākāsānañcāyatanaṁ, viññāṇañcāyatanaṁ, ākiñcaññāyatanaṁ, nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṁ, saññāvedayitanirodho— ime kho, bhikkhave, nava anupubbavihārā”ti.
247Paṭhamaṁ.
