395.
851Hetu mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo hetussa mahaggatārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… sahajātapaccayena paccayo… upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
396.
852Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
853Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
854Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
855(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṁ, evaṁ gaṇetabbaṁ.)
