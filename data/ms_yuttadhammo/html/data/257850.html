17 Āsavasampayuttaduka, Hetuduka
Āsavasampayuttaduka, Hetuduka
55.
130Āsavasampayuttaṁ hetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
131Āsavavippayuttaṁ hetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavavippayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
132Āsavasampayuttaṁ hetuñca āsavavippayuttaṁ hetuñca dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke ekaṁ…pe…  avigate nava.
133Āsavasampayuttaṁ nahetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
134Āsavavippayuttaṁ nahetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavasampayutto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cattāri…pe…  vipāke ekaṁ…pe…  avigate cattāri.
