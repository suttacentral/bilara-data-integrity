50 Sanidassanattika, Hetuhetusampayuttaduka
Sanidassanattika, Hetuhetusampayuttaduka
93.
231Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetuñceva hetusampayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetu ceva nahetuvippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
232Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ hetusampayuttañceva na ca hetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nahetuvippayutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
