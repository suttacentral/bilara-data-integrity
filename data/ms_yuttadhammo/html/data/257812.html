12 Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
24.
63Kusalaṁ parittārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo naparittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
64Kusalaṁ mahaggatārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo namahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  avigate nava.
65Kusalaṁ appamāṇārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo naappamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
