4.
2163Parittārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā— ahetukaṁ parittārammaṇaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhā. Ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe parittārammaṇaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhā, vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
