2.1.2.13 Niruddhanirujjhamānavāra
Niddesa
Suddhacittasāmañña
Dhammavāra
Niruddhanirujjhamānavāra
103.
490»  Niruddhaṁ nirujjhamānanti? No.
491«  Nirujjhamānaṁ niruddhanti? No.
492»  Na niruddhaṁ na nirujjhamānanti?
493Bhaṅgakkhaṇe na niruddhaṁ, no ca na nirujjhamānaṁ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṁ na ceva niruddhaṁ na ca nirujjhamānaṁ.
494«  Na nirujjhamānaṁ na niruddhanti?
495Atītaṁ cittaṁ na nirujjhamānaṁ, no ca na niruddhaṁ. Uppādakkhaṇe anāgatañca cittaṁ na ceva nirujjhamānaṁ na ca niruddhaṁ.
