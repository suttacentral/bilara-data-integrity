1.2.2 Kodhapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Kodhapariññāsutta
12.
61Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ— 
62“Kodhaṁ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṁ aparijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ avirājayaṁ appajahaṁ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Kodhañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṁ parijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ virājayaṁ pajahaṁ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati— 
63
“Yena kodhena kuddhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ;
Taṁ kodhaṁ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṁ lokaṁ kudācanan”ti.
64Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
65Dutiyaṁ.
