2.2.6 Gatisutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Satipaṭṭhānavagga
Gatisutta
68.
451“Pañcimā, bhikkhave, gatiyo. Katamā pañca? Nirayo, tiracchānayoni, pettivisayo, manussā, devā— imā kho, bhikkhave, pañca gatiyo.
452Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ gatīnaṁ pahānāya…pe… ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.
453Chaṭṭhaṁ.
