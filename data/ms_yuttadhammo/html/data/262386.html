Sampayutta
297.
978Kusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo— kusalo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
979Akusalo dhammo akusalassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo— akusalo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
980Abyākato dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo— vipākābyākato kiriyābyākato eko khandho tiṇṇannaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe vipākābyākato eko khandho tiṇṇannaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
