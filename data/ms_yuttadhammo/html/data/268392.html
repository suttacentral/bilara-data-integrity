1.2.7 Dutiyasekhasutta
Ekakanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Dutiyasekhasutta
17.
88Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ—
89“Sekhassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appattamānasassa anuttaraṁ yogakkhemaṁ patthayamānassa viharato bāhiraṁ aṅganti karitvā nāññaṁ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṁ evaṁ bahūpakāraṁ yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, kalyāṇamittatā. Kalyāṇamitto, bhikkhave, bhikkhu akusalaṁ pajahati, kusalaṁ bhāvetī”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati—
90
“Kalyāṇamitto yo bhikkhu,
sappatisso sagāravo;
Karaṁ mittānaṁ vacanaṁ,
sampajāno patissato;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṁyojanakkhayan”ti.
91Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
92Sattamaṁ.
