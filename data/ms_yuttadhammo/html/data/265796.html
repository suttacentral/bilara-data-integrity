Upanissayādi
102.
176Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo…  nava, āsevanapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
103.
177Nahetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo nahetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
104.
178Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa vipākapaccayena paccayo…  nava…pe…  avigatapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
105.
179Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, vipāke nava, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate nava. (Anulomaṁ.)
