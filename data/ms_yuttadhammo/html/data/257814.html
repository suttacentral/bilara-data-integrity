14 Kusalattika, Micchattaniyatattika
Kusalattika, Micchattaniyatattika
26.
69Akusalaṁ micchattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo namicchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
70Kusalaṁ sammattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo nasammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
71Abyākataṁ aniyataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā naabyākato naaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
