4.4.10 Sacchikātabbasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyamaggavagga
Sacchikātabbasutta
198.
1382“Sacchikātabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na sacchikātabbañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe… micchādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo.
1383Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe… sammādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo”ti.
1384Dasamaṁ.
1385Ariyamaggavaggo catuttho.
