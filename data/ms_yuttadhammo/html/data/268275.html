3.5.3 Saddhammasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyavagga
Saddhammasutta
147.
1133“Saddhammañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi asaddhammañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaddhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaddhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, saddhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, saddhammo”ti.
1134Tatiyaṁ.
