6.2.12.2 Rūpāvacarakusalacitta
Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Avijjāmūlakakusalaniddesa
Rūpāvacarakusalacitta
340.
1303Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṁ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṁ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe… paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṁ, tasmiṁ samaye avijjāpaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṁ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṁ, nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṁ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayā pasādo, pasādapaccayā adhimokkho, adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti. ( )
