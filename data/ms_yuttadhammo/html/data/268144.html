1.2.6 Āhuneyyasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Nāthavagga
Āhuneyyasutta
16.
90“Dasayime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassa. Katame dasa? Tathāgato arahaṁ sammāsambuddho, paccekabuddho, ubhatobhāgavimutto, paññāvimutto, kāyasakkhī, diṭṭhippatto, saddhāvimutto, saddhānusārī, dhammānusārī, gotrabhū—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa puggalā āhuneyyā…pe…  anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassā”ti.
91Chaṭṭhaṁ.
