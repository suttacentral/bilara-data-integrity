3.5.1 Ariyamaggasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyavagga
Ariyamaggasutta
145.
1129“Ariyamaggañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi anariyamaggañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, anariyo maggo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāvimutti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anariyo maggo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, ariyo maggo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāvimutti—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyo maggo”ti.
1130Paṭhamaṁ.
