4.7.3 Pariññāsutta
Indriyasaṁyutta
Bodhipakkhiyavagga
Pariññāsutta
533.
1233“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhānapariññāya saṁvattanti. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṁ…pe… paññindriyaṁ— imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhānapariññāya saṁvattantī”ti.
1234Tatiyaṁ.
