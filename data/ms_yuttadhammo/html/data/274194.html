3.2.18 Nevahotinanahotisutta
Diṭṭhisaṁyutta
Dutiyagamanavagga
Nevahotinanahotisutta
241.
986Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe… 
987“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti. “Vedanāya sati…  saññāya sati…  saṅkhāresu sati…  viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’ti.
988Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti? “Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…  vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “No hetaṁ, bhante”. “Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti. “Vedanā…  saññā…  saṅkhārā…  viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti? “Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…  vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “No hetaṁ, bhante”. “Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati—  ‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.
989Aṭṭhārasamaṁ.
