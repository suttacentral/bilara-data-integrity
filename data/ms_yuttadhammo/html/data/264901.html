53.
1110Āsavasampayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā…  tīṇi.
1111Āsavavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  āsavavippayuttaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  domanassasahagataṁ mohaṁ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ, ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ upādārūpaṁ. (1)
1112Āsavavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṁ mohaṁ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā. (2)
1113Āsavavippayuttaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavasampayutto ca āsavavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṁ mohaṁ paṭicca sampayuttakā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ. (3)
54.
1114Āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavasampayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṁ ekaṁ khandhañca mohañca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1115Āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavavippayutto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  āsavasampayutte khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ, domanassasahagate khandhe ca mohañca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (2)
1116Āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca āsavasampayutto ca āsavavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  domanassasahagataṁ ekaṁ khandhañca mohañca paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
1117(Evaṁ sabbe paccayā vitthāretabbā. Saṁkhittaṁ.)
