2.4.1.3 Talaghātakasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Lasuṇavagga
Talaghātakasikkhāpada
802.
2587Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena dve bhikkhuniyo anabhiratiyā pīḷitā ovarakaṁ pavisitvā talaghātakaṁ karonti. Bhikkhuniyo tena saddena upadhāvitvā tā bhikkhuniyo etadavocuṁ—  “kissa tumhe, ayye, purisena saddhiṁ sampadussathā”ti? “Na mayaṁ, ayye, purisena saddhiṁ sampadussāmā”ti bhikkhunīnaṁ etamatthaṁ ārocesuṁ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo talaghātakaṁ karissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṁ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo talaghātakaṁ karontī”ti? “Saccaṁ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo talaghātakaṁ karissanti. Netaṁ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṁ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṁ sikkhāpadaṁ uddisantu— 
803.
2588“Talaghātake pācittiyan”ti. (3:58)
804.
2589Talaghātakaṁ nāma samphassaṁ sādiyantī antamaso uppalapattenapi muttakaraṇe pahāraṁ deti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
805.
2590Anāpatti—  ābādhapaccayā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
2591Tatiyasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
