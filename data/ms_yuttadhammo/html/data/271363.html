2.3.2.2.4 Sappaṭighaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Cūḷantaraduka
Sappaṭighaduka
1095.
1764Katame dhammā sappaṭighā? Cakkhāyatanaṁ…pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṁ—  ime dhammā sappaṭighā.
1096.
1765Katame dhammā appaṭighā? Vedanākkhandho…pe…  viññāṇakkhandho, yañca rūpaṁ anidassanaṁ appaṭighaṁ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā appaṭighā.
