3.1.13 Muṭṭhassatisutta
Mātugāmasaṁyutta
Paṭhamapeyyālavagga
Muṭṭhassatisutta
292.
1265“Pañcahi, anuruddha, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo…pe… nirayaṁ upapajjati. Katamehi pañcahi? Assaddho ca hoti, ahiriko ca hoti, anottappī ca hoti, muṭṭhassati ca hoti, duppañño ca hoti— imehi kho, anuruddha, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo…pe… nirayaṁ upapajjatī”ti.
1266Terasamaṁ.
