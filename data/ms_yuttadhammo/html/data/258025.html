47 Sanidassanattika, Dassanahetuttika
Sanidassanattika, Dassanahetuttika
56.
177Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
178Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
179Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe… avigate ekavīsa.
