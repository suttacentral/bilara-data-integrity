44 Lokiyaduka, Sanidassanattika
Lokiyaduka, Sanidassanattika
92.
235Nalokiyaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṁ.
236Nalokuttaraṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  ekaṁ.
237(Gaṇitakena gaṇetabbā tīṇi pañhā. Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
238Nalokiyaṁ naanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo anidassanasappaṭigho… . (Tīṇiyeva pañhā kātabbā. Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe ekaṁ…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
239Nalokuttaraṁ naanidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca lokiyo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ…pe…  avigate ekaṁ.
