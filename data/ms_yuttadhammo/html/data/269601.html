4.2.7 Āvaṭṭahārasampāta
Paṭiniddesavāra
Sampāta
Āvaṭṭahārasampāta
69.
509Tattha katamo āvaṭṭo hārasampāto?
510“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. “Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti samatho. “Sammādiṭṭhipurekkhāro”ti vipassanā. “Ñatvāna udayabbayan”ti dukkhapariññā. “Thinamiddhābhibhū bhikkhū”ti samudayapahānaṁ. “Sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti nirodho. Imāni cattāri saccāni.
511Niyutto āvaṭṭo hārasampāto.
