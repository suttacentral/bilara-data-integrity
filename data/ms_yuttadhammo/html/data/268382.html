1.1.7 Sabbapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Sabbapariññāsutta
7.
32Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ—
33“Sabbaṁ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṁ aparijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ avirājayaṁ appajahaṁ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Sabbañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṁ parijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ virājayaṁ pajahaṁ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati—
34
“Yo sabbaṁ sabbato ñatvā,
sabbatthesu na rajjati;
Sa ve sabbapariññā so,
sabbadukkhamupaccagā”ti.
35Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
36Sattamaṁ.
