2.2.5 Orambhāgiyasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Satipaṭṭhānavagga
Orambhāgiyasutta
67.
448“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, orambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Sakkāyadiṭṭhi, vicikicchā, sīlabbataparāmāso, kāmacchando, byāpādo— imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcorambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni.
449Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ orambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ pahānāya…pe… ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.
450Pañcamaṁ.
