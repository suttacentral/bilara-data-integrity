1.3.1.6 Dutiyasamaṇasutta
Khandhasaṁyutta
Uparipaṇṇāsaka
Antavagga
Dutiyasamaṇasutta
108.
668Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, upādānakkhandhā. Katame pañca? Seyyathidaṁ— rūpupādānakkhandho, vedanupādānakkhandho, saññupādānakkhandho, saṅkhārupādānakkhandho, viññāṇupādānakkhandho. Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā imesaṁ pañcannaṁ upādānakkhandhānaṁ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṁ nappajānanti…pe… pajānanti, sayaṁ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharantī”ti.
669Chaṭṭhaṁ.
