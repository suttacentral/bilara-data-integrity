4.4.4 Sappurisadhammasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyamaggavagga
Sappurisadhammasutta
192.
1364“Sappurisadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asappurisadhammañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo.
1365Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo”ti.
1366Catutthaṁ.
