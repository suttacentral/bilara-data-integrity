190.
349Hetu saṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesiko dhammo hetussa saṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesikassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo… tīṇi.
350Hetu saṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesiko dhammo hetussa saṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesikassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… nava.
351Hetu saṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesiko dhammo hetussa saṁkiliṭṭhasaṁkilesikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇādhipati… nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)

352Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye tīṇi, jhāne tīṇi, magge tīṇi, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate nava.
