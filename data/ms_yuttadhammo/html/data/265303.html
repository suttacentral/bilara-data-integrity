2937Nocittasahabhuṁ dhammaṁ paccayā nocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā…pe…  cakkhāyatanaṁ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṁ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṁ…pe… . (Imaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānadukaṁ paccayavāre ārammaṇasadisaṁ. Channampi imesaṁ pañcaviññāṇamūlā kātabbā. Saṁkhittaṁ.)
