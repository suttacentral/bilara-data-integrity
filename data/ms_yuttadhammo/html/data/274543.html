187“Nāhaṁ, bhikkhave, aññaṁ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi, yena anuppanno vā ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo uppajjati, uppanno vā ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvanāpāripūriṁ gacchati, yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, sīlasampadā…pe… yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, chandasampadā…pe… yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, attasampadā…pe… yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, diṭṭhisampadā…pe… yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, appamādasampadā…pe….
