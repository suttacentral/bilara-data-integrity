82 Saraṇaduka, Parittārammaṇattika
Saraṇaduka, Parittārammaṇattika
94.
259Saraṇaṁ parittārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṁ parittārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo naparittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve…pe… avigate dve.
260Saraṇaṁ mahaggatārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo namahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṁ mahaggatārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo namahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve…pe… avigate dve.
261Araṇaṁ appamāṇārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo naappamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṁ…pe… avigate ekaṁ.
