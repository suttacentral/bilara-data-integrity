2572Acetasikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca acetasiko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— cittaṁ paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (Yāva asaññasattā.) (1)
2573Cetasikañca acetasikañca dhammaṁ paṭicca acetasiko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā— cetasike khandhe ca cittañca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ, cetasike khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (Paṭisandhikkhaṇe dvepi kātabbā, saṁkhittaṁ.)
