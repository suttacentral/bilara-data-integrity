3.1.15 Hotitathāgatosutta
Diṭṭhisaṁyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Hotitathāgatosutta
220.
965Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati— ‘hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe… niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
966Pannarasamaṁ.
