4.2.9 Parivattanahārasampāta
Paṭiniddesavāra
Sampāta
Parivattanahārasampāta
71.
515Tattha katamo parivattano hārasampāto?
516“Tasmā rakkhitacittassa, sammāsaṅkappagocaro”ti gāthā. Samathavipassanāya bhāvitāya nirodho phalaṁ, pariññātaṁ dukkhaṁ, samudayo pahīno, maggo bhāvito paṭipakkhena.
517Niyutto parivattano hārasampāto.
