1.13.3 Āsavasutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Esanāvagga
Āsavasutta
163.
314“Tayome, bhikkhave, āsavā. Katame tayo? Kāmāsavo, bhavāsavo, avijjāsavo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āsavā. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṁ āsavānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
315Tatiyaṁ.
