4.2.2 Sukhitasutta
Anamataggasaṁyutta
Dutiyavagga
Sukhitasutta
135.
866Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro…pe… yaṁ, bhikkhave, passeyyātha sukhitaṁ susajjitaṁ, niṭṭhamettha gantabbaṁ— ‘amhehipi evarūpaṁ paccanubhūtaṁ iminā dīghena addhunā’ti. Taṁ kissa hetu? Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro. Pubbā koṭi na paññāyati…pe… alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.
867Dutiyaṁ.
