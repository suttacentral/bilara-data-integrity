1.7.1.3 Suppaṭicchannasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Suppaṭicchannasikkhāpada
578.
1695Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kāyaṁ vivaritvā antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .
1696“Suppaṭicchanno antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (3:148)
1697Suppaṭicchannena antaraghare gantabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca kāyaṁ vivaritvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1698Anāpatti—  asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1699Tatiyasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
