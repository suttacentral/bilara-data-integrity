2.4.5.4 Gihiveyyāvaccasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Cittāgāravagga
Gihiveyyāvaccasikkhāpada
990.
3019Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṁ karonti. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṁ karissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṁ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṁ karontī”ti? “Saccaṁ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṁ karissanti. Netaṁ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṁ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṁ sikkhāpadaṁ uddisantu— 
991.
3020“Yā pana bhikkhunī gihiveyyāvaccaṁ kareyya, pācittiyan”ti. (44:99)
992.
3021Yā panāti yā yādisā…pe…  bhikkhunīti…pe…  ayaṁ imasmiṁ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
3022Gihiveyyāvaccaṁ nāma agārikassa yāguṁ vā bhattaṁ vā khādanīyaṁ vā pacati, sāṭakaṁ vā veṭhanaṁ vā dhovati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
993.
3023Anāpatti—  yāgupāne, saṁghabhatte, cetiyapūjāya, attano veyyāvaccakarassa yāguṁ vā bhattaṁ vā khādanīyaṁ vā pacati, sāṭakaṁ vā veṭhanaṁ vā dhovati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
3024Catutthasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
