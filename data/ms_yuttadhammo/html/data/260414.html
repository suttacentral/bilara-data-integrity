Paṇṇattivāra
Paṇṇattivāraniddesa

Paccanīka
141.
1016» Na cakkhu na indriyanti?
1017Cakkhuṁ ṭhapetvā avasesā indriyā na cakkhu, indriyā. Cakkhuñca indriye ca ṭhapetvā avasesā na ceva cakkhu na ca indriyā.
1018« Na indriyā na cakkhundriyanti? Āmantā.
