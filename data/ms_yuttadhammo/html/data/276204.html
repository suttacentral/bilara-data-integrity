1.1.8 Saṁyojanasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Dhanavagga
Saṁyojanasutta
8.
49“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, saṁyojanāni. Katamāni satta? Anunayasaṁyojanaṁ, paṭighasaṁyojanaṁ, diṭṭhisaṁyojanaṁ, vicikicchāsaṁyojanaṁ, mānasaṁyojanaṁ, bhavarāgasaṁyojanaṁ, avijjāsaṁyojanaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta saṁyojanānī”ti.
50Aṭṭhamaṁ.
