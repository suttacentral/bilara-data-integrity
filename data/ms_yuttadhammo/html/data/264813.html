720Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo— appaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ appaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṁ rūpānaṁ āhārapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. Kabaḷīkāro āhāro— imassa appaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (Avasesā dvepi pañhā kātabbā, paṭisandhi kabaḷīkāro āhāro dvīsupi kātabbo agge.) (3)
