75.
1652Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe satta, adhipatiyā dasa, anantare cha, samanantare cha, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe tīṇi, nissaye terasa, upanissaye nava, purejāte tīṇi, pacchājāte tīṇi, āsevane tīṇi, kamme satta, vipāke ekaṁ, āhāre satta, indriye jhāne magge satta, sampayutte tīṇi, vippayutte pañca, atthiyā terasa, natthiyā cha, vigate cha, avigate terasa. (Evaṁ gaṇetabbaṁ.)

1654Ācayagāmittikaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
