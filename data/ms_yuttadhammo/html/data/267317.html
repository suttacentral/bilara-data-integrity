19.3 Koṭumbariyattheraapadāna
Kuṭajapupphiyavagga
Koṭumbariyattheraapadāna
12.
2596
“Kaṇikāraṁva jotantaṁ,
nisinnaṁ pabbatantare;
Appameyyaṁva udadhiṁ,
vitthataṁ dharaṇiṁ yathā.
13.
2597
Pūjitaṁ devasaṅghena,
nisabhājāniyaṁ yathā;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
upāgacchiṁ naruttamaṁ.
14.
2598
Sattapupphāni paggayha,
koṭumbarasamākulaṁ;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṁ,
sikhino lokabandhuno.
15.
2599
Ekattiṁse ito kappe,
yaṁ pupphamabhiropayiṁ;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
16.
2600
Ito vīsatikappamhi,
mahānelasanāmako;
Eko āsi mahātejo,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
17.
2601
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
2602Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā koṭumbariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2603Koṭumbariyattherassāpadānaṁ tatiyaṁ.
