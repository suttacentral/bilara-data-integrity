196Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nasammattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
197Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ naaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho aniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
