Hetuduka, Duka
Hetuduka, āsavasāsavaduka
38.
111Hetuṁ āsavañceva sāsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu āsavo ceva sāsavo ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
112Hetuyā pañca…pe… avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
39.
113Hetuṁ sāsavañceva no ca āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
114Hetuyā nava…pe… avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
