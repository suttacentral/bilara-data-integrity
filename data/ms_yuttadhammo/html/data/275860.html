2.1.10 Dutiyavuḍḍhapabbajitasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Nīvaraṇavagga
Dutiyavuḍḍhapabbajitasutta
60.
394Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato dullabho vuḍḍhapabbajito. Katamehi pañcahi? Dullabho, bhikkhave, vuḍḍhapabbajito suvaco, dullabho suggahitaggāhī, dullabho padakkhiṇaggāhī, dullabho dhammakathiko, dullabho vinayadharo. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato dullabho vuḍḍhapabbajito”ti.
395Dasamaṁ.
396Nīvaraṇavaggo paṭhamo.
397Tassuddānaṁ
398
Āvaraṇaṁ rāsi aṅgāni,
samayaṁ mātuputtikā;
Upajjhā ṭhānā licchavi,
kumārā aparā duveti.
