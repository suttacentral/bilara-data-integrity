10.1.2 Sakkanāmasutta
Yakkhasaṁyutta
Indakavagga
Sakkanāmasutta
236.
1455Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Atha kho sakkanāmako yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
1456
“Sabbaganthappahīnassa,
vippamuttassa te sato;
Samaṇassa na taṁ sādhu,
yadaññamanusāsasī”ti.
1457
“Yena kenaci vaṇṇena,
saṁvāso sakka jāyati;
Na taṁ arahati sappañño,
manasā anukampituṁ.
1458
Manasā ce pasannena,
yadaññamanusāsati;
Na tena hoti saṁyutto,
yānukampā anuddayā”ti.
