2.3.3.14 Pubbantānudiṭṭhiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Pubbantānudiṭṭhiduka
1326.
2031Tattha katamā pubbantānudiṭṭhi? Pubbantaṁ ārabbha yā uppajjati diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṁ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṁ vuccati pubbantānudiṭṭhi.
1327.
2032Tattha katamā aparantānudiṭṭhi? Aparantaṁ ārabbha yā uppajjati diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṁ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṁ vuccati aparantānudiṭṭhi.
