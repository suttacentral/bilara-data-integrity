2.3.3.11 Bhavadiṭṭhiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Bhavadiṭṭhiduka
1320.
2025Tattha katamā bhavadiṭṭhi? Bhavissati attā ca loko cāti, yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṁ…pe… vipariyāsaggāho— ayaṁ vuccati bhavadiṭṭhi.
1321.
2026Tattha katamā vibhavadiṭṭhi? Na bhavissati attā ca loko cāti, yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṁ…pe… vipariyāsaggāho— ayaṁ vuccati vibhavadiṭṭhi.
