18 Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya
Kusalattika, Ajjhattattikadvaya
23.
76Nakusalo naajjhatto dhammo ajjhattassa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā soḷasa, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.
77Nakusalo nabahiddhā dhammo bahiddhā kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā cha, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa, purejāte atthiyā avigate nava.
24.
78Nakusalaṁ naajjhattārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
79Nakusalaṁ nabahiddhārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
