1.4.4.4 Adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhaniddesa
Mahāvagga
Indriyakathā
Catutthasuttantaniddesa
Adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhaniddesa
202.
1083Kathaṁ adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena indriyāni daṭṭhabbāni? Saddhindriyassa bhāvanāya chando uppajjati—  chandavasena saddhāvasena saddhindriyaṁ adhiṭṭhāti. Chandavasena pāmojjaṁ uppajjati—  pāmojjavasena saddhāvasena saddhindriyaṁ adhiṭṭhāti…pe…  evaṁ adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena indriyāni daṭṭhabbāni.
