3027Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo— purimā purimā anidassanaappaṭighā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ anidassanaappaṭighānaṁ khandhānaṁ āsevanapaccayena paccayo.
