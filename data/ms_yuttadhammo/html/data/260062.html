2.2.3.6 Paccanīkapuggalokāsa
Pavattivāra
Nirodhavāra
Anāgatavāra
Paccanīkapuggalokāsa
104.
536» Yassa yattha dukkhasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
537« Yassa vā pana yattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha dukkhasaccaṁ na nirujjhissatīti?
538Aggamaggasamaṅgīnaṁ arahantānaṁ yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṁ paṭilabhissanti asaññasattānaṁ tesaṁ tattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṁ tattha dukkhasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati. Pacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṁ tattha samudayasaccañca na nirujjhissati dukkhasaccañca na nirujjhissati.
539» Yassa yattha dukkhasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissatīti? Āmantā.
540« Yassa vā pana yattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha dukkhasaccaṁ na nirujjhissatīti?
541Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe arahantānaṁ ye ca puthujjanā maggaṁ na paṭilabhissanti āpāyikānaṁ asaññasattānaṁ tesaṁ tattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṁ tattha dukkhasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati. Pacchimacittassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe tesaṁ tattha maggasaccañca na nirujjhissati dukkhasaccañca na nirujjhissati.
105.
542» Yassa yattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissatīti?
543Aggamaggassa uppādakkhaṇe yassa cittassa anantarā aggamaggaṁ paṭilabhissanti tesaṁ tattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṁ tattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati. Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe arahantānaṁ asaññasattānaṁ tesaṁ tattha samudayasaccañca na nirujjhissati maggasaccañca na nirujjhissati.
544« Yassa vā pana yattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati tassa tattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissatīti?
545Āpāyikānaṁ ye ca puthujjanā maggaṁ na paṭilabhissanti tesaṁ tattha maggasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati, no ca tesaṁ tattha samudayasaccaṁ na nirujjhissati. Aggamaggassa bhaṅgakkhaṇe arahantānaṁ asaññasattānaṁ tesaṁ tattha maggasaccañca na nirujjhissati samudayasaccañca na nirujjhissati.
