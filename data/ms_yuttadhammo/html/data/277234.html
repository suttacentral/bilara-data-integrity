1.3.5.10 Dhammakathikapucchasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Navapurāṇavagga
Dhammakathikapucchasutta
155.
852Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe…  ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca—  “‘dhammakathiko, dhammakathiko’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, dhammakathiko hotī”ti?
853“Cakkhussa ce, bhikkhu, nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya dhammaṁ deseti, ‘dhammakathiko bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāya. Cakkhussa ce, bhikkhu, nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti, ‘dhammānudhammappaṭipanno bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāya. Cakkhussa ce, bhikkhu, nibbidā virāgā nirodhā anupādāvimutto hoti, ‘diṭṭhadhammanibbānappatto bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāya…pe…  jivhāya ce, bhikkhu, nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya dhammaṁ deseti, ‘dhammakathiko bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāya…pe…  manassa ce, bhikkhu, nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya dhammaṁ deseti, ‘dhammakathiko bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāya. Manassa ce, bhikkhu, nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti, ‘dhammānudhammappaṭipanno bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāya. Manassa ce, bhikkhu, nibbidā virāgā nirodhā anupādāvimutto hoti, ‘diṭṭhadhammanibbānappatto bhikkhū’ti alaṁvacanāyā”ti.
854Dasamaṁ.
855Navapurāṇavaggo pañcamo.
856Tassuddānaṁ
857
Kammaṁ cattāri sappāyā,
anantevāsi kimatthiyā;
Atthi nu kho pariyāyo,
indriyakathikena cāti.
858Saḷāyatanavagge tatiyapaṇṇāsako samatto.
859Tassa vagguddānaṁ
860
Yogakkhemi ca loko ca,
Gahapati devadahena ca;
Navapurāṇena paṇṇāso,
Tatiyo tena vuccatīti.
