1.2.1 Mohapariññāsutta
Ekakanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Mohapariññāsutta
11.
56Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ—
57“Mohaṁ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṁ aparijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ avirājayaṁ appajahaṁ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Mohañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṁ parijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ virājayaṁ pajahaṁ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati—
58
“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,
sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ;
Taṁ mohaṁ sammadaññāya,
pajahanti vipassino;
Pahāya na punāyanti,
imaṁ lokaṁ kudācanan”ti.
59Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
60Paṭhamaṁ.
