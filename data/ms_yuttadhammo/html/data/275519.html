3.5.10 Pubbaṇhasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Maṅgalavagga
Pubbaṇhasutta
156.
1089“Ye, bhikkhave, sattā pubbaṇhasamayaṁ kāyena sucaritaṁ caranti, vācāya sucaritaṁ caranti, manasā sucaritaṁ caranti, supubbaṇho, bhikkhave, tesaṁ sattānaṁ.
1090Ye, bhikkhave, sattā majjhanhikasamayaṁ kāyena sucaritaṁ caranti, vācāya sucaritaṁ caranti, manasā sucaritaṁ caranti, sumajjhanhiko, bhikkhave, tesaṁ sattānaṁ.
1091Ye, bhikkhave, sattā sāyanhasamayaṁ kāyena sucaritaṁ caranti, vācāya sucaritaṁ caranti, manasā sucaritaṁ caranti, susāyanho, bhikkhave, tesaṁ sattānanti.
1092
Sunakkhattaṁ sumaṅgalaṁ,
suppabhātaṁ suhuṭṭhitaṁ;
Sukhaṇo sumuhutto ca,
suyiṭṭhaṁ brahmacārisu.
1093
Padakkhiṇaṁ kāyakammaṁ,
vācākammaṁ padakkhiṇaṁ;
Padakkhiṇaṁ manokammaṁ,
paṇīdhi te padakkhiṇe;
Padakkhiṇāni katvāna,
labhantatthe padakkhiṇe.
1094
Te atthaladdhā sukhitā,
viruḷhā buddhasāsane;
Arogā sukhitā hotha,
saha sabbehi ñātibhī”ti.
1095Dasamaṁ.
1096Maṅgalavaggo pañcamo.
1097Tassuddānaṁ
1098
Akusalañca sāvajjaṁ,
visamāsucinā saha;
Caturo khatā vandanā,
pubbaṇhena ca te dasāti.
1099Tatiyo paṇṇāsako samatto.
