4.4.5 Uppādetabbadhammasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyamaggavagga
Uppādetabbadhammasutta
193.
1367“Uppādetabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na uppādetabbañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe… micchādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo.
1368Katamo ca, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe… sammādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo”ti.
1369Pañcamaṁ.
