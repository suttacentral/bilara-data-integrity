1.6.1 Adhammakammadvādasaka
Kammakkhandhaka
Āpattiyāappaṭikammeukkhepanīyakamma
Adhammakammadvādasaka
57.
267Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Asammukhā kataṁ hoti, appaṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
268Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Anāpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, desitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti…pe…  acodetvā kataṁ hoti, asāretvā kataṁ hoti, āpattiṁ anāropetvā kataṁ hoti…pe…  asammukhā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  appaṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  anāpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  desitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  acodetvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  asāretvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe…  āpattiṁ anāropetvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ, āpattiyā appaṭikamme, ukkhepanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
269Āpattiyā appaṭikamme ukkhepanīyakamme
adhammakammadvādasakaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
