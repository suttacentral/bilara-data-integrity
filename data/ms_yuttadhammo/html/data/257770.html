65 Sanidassanattika, Sārammaṇaduka
Sanidassanattika, Sārammaṇaduka
111.
271Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ sārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ sārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe…  anidassanaappaṭighaṁ sārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho nasārammaṇo ca naanidassanasappaṭigho nasārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
272Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ anārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
