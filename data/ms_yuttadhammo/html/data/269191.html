3.5.8 Udumbarajātaka
Tikanipāta
Kumbhavagga
Udumbarajātaka
142.
866
“Udumbarā cime pakkā,
nigrodhā ca kapitthanā;
Ehi nikkhama bhuñjassu,
kiṁ jighacchāya miyyasi”.
143.
867
“Evaṁ so suhito hoti,
yo vuḍḍhamapacāyati;
Yathāhamajja suhito,
dumapakkāni māsito”.
144.
868
“Yaṁ vanejo vanejassa,
vañceyya kapino kapi;
Daharo kapi saddheyya,
na hi jiṇṇo jarākapī”ti.
869Udumbarajātakaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.
