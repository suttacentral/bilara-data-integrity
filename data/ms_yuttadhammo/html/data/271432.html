2.3.2.13.9 Sukhasahagataduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Piṭṭhiduka
Sukhasahagataduka
1283.
1984Katame dhammā sukhasahagatā? Sukhabhūmiyaṁ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare apariyāpanne, sukhaṁ ṭhapetvā, taṁsampayutto saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho— ime dhammā sukhasahagatā.
1284.
1985Katame dhammā na sukhasahagatā? Na sukhabhūmiyaṁ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare arūpāvacare apariyāpanne; vedanākkhandho…pe… viññāṇakkhandho; sukhañca, sabbañca rūpaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu— ime dhammā na sukhasahagatā.
