3.2.2 Cakkhusutta
Tikanipāta
Dutiyavagga
Cakkhusutta
61.
393Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ—
394“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, cakkhūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Maṁsacakkhu, dibbacakkhu, paññācakkhu— imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi cakkhūnī”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati—
395
“Maṁsacakkhu dibbacakkhu,
paññācakkhu anuttaraṁ;
Etāni tīṇi cakkhūni,
akkhāsi purisuttamo.
396
Maṁsacakkhussa uppādo,
maggo dibbassa cakkhuno;
Yato ñāṇaṁ udapādi,
paññācakkhu anuttaraṁ;
Yassa cakkhussa paṭilābhā,
sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.
397Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
398Dutiyaṁ.
