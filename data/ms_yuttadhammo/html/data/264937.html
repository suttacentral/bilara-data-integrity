121.
1294Āsavo ceva āsavasampayutto ca dhammo āsavassa ceva āsavasampayuttassa ca dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
1295Āsavasampayutto ceva no ca āsavo dhammo āsavasampayuttassa ceva no ca āsavassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo—  āsavasampayuttā ceva no ca āsavā hetū sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ hetupaccayena paccayo. (1)
