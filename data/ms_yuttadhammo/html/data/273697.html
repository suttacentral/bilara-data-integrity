2.1.2 Dutiyakassapasutta
Devaputtasaṁyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Dutiyakassapasutta
83.
374Sāvatthinidānaṁ. Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho kassapo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi— 
375
“Bhikkhu siyā jhāyī vimuttacitto,
Ākaṅkhe ce hadayassānupattiṁ;
Lokassa ñatvā udayabbayañca,
Sucetaso anissito tadānisaṁso”ti.
