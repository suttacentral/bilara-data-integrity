1.4.1 Paṭhamapaṭipattisutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Paṭhamapaṭipattisutta
31.
117Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Micchāpaṭipattiñca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipattiñca. Taṁ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṁ—  micchādiṭṭhi…pe…  micchāsamādhi. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipatti. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṁ—  sammādiṭṭhi…pe…  sammāsamādhi. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipattī”ti.
118Paṭhamaṁ.
