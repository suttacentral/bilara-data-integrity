11 Dasuttarasutta
Dasuttarasutta
350.
1132Evaṁ me sutaṁ— ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā campāyaṁ viharati gaggarāya pokkharaṇiyā tīre mahatā bhikkhusaṁghena saddhiṁ pañcamattehi bhikkhusatehi. Tatra kho āyasmā sāriputto bhikkhū āmantesi— “āvuso bhikkhave”ti. “Āvuso”ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato sāriputtassa paccassosuṁ. Āyasmā sāriputto etadavoca—
Dasuttarasutta
1133
“Dasuttaraṁ pavakkhāmi,
dhammaṁ nibbānapattiyā;
Dukkhassantakiriyāya,
sabbaganthappamocanaṁ.
