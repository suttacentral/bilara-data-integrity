Hetvādi
401.
865Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo… tīṇi.
866Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… nava.
867Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati… tīṇi.
868Nahetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo nahetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati… tīṇi.
869Hetu appamāṇārammaṇo ca nahetu appamāṇārammaṇo ca dhammā hetussa appamāṇārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇādhipati… tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
402.
870Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, vipāke nava, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava…pe… avigate nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
