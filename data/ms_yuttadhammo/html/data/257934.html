47 Sāsavaduka, Sanidassanattika
Sāsavaduka, Sanidassanattika
95.
249Nasāsavaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… ekaṁ.
250Naanāsavaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sāsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… ekaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
251(Nasāsavaṁ naanidassanasappaṭighamūlassa purimanayena tīṇi pañhā.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… aññamaññe ekaṁ…pe… avigate tīṇi.
252Naanāsavaṁ naanidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sāsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ…pe… avigate ekaṁ.
