4.6.2 Dhammakammadvādasaka
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
Dhammakammadvādasaka
209.
862Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṁ tassapāpiyasikākammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. Sammukhā kataṁ hoti, paṭipucchākataṁ hoti, paṭiññāya kataṁ hoti…pe…  dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ tassapāpiyasikākammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca.
