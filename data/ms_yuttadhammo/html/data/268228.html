2.5.10 Abhabbasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Upālivagga
Abhabbasutta
100.
918“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. Katame dasa? Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ, mānaṁ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ.
919Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. Katame dasa? Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ, mānaṁ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātun”ti.
920Dasamaṁ.
921Upālivaggo pañcamo.
922Tassuddānaṁ
923
Kāmabhogī bhayaṁ diṭṭhi,
vajjiyamāhituttiyā;
Kokanudo āhuneyyo,
thero upāli abhabboti.
