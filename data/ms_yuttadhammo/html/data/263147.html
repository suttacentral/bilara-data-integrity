7.
2172Parittārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— parittārammaṇe khandhe paṭicca parittārammaṇā cetanā. (1)
2173Mahaggatārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— mahaggatārammaṇe khandhe paṭicca mahaggatārammaṇā cetanā. (1)
2174Appamāṇārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā— appamāṇārammaṇe khandhe paṭicca appamāṇārammaṇā cetanā. (1)
