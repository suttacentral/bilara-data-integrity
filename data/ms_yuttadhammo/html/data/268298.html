4.3.3 Kusalasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Kusalasutta
180.
1327“Kusalañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi akusalañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamañca, bhikkhave, akusalaṁ? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, akusalaṁ.
1328Katamañca, bhikkhave, kusalaṁ? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, kusalan”ti.
1329Tatiyaṁ.
