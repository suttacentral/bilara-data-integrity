8 Kusalattika, Dassanahetuttika
Kusalattika, Dassanahetuttika
20.
50Akusalaṁ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
51Akusalaṁ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
52Akusalaṁ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
53Abyākataṁ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā naabyākato nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
