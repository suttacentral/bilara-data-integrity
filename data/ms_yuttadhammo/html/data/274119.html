9.1.11 Siṅgālasutta
Opammasaṁyutta
Opammavagga
Siṅgālasutta
233.
1243Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Assuttha no tumhe, bhikkhave, rattiyā paccūsasamayaṁ jarasiṅgālassa vassamānassā”ti? “Evaṁ, bhante”. “Eso kho, bhikkhave, jarasiṅgālo ukkaṇḍakena nāma rogajātena phuṭṭho. So yena yena icchati tena tena gacchati; yattha yattha icchati tattha tattha tiṭṭhati; yattha yattha icchati tattha tattha nisīdati; yattha yattha icchati tattha tattha nipajjati; sītakopi naṁ vāto upavāyati. Sādhu khvassa, bhikkhave, yaṁ idhekacco sakyaputtiyapaṭiñño evarūpampi attabhāvapaṭilābhaṁ paṭisaṁvediyetha. Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, evaṁ sikkhitabbaṁ— ‘appamattā viharissāmā’ti. Evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.
1244Ekādasamaṁ.
