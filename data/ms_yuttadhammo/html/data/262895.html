847Dassanena pahātabbaṁ…pe…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe dassanena pahātabbaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca…pe… . (1)
848Bhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ…pe…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe bhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ…pe… . (1)
849Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ…pe…  navippayuttapaccayā—  arūpe nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca…pe…  bāhiraṁ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṁ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṁ…  asaññasattānaṁ…pe… . (1)
