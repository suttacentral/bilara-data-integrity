3.1.4 Dutiyavedanāsutta
Tikanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dutiyavedanāsutta
53.
344Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ—
345“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā. Sukhā, bhikkhave, vedanā dukkhato daṭṭhabbā; dukkhā vedanā sallato daṭṭhabbā; adukkhamasukhā vedanā aniccato daṭṭhabbā. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno sukhā vedanā dukkhato diṭṭhā hoti, dukkhā vedanā sallato diṭṭhā hoti, adukkhamasukhā vedanā aniccato diṭṭhā hoti; ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘bhikkhu ariyo sammaddaso acchecchi, taṇhaṁ, vivattayi saṁyojanaṁ, sammā mānābhisamayā antamakāsi dukkhassā’”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati—
346
“Yo sukhaṁ dukkhato adda,
Dukkhamaddakkhi sallato;
Adukkhamasukhaṁ santaṁ,
Adakkhi naṁ aniccato.
347
Sa ve sammaddaso bhikkhu,
yato tattha vimuccati;
Abhiññāvosito santo,
sa ve yogātigo munī”ti.
348Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
349Catutthaṁ.
