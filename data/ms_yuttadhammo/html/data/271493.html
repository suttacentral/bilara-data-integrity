2.4.2.10.10 Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhūduka
1543.
2254Katame dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho— ime dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
1544.
2255Katame dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca— ime dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno.
