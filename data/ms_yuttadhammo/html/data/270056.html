16.2.6 Chakkaniddesa
Ñāṇavibhaṅga
Niddesa
Chakkaniddesa
805.
2547Tattha katamā chasu abhiññāsu paññā? Iddhividhe ñāṇaṁ, sotadhātuvisuddhiyā ñāṇaṁ, paracitte ñāṇaṁ, pubbenivāsānussatiyā ñāṇaṁ, sattānaṁ cutūpapāte ñāṇaṁ, āsavānaṁ khaye ñāṇaṁ— imā chasu abhiññāsu paññā. Evaṁ chabbidhena ñāṇavatthu.
2548Chakkaṁ.
