910Lokiyo dhammo lokiyassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā lokiyā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ lokiyānaṁ khandhānaṁ āsevanapaccayena paccayo; anulomaṁ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṁ vodānassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (1)
911Lokiyo dhammo lokuttarassa dhammassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo—  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṁ maggassa āsevanapaccayena paccayo. (2)
