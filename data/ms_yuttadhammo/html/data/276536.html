1.7.1.5 Susaṁvutasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Parimaṇḍalavagga
Susaṁvutasikkhāpada
580.
1705Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū hatthampi pādampi kīḷāpentā antaraghare gacchanti…pe….
1706“Susaṁvuto antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (5:150)
1707Susaṁvutena antaraghare gantabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca hatthaṁ vā pādaṁ vā kīḷāpento antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1708Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1709Pañcamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
