1.5.7 Jāṇussoṇisutta
Nidānasaṁyutta
Gahapativagga
Jāṇussoṇisutta
47.
337Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. Atha kho jāṇussoṇi brāhmaṇo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavatā saddhiṁ…pe…  ekamantaṁ nisinno kho jāṇussoṇi brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṁ etadavoca— 
338“Kiṁ nu kho, bho gotama, sabbamatthī”ti? “‘Sabbamatthī’ti kho, brāhmaṇa, ayameko anto”.
339“Kiṁ pana, bho gotama, sabbaṁ natthī”ti? “‘Sabbaṁ natthī’ti kho, brāhmaṇa, ayaṁ dutiyo anto. Ete te, brāhmaṇa, ubho ante anupagamma majjhena tathāgato dhammaṁ deseti—  ‘avijjāpaccayā saṅkhārā; saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṁ…pe…  evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti. Avijjāya tveva asesavirāganirodhā saṅkhāranirodho; saṅkhāranirodhā viññāṇanirodho…pe…  evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hotī’”ti.
340Evaṁ vutte, jāṇussoṇi brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṁ etadavoca—  “abhikkantaṁ, bho gotama…pe…  pāṇupetaṁ saraṇaṁ gatan”ti.
341Sattamaṁ.
