1.14.1 Oghasutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Oghavagga
Oghasutta
172.
335Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā. Katame cattāro? Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho— ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro oghā. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṁ oghānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe… ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti. (Yathā esanā, evaṁ vitthāretabbaṁ.)
336Paṭhamaṁ.
