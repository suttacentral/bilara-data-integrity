32.
2821Ajjhatto dhammo ajjhattassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo— purimā purimā ajjhattā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ ajjhattānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṁ gotrabhussa, anulomaṁ vodānassa, gotrabhu maggassa, vodānaṁ maggassa, maggo phalassa, phalaṁ phalassa, anulomaṁ phalasamāpattiyā, nirodhā vuṭṭhahantassa nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṁ phalasamāpattiyā anantarapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2822Bahiddhā dhammo bahiddhā dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (Purimā purimā bahiddhāti nānākaraṇaṁ, taṁyeva gamanaṁ.) (1)
