2.1.11 Abhabbasutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Khemavagga
Abhabbasutta
62.
430“Nava, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. Katame nava? Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ.
431Nava, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ. Katame nava? Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ—  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātun”ti.
432Ekādasamaṁ.
433Khemavaggo paṭhamo.
434Tassuddānaṁ
435
Khemo ca amatañceva,
Abhayaṁ passaddhiyena ca;
Nirodho anupubbo ca,
Dhammaṁ pahāya bhabbena cāti.
