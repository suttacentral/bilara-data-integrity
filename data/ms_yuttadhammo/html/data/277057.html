1.3.2.8 Saṁyojaniyasutta
Khandhasaṁyutta
Uparipaṇṇāsaka
Dhammakathikavagga
Saṁyojaniyasutta
120.
696Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Saṁyojaniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi saṁyojanañca. Taṁ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyā dhammā, katamaṁ saṁyojanaṁ? Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyo dhammo; yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha saṁyojanaṁ. Vedanā…pe… saññā… saṅkhārā… viññāṇaṁ saṁyojaniyo dhammo; yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha saṁyojanaṁ. Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyā dhammā, idaṁ saṁyojanan”ti.
697Aṭṭhamaṁ.
