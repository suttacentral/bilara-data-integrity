7.
1630Ganthaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca gantho dhammo uppajjati napurejātapaccayā— arūpe idaṁsaccābhinivesaṁ kāyaganthaṁ paṭicca abhijjhākāyagantho, abhijjhākāyaganthaṁ paṭicca idaṁsaccābhiniveso kāyagantho (arūpe sīlabbataparāmāso natthi, evaṁ nava pañhā kātabbā), napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṁ, naindriye ekaṁ, najhāne ekaṁ, namagge ekaṁ, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte nava, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi.
