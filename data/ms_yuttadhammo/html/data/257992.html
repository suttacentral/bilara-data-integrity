14 Kusalattika, Micchattaniyatattika
Kusalattika, Micchattaniyatattika
18.
63Nakusalaṁ namicchattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā akusalo micchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
64Nakusalaṁ nasammattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā kusalo sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
65Nakusalaṁ naaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato aniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe… avigate pañca.
