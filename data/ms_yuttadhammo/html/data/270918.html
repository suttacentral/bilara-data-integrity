6.2.10.2 Rūpāvacarakusalacitta
Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Kusalaniddesa
Rūpāvacarakusalacitta
300.
1212Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṁ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṁ bhāveti vivicceva kāmehi…pe… paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharati pathavīkasiṇaṁ, tasmiṁ samaye kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṁ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṁ, nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṁ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayā pasādo, pasādapaccayā adhimokkho, adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ. Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.
301.
1213Tattha katame kusalamūlā? Alobho, adoso, amoho— ime vuccanti “kusalamūlā”.
1214Tattha katamo kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṁ— ayaṁ vuccati “kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro” …pe… tena vuccati “evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti.
