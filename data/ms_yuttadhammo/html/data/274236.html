6.1.8 Taṇhāsutta
Kilesasaṁyutta
Kilesavagga
Taṇhāsutta
329.
1107Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Yo, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yo saddataṇhāya…  yo gandhataṇhāya…  yo rasataṇhāya…  yo phoṭṭhabbataṇhāya…  yo dhammataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno…pe…  abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.
1108Aṭṭhamaṁ.
