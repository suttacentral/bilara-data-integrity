44 Ajjhattattika, Hetuduka
Ajjhattattika, Hetuduka
86.
219Ajjhattaṁ hetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabahiddhā nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bahiddhā hetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhatto nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā dve…pe… avigate dve.
220Ajjhattaṁ nahetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabahiddhā nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Bahiddhā nahetuṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhatto nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā dve…pe… avigate dve.
