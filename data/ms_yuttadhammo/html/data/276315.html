1.2.8 Dutiyabandhanasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Mahāvagga
Dutiyabandhanasutta
18.
178“Aṭṭhahi, bhikkhave, ākārehi puriso itthiṁ bandhati. Katamehi aṭṭhahi? Ruṇṇena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; hasitena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; bhaṇitena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ākappena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; vanabhaṅgena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; gandhena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; rasena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; phassena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭhahākārehi puriso itthiṁ bandhati. Te, bhikkhave, sattā subaddhā, ye phassena baddhā”ti.
179Aṭṭhamaṁ.
