1.4.1.11 Sakkāyadiṭṭhipahānasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Nandikkhayavagga
Sakkāyadiṭṭhipahānasutta
166.
883Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu…pe… etadavoca— “kathaṁ nu kho, bhante, jānato kathaṁ passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyatī”ti? “Cakkhuṁ kho, bhikkhu, dukkhato jānato passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyati. Rūpe dukkhato jānato passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyati. Cakkhuviññāṇaṁ dukkhato jānato passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyati. Cakkhusamphassaṁ dukkhato jānato passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyati…pe… yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi dukkhato jānato passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyati. Evaṁ kho, bhikkhu, jānato evaṁ passato sakkāyadiṭṭhi pahīyatī”ti.
884Ekādasamaṁ.
