159.
440Nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe… paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (1)
441Nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— nahetū sahetuke khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (2)
442Nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ca nahetu ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā— nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe… dve khandhe…pe… paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (3)
160.
443Nahetuṁ ahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…pe… ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ paṭicca…pe… mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ kaṭattārūpaṁ upādārūpaṁ. (1)
444Nahetuṁ ahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṁ paṭicca nahetū sahetukā khandhā. (2)
445Nahetuṁ ahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ca nahetu ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā— paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthuṁ paṭicca nahetū sahetukā khandhā, mahābhūte paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṁ. (3)
161.
446Nahetuṁ sahetukañca nahetuṁ ahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— paṭisandhikkhaṇe nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ ekaṁ khandhañca vatthuñca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe ca…pe…. (1)
447Nahetuṁ sahetukañca nahetuṁ ahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu ahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— nahetū sahetuke khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (2)
448Nahetuṁ sahetukañca nahetuṁ ahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu sahetuko ca nahetu ahetuko ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā— paṭisandhikkhaṇe nahetuṁ sahetukaṁ ekaṁ khandhañca vatthuñca paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe… nahetū sahetuke khandhe ca mahābhūte ca paṭicca kaṭattārūpaṁ. (3)
