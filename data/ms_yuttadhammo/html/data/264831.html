809Arūpī dhammo arūpissa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā arūpino khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ arūpīnaṁ khandhānaṁ…pe…  phalasamāpattiyā anantarapaccayena paccayo, samanantarapaccayena paccayo.
