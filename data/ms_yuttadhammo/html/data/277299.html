9.2.1.2 Vipassanā
Asaṅkhatasaṁyutta
Dutiyavagga
Asaṅkhatasutta
Vipassanā
1697Asaṅkhatañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asaṅkhatagāmiñca maggaṁ. Taṁ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhataṁ? Yo, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—  idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhataṁ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Vipassanā. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo. Iti kho, bhikkhave, desitaṁ vo mayā asaṅkhataṁ…pe…  ayaṁ vo amhākaṁ anusāsanīti. (1.2)
