7 Kusalattika, Dassanattika
Kusalattika, Dassanattika
19.
47Akusalaṁ dassanena pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
48Akusalaṁ bhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naakusalo nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
49Abyākataṁ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā naabyākato nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
