45.
3034Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo— anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ anidassanaappaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṁ rūpānaṁ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ anidassanaappaṭighānañca kaṭattārūpānaṁ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Kabaḷīkāro āhāro— imassa anidassanaappaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3035Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo— anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṁ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṁ rūpānaṁ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṁ kaṭattārūpānaṁ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Kabaḷīkāro āhāro— imassa sanidassanasappaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3036(Evaṁ satta pañhā pavattipaṭisandhi vibhajitabbā, sattasupi kabaḷīkāro āhāro kātabbo.) (7)
