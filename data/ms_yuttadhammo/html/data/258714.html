17.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra
Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduka
Paṭiccādivāra
20.
68Āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi.
69Āsavasampayuttañceva no ca āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naāsavavippayutto ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi.
70Āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca āsavavippayuttañceva no ca āsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
71Hetuyā nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
