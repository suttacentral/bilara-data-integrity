Paccanīyuddhāra
87.
739Sappaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
740Sappaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo, sahajātapaccayena paccayo… upanissayapaccayena paccayo… purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
741Sappaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
88.
742Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… sahajātapaccayena paccayo… upanissayapaccayena paccayo… purejātapaccayena paccayo… pacchājātapaccayena paccayo… kammapaccayena paccayo… āhārapaccayena paccayo… indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
743Appaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo… pacchājātapaccayena paccayo… kammapaccayena paccayo… āhārapaccayena paccayo… indriyapaccayena paccayo. (2)
744Appaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo… pacchājātapaccayena paccayo… kammapaccayena paccayo… āhārapaccayena paccayo… indriyapaccayena paccayo. (3)
89.
745Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā sappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
746Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā appaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātaṁ, purejātaṁ. (2)
747Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
