59.
3066Sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3067Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3068Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3069Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
3070Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)
3071Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (5)
3072Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (6)
3073Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (7)
60.
3074Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3075Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3076Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (3)
3077Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (4)
3078Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (5)
3079Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (6)
3080Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  kammapaccayena paccayo…  āhārapaccayena paccayo…  indriyapaccayena paccayo. (7)
61.
3081Sanidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa purejātaṁ. (1)
3082Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3083Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3084Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātaṁ, purejātaṁ. (3)
3085Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (4)
3086Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (5)
3087Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (6)
3088Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā sanidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanasappaṭighassa ca anidassanaappaṭighassa ca dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (7)
62.
3089Sanidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanasappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa purejātaṁ. (1)
3090Sanidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo…  purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
