34 Sārammaṇaduka
Sārammaṇaduka
53.
167Nasārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasārammaṇo ca naanārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
168Naanārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naanārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naanārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasārammaṇo ca naanārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
169Nasārammaṇañca naanārammaṇañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nasārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇañca naanārammaṇañca dhammaṁ paṭicca naanārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nasārammaṇañca naanārammaṇañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nasārammaṇo ca naanārammaṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3) (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
