1620Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya atthi avigatanti nahetuyā satta…pe…  naaññamaññe tīṇi, naupanissaye satta…pe…  nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte tīṇi, nonatthiyā satta, novigate satta.
1621Magga adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya indriya sampayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  novigate tīṇi.
1622Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya indriya vippayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā tīṇi…pe…  novigate tīṇi. [Avipāka—  3]
1623Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya atthi avigatanti nahetuyā ekaṁ…pe…  novigate ekaṁ.
1624Magga adhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya vipāka indriya sampayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā ekaṁ…pe…  novigate ekaṁ.
1625Magga adhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka indriya vippayutta atthi avigatanti nahetuyā ekaṁ…pe…  novigate ekaṁ. [Savipāka—  3]
