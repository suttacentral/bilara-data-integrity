3.4.10 Dukkhudrayasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Dukkhudrayasutta
143.
1124“Dukkhudrayañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi sukhudrayañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhudrayo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dukkhudrayo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukhudrayo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukhudrayo dhammo”ti.
1125Dasamaṁ.
