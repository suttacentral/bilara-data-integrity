6.4.3 Kusaladhammasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṁyutta
Catutthavagga
Kusaladhammasutta
182.
1088Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtassa pariyādiṇṇacittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa kusalo dhammo samucchedamagamā. Evaṁ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe… sikkhitabban”ti.
1089Tatiyaṁ.
