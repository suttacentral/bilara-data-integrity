5.1.2 Dhammassavanasutta
Pañcamapaṇṇāsaka
Kimilavagga
Dhammassavanasutta
202.
1194“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṁsā dhammassavane. Katame pañca? Assutaṁ suṇāti, sutaṁ pariyodāpeti, kaṅkhaṁ vitarati, diṭṭhiṁ ujuṁ karoti, cittamassa pasīdati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṁsā dhammassavane”ti.
1195Dutiyaṁ.
