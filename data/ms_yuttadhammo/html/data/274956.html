11.5.10 Aṅgasutta
Sotāpattisaṁyutta
Sagāthakapuññābhisandavagga
Aṅgasutta
1046.
2086“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, sotāpattiyaṅgāni. Katamāni cattāri? Sappurisasaṁsevo, saddhammassavanaṁ, yonisomanasikāro, dhammānudhammappaṭipatti—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri sotāpattiyaṅgānī”ti.
2087Dasamaṁ.
2088Sagāthakapuññābhisandavaggo pañcamo.
2089Tassuddānaṁ
2090
Abhisandā tayo vuttā,
Duve mahaddhanena ca;
Suddhaṁ nandiyaṁ bhaddiyaṁ,
Mahānāmaṅgena te dasāti.
