1.1.2.7.3 Khambhakatavagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
177.
513Anādariyaṁ paṭicca khambhakato antaraghare gacchanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (1)
514Anādariyaṁ paṭicca khambhakato antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (2)
515Anādariyaṁ paṭicca oguṇṭhito antaraghare gacchanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (3)
516Anādariyaṁ paṭicca oguṇṭhito antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (4)
517Anādariyaṁ paṭicca ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (5)
518Anādariyaṁ paṭicca pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (6)
519Anādariyaṁ paṭicca asakkaccaṁ piṇḍapātaṁ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (7)
520Anādariyaṁ paṭicca tahaṁ tahaṁ olokento piṇḍapātaṁ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (8)
521Anādariyaṁ paṭicca sūpaññeva bahuṁ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (9)
522Anādariyaṁ paṭicca thūpīkataṁ piṇḍapātaṁ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (10)
523Khambhakatavaggo tatiyo.
