3.3.4 Suddhasutta
Satipaṭṭhānasaṁyutta
Sīlaṭṭhitivagga
Suddhasutta
390.
874Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Cattārome, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ; vedanāsu…pe… citte…pe… dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro satipaṭṭhānā”ti.
875Catutthaṁ.
