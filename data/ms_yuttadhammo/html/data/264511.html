1095Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya āhāra atthi avigatanti satta. Indriyādhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya āhāra sampayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya āhāra vippayutta atthi avigatanti tīṇi. [Avipāka— 3]
1096Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka āhāra atthi avigatanti ekaṁ. Indriyādhipati sahajāta aññamañña nissaya vipāka āhāra sampayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṁ. Indriyādhipati sahajāta nissaya vipāka āhāra vippayutta atthi avigatanti ekaṁ. [Savipāka— 3]
