1.1.2.7.6 Surusuruvagga
Bhikkhuvibhaṅga
Paṭhamabhāga
Katāpattivāra
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
180.
546Anādariyaṁ paṭicca surusurukārakaṁ bhuñjanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (1)
547Anādariyaṁ paṭicca hatthanillehakaṁ bhuñjanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (2)
548Anādariyaṁ paṭicca pattanillehakaṁ bhuñjanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (3)
549Anādariyaṁ paṭicca oṭṭhanillehakaṁ bhuñjanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (4)
550Anādariyaṁ paṭicca sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggaṇhanto ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (5)
551Anādariyaṁ paṭicca sasitthakaṁ pattadhovanaṁ antaraghare chaḍḍento ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (6)
552Anādariyaṁ paṭicca chattapāṇissa dhammaṁ desento ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (7)
553Anādariyaṁ paṭicca daṇḍapāṇissa dhammaṁ desento ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (8)
554Anādariyaṁ paṭicca satthapāṇissa dhammaṁ desento ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (9)
555Anādariyaṁ paṭicca āvudhapāṇissa dhammaṁ desento ekaṁ āpattiṁ āpajjati. Dukkaṭaṁ. (10)
556Surusuruvaggo chaṭṭho.
