1902Nonīvaraṇaṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nīvaraṇo akusalo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā— vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca avijjānīvaraṇaṁ. (1)
