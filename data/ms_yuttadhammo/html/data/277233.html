1.3.5.9 Indriyasampannasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Navapurāṇavagga
Indriyasampannasutta
154.
849Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe… ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca— “‘indriyasampanno, indriyasampanno’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, indriyasampanno hotī”ti?
850“Cakkhundriye ce, bhikkhu, udayabbayānupassī viharanto cakkhundriye nibbindati…pe… jivhindriye ce, bhikkhu, udayabbayānupassī viharanto jivhindriye nibbindati…pe… manindriye ce, bhikkhu, udayabbayānupassī viharanto manindriye nibbindati. Nibbindaṁ virajjati…pe… vimuttasmiṁ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṁ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti. Ettāvatā kho, bhikkhu, indriyasampanno hotī”ti.
851Navamaṁ.
