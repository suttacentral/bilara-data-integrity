1.3.3 Ubhayatthasutta
Ekakanipāta
Tatiyavagga
Ubhayatthasutta
23.
128Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ— 
129“Ekadhammo, bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati—  diṭṭhadhammikañceva atthaṁ samparāyikañca. Katamo ekadhammo? Appamādo kusalesu dhammesu. Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, ekadhammo bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati—  diṭṭhadhammikañceva atthaṁ samparāyikañcā”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati— 
130
“Appamādaṁ pasaṁsanti,
puññakiriyāsu paṇḍitā;
Appamatto ubho atthe,
adhigaṇhāti paṇḍito.
131
Diṭṭhe dhamme ca yo attho,
yo cattho samparāyiko;
Atthābhisamayā dhīro,
paṇḍitoti pavuccatī”ti.
132Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
133Tatiyaṁ.
