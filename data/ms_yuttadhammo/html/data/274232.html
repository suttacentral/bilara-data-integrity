6.1.4 Samphassasutta
Kilesasaṁyutta
Kilesavagga
Samphassasutta
325.
1099Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Yo, bhikkhave, cakkhusamphassasmiṁ chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yo sotasamphassasmiṁ…  yo ghānasamphassasmiṁ…  yo jivhāsamphassasmiṁ…  yo kāyasamphassasmiṁ…  yo manosamphassasmiṁ chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno…pe…  abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.
1100Catutthaṁ.
