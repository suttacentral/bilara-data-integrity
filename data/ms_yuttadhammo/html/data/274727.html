3.4.9 Bhāvanāsutta
Satipaṭṭhānasaṁyutta
Ananussutavagga
Bhāvanāsutta
405.
930“Catunnaṁ, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvanaṁ desessāmi. Taṁ suṇātha. Katamā, bhikkhave, catunnaṁ satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvanā? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ; vedanāsu…pe…  citte…pe…  dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ. Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṁ satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvanā”ti.
