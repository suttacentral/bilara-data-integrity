3145Ajjhattikaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā ajjhattiko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā—  ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṁ paccayā ajjhattikaṁ kaṭattārūpaṁ, cakkhāyatanaṁ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ. Evaṁ navapi pañhā kātabbā. Pañcaviññāṇampi pavesetvā tīṇiyeva moho.)
