2.4.2.10.9 Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaduka
1541.
2252Katame dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho— ime dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā.
1542.
2253Katame dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca— ime dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā.
