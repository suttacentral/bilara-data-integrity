12.3.3 Sammāsambuddhasutta
Saccasaṁyutta
Koṭigāmavagga
Sammāsambuddhasutta
1093.
2278Sāvatthinidānaṁ. Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, ariyasaccāni. Katamāni cattāri? Dukkhaṁ ariyasaccaṁ…pe… dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā ariyasaccaṁ— imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri ariyasaccāni. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṁ ariyasaccānaṁ yathābhūtaṁ abhisambuddhattā tathāgato ‘arahaṁ sammāsambuddho’ti vuccati.
2279Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṁ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo…pe… ‘ayaṁ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2280Tatiyaṁ.
