4.3.4 Atthasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Atthasutta
181.
1330“Atthañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anatthañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, anattho? Pāṇātipāto…pe… micchādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anattho.
1331Katamo ca, bhikkhave, attho? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe… sammādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, attho”ti.
1332Catutthaṁ.
