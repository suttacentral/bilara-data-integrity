1.3.1 Adhammakammadvādasaka
Kammakkhandhaka
Pabbājanīyakamma
Adhammakammadvādasaka
25.
129“Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṁ pabbājanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Asammukhā kataṁ hoti, appaṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti— imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ pabbājanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
130Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ pabbājanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Anāpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, desitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti…pe… acodetvā kataṁ hoti, asāretvā kataṁ hoti, āpattiṁ anāropetvā kataṁ hoti…pe… asammukhā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… appaṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… anāpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… adesanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… desitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… acodetvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… asāretvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti…pe… āpattiṁ anāropetvā kataṁ hoti, adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti— imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ pabbājanīyakammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
131Adhammakammadvādasakaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
