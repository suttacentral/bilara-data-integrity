105Vipākadhammadhammaṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca navipākadhammadhammo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
