301.
3057Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo— cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāne khandhe ārabbha cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā khandhā uppajjanti. (Mūlaṁ kātabbaṁ.) Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāne khandhe ārabbha cittaṁ uppajjati. (Mūlaṁ kātabbaṁ.) Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāne khandhe ārabbha cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā uppajjanti. (3)
3058Nocittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo— ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā maggaṁ paccavekkhanti…pe… nibbānaṁ paccavekkhanti. Nibbānaṁ gotrabhussa…pe…. (Saṁkhittaṁ. Yathā cittasahabhūduke ārammaṇaṁ evaṁ kātabbaṁ, ninnānākaraṇaṁ. Navapi pañhā.)
