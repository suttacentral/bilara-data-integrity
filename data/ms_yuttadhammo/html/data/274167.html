2.2.10 Vayadhammasutta
Rādhasaṁyutta
Dutiyamāravagga
Vayadhammasutta
179.
866Sāvatthinidānaṁ. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca—  “‘vayadhammo, vayadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, vayadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṁ kho, rādha, vayadhammo, vedanā vayadhammo, saññā vayadhammo, saṅkhārā vayadhammo, viññāṇaṁ vayadhammo. Evaṁ passaṁ…pe…  nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
867Dasamaṁ.
