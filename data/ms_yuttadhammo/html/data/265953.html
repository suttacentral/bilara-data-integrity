1224Nahetuṁ anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṁ anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2)
1225Hetuṁ anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nahetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi.
1226Hetuṁ anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca hetu anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
548.
1227Nahetuyā dve, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā nava…pe…  napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, nakamme tīṇi, navipāke nava, naāhāre ekaṁ, naindriye ekaṁ, najhāne ekaṁ, namagge ekaṁ, nasampayutte tīṇi, navippayutte nava, nonatthiyā tīṇi, novigate tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
