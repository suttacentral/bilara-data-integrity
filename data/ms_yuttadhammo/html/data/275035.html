12.6.5 Paṭhamamahāpathavīsutta
Saccasaṁyutta
Abhisamayavagga
Paṭhamamahāpathavīsutta
1125.
2417“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, puriso mahāpathaviyā satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā upanikkhipeyya. Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, katamaṁ nu kho bahutaraṁ— yā vā satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā upanikkhittā, ayaṁ vā mahāpathavī”ti? “Etadeva, bhante, bahutaraṁ, yadidaṁ— mahāpathavī; appamattikā satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā upanikkhittā. Saṅkhampi na upenti, upanidhampi na upenti, kalabhāgampi na upenti mahāpathaviṁ upanidhāya satta kolaṭṭhimattiyo guḷikā upanikkhittā”ti. “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa…pe… yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2418Pañcamaṁ.
