1.3.4.8 Ajjhattadukkhahetusutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Devadahavagga
Ajjhattadukkhahetusutta
141.
797“Cakkhuṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ. Yopi hetu yopi paccayo cakkhussa uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Dukkhasambhūtaṁ, bhikkhave, cakkhu kuto sukhaṁ bhavissati…pe… jivhā dukkhā. Yopi hetu, yopi paccayo jivhāya uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Dukkhasambhūtā, bhikkhave, jivhā kuto sukhā bhavissati…pe… mano dukkho. Yopi hetu yopi paccayo manassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Dukkhasambhūto, bhikkhave, mano kuto sukho bhavissati. Evaṁ passaṁ…pe… nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.
798Aṭṭhamaṁ.
