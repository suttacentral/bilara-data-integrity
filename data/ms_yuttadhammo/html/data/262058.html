353Nasaraṇaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca araṇo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā…  ekaṁ.
354Nasaraṇaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca araṇo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā…  tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Nahetuyā ekaṁ, naārammaṇe tīṇi, naadhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  nakamme ekaṁ, naāhāre naindriye najhāne namagge ekaṁ…pe…  novigate tīṇi.
355Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
356Naārammaṇapaccayā hetuyā tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
