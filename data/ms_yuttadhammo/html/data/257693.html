78 Saraṇaduka, Dassanahetuttika
Saraṇaduka, Dassanahetuttika
90.
247Saraṇaṁ dassanena pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo nadassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṁ, ārammaṇe ekaṁ…pe… sahajāte tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
248Saraṇaṁ bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasaraṇo nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṁ, ārammaṇe ekaṁ…pe… sahajāte tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
249Araṇaṁ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naaraṇo nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṁ…pe… avigate ekaṁ.
