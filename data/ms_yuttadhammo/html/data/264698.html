82.
224Sahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā— vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (1)
225Ahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā— ahetukaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ…pe… paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe… (yāva asaññasattā), cakkhāyatanaṁ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṁ…pe… kāyāyatanaṁ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṁ, vatthuṁ paccayā ahetukā khandhā moho ca. (1)
226Sahetukañca ahetukañca dhammaṁ paccayā ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā— vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca vatthuñca paccayā vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) (1)
