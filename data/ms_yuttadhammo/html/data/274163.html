2.2.6 Dukkhadhammasutta
Rādhasaṁyutta
Dutiyamāravagga
Dukkhadhammasutta
175.
858Sāvatthinidānaṁ. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca— “‘dukkhadhammo, dukkhadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, dukkhadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṁ kho, rādha, dukkhadhammo, vedanā dukkhadhammo, saññā dukkhadhammo, saṅkhārā dukkhadhammo, viññāṇaṁ dukkhadhammo. Evaṁ passaṁ…pe… nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
859Chaṭṭhaṁ.
