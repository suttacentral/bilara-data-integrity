31 Kenaciviññeyyaduka, Kusalattika
Kenaciviññeyyaduka, Kusalattika
40.
115Kenaci viññeyyaṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakenaci viññeyyo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…. Hetuyā nava…pe… avigate nava.
116Kenaci viññeyyaṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakenaci viññeyyo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…. Hetuyā nava…pe… avigate nava.
117Kenaci viññeyyaṁ abyākataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā nakenaci viññeyyo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…. Hetuyā nava…pe… avigate nava.
118Cūḷantaradukaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
