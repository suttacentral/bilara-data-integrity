2.5.4 Duppaññasutta
Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta
Cakkavattivagga
Duppaññasutta
225.
538Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe…  ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca—  “‘duppañño eḷamūgo, duppañño eḷamūgo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, ‘duppañño eḷamūgo’ti vuccatī”ti? “Sattannaṁ kho, bhikkhu, bojjhaṅgānaṁ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā ‘duppañño eḷamūgo’ti vuccati. Katamesaṁ sattannaṁ? Satisambojjhaṅgassa…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgassa—  imesaṁ kho, bhikkhu, sattannaṁ bojjhaṅgānaṁ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā ‘duppañño eḷamūgo’ti vuccatī”ti.
539Catutthaṁ.
