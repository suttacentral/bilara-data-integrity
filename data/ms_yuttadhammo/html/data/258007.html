29 Sekkhattika, Kusalattika
Sekkhattika, Kusalattika
38.
121Nasekkhaṁ nakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā sekkho kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha…pe… avigate cha.
122Nasekkhaṁ naakusalaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā nevasekkhanāsekkho akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe… avigate tīṇi.
123Nasekkhaṁ naabyākataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nevasekkhanāsekkho abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe… avigate pañca.
