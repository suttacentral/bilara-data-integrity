3.2 Abhidhammabhājanīya
Dhātuvibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
183.
644Aṭṭhārasa dhātuyo— cakkhudhātu, rūpadhātu, cakkhuviññāṇadhātu, sotadhātu, saddadhātu, sotaviññāṇadhātu, ghānadhātu, gandhadhātu, ghānaviññāṇadhātu, jivhādhātu, rasadhātu, jivhāviññāṇadhātu, kāyadhātu, phoṭṭhabbadhātu, kāyaviññāṇadhātu, manodhātu, dhammadhātu, manoviññāṇadhātu.
184.
645Tattha katamā cakkhudhātu? Yaṁ cakkhu catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya pasādo…pe… suñño gāmopeso— ayaṁ vuccati “cakkhudhātu”. (1)
646Tattha katamā rūpadhātu? Yaṁ rūpaṁ catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya vaṇṇanibhā…pe… rūpadhātupesā— ayaṁ vuccati “rūpadhātu”. (2)
647Tattha katamā cakkhuviññāṇadhātu? Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjācakkhuviññāṇadhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “cakkhuviññāṇadhātu”. (3)
648Tattha katamā sotadhātu? Yaṁ sotaṁ catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya pasādo…pe… suñño gāmopeso— ayaṁ vuccati “sotadhātu”. (4)
649Tattha katamā saddadhātu? Yo saddo catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya anidassano sappaṭigho…pe… saddadhātupesā— ayaṁ vuccati “saddadhātu”. (5)
650Tattha katamā sotaviññāṇadhātu? Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjāsotaviññāṇadhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “sotaviññāṇadhātu”. (6)
651Tattha katamā ghānadhātu? Yaṁ ghānaṁ catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya pasādo…pe… suñño gāmopeso— ayaṁ vuccati “ghānadhātu”. (7)
652Tattha katamā gandhadhātu? Yo gandho catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya anidassano sappaṭigho…pe… gandhadhātupesā— ayaṁ vuccati “gandhadhātu”. (8)
653Tattha katamā ghānaviññāṇadhātu? Ghānañca paṭicca gandhe ca uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjāghānaviññāṇadhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “ghānaviññāṇadhātu”. (9)
654Tattha katamā jivhādhātu? Yā jivhā catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya pasādo…pe… suñño gāmopeso— ayaṁ vuccati “jivhādhātu”. (10)
655Tattha katamā rasadhātu? Yo raso catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya anidassano sappaṭigho…pe… rasadhātupesā— ayaṁ vuccati “rasadhātu”. (11)
656Tattha katamā jivhāviññāṇadhātu? Jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjājivhāviññāṇadhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “jivhāviññāṇadhātu”. (12)
657Tattha katamā kāyadhātu? Yo kāyo catunnaṁ mahābhūtānaṁ upādāya pasādo…pe… suñño gāmopeso— ayaṁ vuccati “kāyadhātu”. (13)
658Tattha katamā phoṭṭhabbadhātu? Pathavīdhātu…pe… phoṭṭhabbadhātupesā— ayaṁ vuccati “phoṭṭhabbadhātu”. (14)
659Tattha katamā kāyaviññāṇadhātu? Kāyañca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjākāyaviññāṇadhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “kāyaviññāṇadhātu”. (15)
660Tattha katamā manodhātu? Cakkhuviññāṇadhātuyā uppajjitvā niruddhasamanantarā uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjāmanodhātu; sotaviññāṇadhātuyā…pe… ghānaviññāṇadhātuyā…pe… jivhāviññāṇadhātuyā…pe… kāyaviññāṇadhātuyā uppajjitvā niruddhasamanantarā uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjāmanodhātu sabbadhammesu vā pana paṭhamasamannāhāro uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjāmanodhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “manodhātu”. (16)
661Tattha katamā dhammadhātu? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, yañca rūpaṁ anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu.
662Tattha katamo vedanākkhandho? Ekavidhena vedanākkhandho— phassasampayutto. Duvidhena vedanākkhandho— atthi sahetuko, atthi ahetuko. Tividhena vedanākkhandho— atthi kusalo, atthi akusalo, atthi abyākato…pe… evaṁ dasavidhena vedanākkhandho…pe… evaṁ bahuvidhena vedanākkhandho. Ayaṁ vuccati “vedanākkhandho”. (1)
663Tattha katamo saññākkhandho? Ekavidhena saññākkhandho— phassasampayutto. Duvidhena saññākkhandho— atthi sahetuko, atthi ahetuko. Tividhena saññākkhandho— atthi kusalo, atthi akusalo, atthi abyākato…pe… evaṁ dasavidhena saññākkhandho…pe… evaṁ bahuvidhena saññākkhandho. Ayaṁ vuccati “saññākkhandho”. (2)
664Tattha katamo saṅkhārakkhandho? Ekavidhena saṅkhārakkhandho— cittasampayutto. Duvidhena saṅkhārakkhandho— atthi hetu, atthi ahetu. Tividhena saṅkhārakkhandho— atthi kusalo, atthi akusalo, atthi abyākato…pe… evaṁ dasavidhena saṅkhārakkhandho…pe… evaṁ bahuvidhena saṅkhārakkhandho— ayaṁ vuccati “saṅkhārakkhandho”. (3)
665Tattha katamaṁ rūpaṁ anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṁ? Itthindriyaṁ…pe… kabaḷīkāro āhāro— idaṁ vuccati rūpaṁ “anidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammāyatanapariyāpannaṁ”. (4)
666Tattha katamā asaṅkhatā dhātu? Rāgakkhayo, dosakkhayo, mohakkhayo— ayaṁ vuccati “asaṅkhatā dhātu”. Ayaṁ vuccati “dhammadhātu”. (5--17)
667Tattha katamā manoviññāṇadhātu? Cakkhuviññāṇadhātuyā uppajjitvā niruddhasamanantarā uppajjati manodhātu, manodhātuyā uppajjitvā niruddhasamanantarā uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ…pe… tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu; sotaviññāṇadhātuyā…pe… ghānaviññāṇadhātuyā…pe… jivhāviññāṇadhātuyā…pe… kāyaviññāṇadhātuyā uppajjitvā niruddhasamanantarā uppajjati manodhātu, manodhātuyāpi uppajjitvā niruddhasamanantarā uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ…pe… tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati cittaṁ mano mānasaṁ hadayaṁ paṇḍaraṁ mano manāyatanaṁ manindriyaṁ viññāṇaṁ viññāṇakkhandho tajjāmanoviññāṇadhātu— ayaṁ vuccati “manoviññāṇadhātu”. (18)
668Abhidhammabhājanīyaṁ.
