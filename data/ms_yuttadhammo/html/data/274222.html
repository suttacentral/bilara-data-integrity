5.1.4 Samphassasutta
Uppādasaṁyutta
Uppādavagga
Samphassasutta
315.
1074Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, cakkhusamphassassa uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe…  yo manosamphassassa uppādo ṭhiti…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, cakkhusamphassassa nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe…  yo manosamphassassa nirodho…pe…  jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
1075Catutthaṁ.
