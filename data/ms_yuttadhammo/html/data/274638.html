2.4.7 Buddhisutta
Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta
Nīvaraṇavagga
Buddhisutta
218.
513“Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā buddhiyā aparihānāya saṁvattanti. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo. Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā buddhiyā aparihānāya saṁvattantī”ti.
514Sattamaṁ.
