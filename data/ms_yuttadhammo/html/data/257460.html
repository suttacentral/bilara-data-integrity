49 Kilesaduka
Kilesaduka
68.
210Nokilesaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nokilesaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nanokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Nokilesaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nokileso ca nanokileso ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)
211Nanokilesaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nanokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi.
212Nokilesañca nanokilesañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
213Hetuyā nava…pe… vipāke ekaṁ…pe… avigate nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
