12.2.2 Arūpāvacarakusala
Jhānavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Arūpāvacarakusala
626.
1880Idha bhikkhu yasmiṁ samaye arūpūpapattiyā maggaṁ bhāveti sabbaso ākiñcaññāyatanaṁ samatikkamma nevasaññānāsaññāyatanasaññāsahagataṁ sukhassa ca pahānā…pe…  catutthaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharati, tasmiṁ samaye duvaṅgikaṁ jhānaṁ hoti—  upekkhā, cittassekaggatā. Idaṁ vuccati “catutthaṁ jhānaṁ”. Avasesā dhammā jhānasampayuttā.
