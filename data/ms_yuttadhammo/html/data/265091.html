2040Nīvaraṇasampayutto dhammo nīvaraṇasampayuttassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā nīvaraṇasampayuttā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ nīvaraṇasampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo.
