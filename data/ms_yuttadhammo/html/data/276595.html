Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Pādukavagga
Sayanasikkhāpada
641.
2018Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sayanagatassa dhammaṁ desenti…pe… .
2019“Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṁ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (64:209)
2020Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca antamaso chamāyampi nipannassa sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṁ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
2021Anāpatti—  asañcicca…pe…  ādikammikassāti.
2022Catutthasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
