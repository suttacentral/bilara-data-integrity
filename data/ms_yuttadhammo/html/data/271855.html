1.44 Anāpucchāvaraṇavatthu
Mahākhandhaka
Anāpucchāvaraṇavatthu
108.
479Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū upajjhāye anāpucchā sāmaṇerānaṁ āvaraṇaṁ karonti. Upajjhāyā gavesanti— “kathaṁ nu kho amhākaṁ sāmaṇerā na dissantī”ti. Bhikkhū evamāhaṁsu— “chabbaggiyehi, āvuso, bhikkhūhi āvaraṇaṁ katan”ti. Upajjhāyā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū amhe anāpucchā amhākaṁ sāmaṇerānaṁ āvaraṇaṁ karissantī”ti. Bhagavato etamatthaṁ ārocesuṁ. “Na, bhikkhave, upajjhāye anāpucchā āvaraṇaṁ kātabbaṁ. Yo kareyya, āpatti dukkaṭassā”ti.
