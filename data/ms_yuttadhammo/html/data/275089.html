12.11.30 Pettidevapettivisayasutta
Saccasaṁyutta
Pañcagatipeyyālavagga
Pettidevapettivisayasutta
1201.
2545… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye pettivisayā cutā devesu paccājāyanti; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye pettivisayā cutā pettivisaye paccājāyanti. Taṁ kissa hetu? Adiṭṭhattā, bhikkhave, catunnaṁ ariyasaccānaṁ. Katamesaṁ catunnaṁ? Dukkhassa ariyasaccassa, dukkhasamudayassa ariyasaccassa, dukkhanirodhassa ariyasaccassa, dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāya ariyasaccassa.
2546Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṁ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo, ‘ayaṁ dukkhasamudayo’ti yogo karaṇīyo, ‘ayaṁ dukkhanirodho’ti yogo karaṇīyo, ‘ayaṁ dukkhanirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.
2547Idamavoca bhagavā. Attamanā te bhikkhū bhagavato bhāsitaṁ abhinandunti.
2548Tiṁsatimaṁ.
2549Pañcagatipeyyālavaggo ekādasamo.
2550Tassuddānaṁ
2551
Manussato cutā chāpi,
devā cutā nirayato;
Tiracchānapettivisayā,
tiṁsamatto gativaggoti.
2552Saccasaṁyuttaṁ dvādasamaṁ.
2553Mahāvaggo pañcamo.
2554Tassuddānaṁ
2555
Maggabojjhaṅgaṁ satiyā,
indriyaṁ sammappadhānaṁ;
Baliddhipādānuruddhā,
jhānānāpānasaṁyutaṁ;
Sotāpatti saccañcāti,
mahāvaggoti vuccatīti.
2556Mahāvaggasaṁyuttapāḷi niṭṭhitā.
2557Saṁyuttanikāyo samatto.
