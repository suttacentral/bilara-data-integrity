1.1.1.2 Ajjhattadukkhasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Aniccavagga
Ajjhattadukkhasutta
2.
5“Cakkhuṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ. Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā; yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ. Sotaṁ dukkhaṁ…pe…  ghānaṁ dukkhaṁ…  jivhā dukkhā…  kāyo dukkho…  mano dukkho. Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā; yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ. Evaṁ passaṁ…pe…  nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
6Dutiyaṁ.
