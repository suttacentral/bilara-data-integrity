2.4.16.10 Nandasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Dhammikavagga
Nandasikkhāpada
3526Uddiṭṭhā kho, ayyāyo, chasaṭṭhisatā pācittiyā dhammā. Tatthāyyāyo pucchāmi— “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Dutiyampi pucchāmi— “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Tatiyampi pucchāmi— “kaccittha parisuddhā”? Parisuddhetthāyyāyo, tasmā tuṇhī, evametaṁ dhārayāmīti.
3527Khuddakaṁ samattaṁ.
Bhikkhunivibhaṅge pācittiyakaṇḍaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ. ( )
