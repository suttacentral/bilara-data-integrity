1127Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati naāhārapaccayā—  bāhiraṁ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṁ…  asaññasattānaṁ…pe…  naindriyapaccayā—  bāhiraṁ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṁ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṁ, asaññasattānaṁ…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca rūpajīvitindriyaṁ…  najhānapaccayā—  pañcaviññāṇaṁ…pe…  (mahābhūtā kātabbā) namaggapaccayā—  ahetukaṁ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ…pe…  asaññasattānaṁ…pe…  nasampayuttapaccayā.
