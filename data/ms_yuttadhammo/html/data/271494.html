2.4.2.10.11 Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattiduka
1545.
2256Katame dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho—  ime dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
1546.
2257Katame dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino? Cittañca, rūpañca, nibbānañca—  ime dhammā no cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino.
