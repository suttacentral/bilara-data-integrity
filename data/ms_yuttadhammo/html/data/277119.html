1.1.3.1 Sabbasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Sabbavagga
Sabbasutta
23.
63Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Sabbaṁ vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi. Taṁ suṇātha. Kiñca, bhikkhave, sabbaṁ? Cakkhuñceva rūpā ca, sotañca saddā ca, ghānañca gandhā ca, jivhā ca rasā ca, kāyo ca phoṭṭhabbā ca, mano ca dhammā ca—  idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sabbaṁ. Yo, bhikkhave, evaṁ vadeyya—  ‘ahametaṁ sabbaṁ paccakkhāya aññaṁ sabbaṁ paññāpessāmī’ti, tassa vācāvatthukamevassa; puṭṭho ca na sampāyeyya, uttariñca vighātaṁ āpajjeyya. Taṁ kissa hetu? Yathā taṁ, bhikkhave, avisayasmin”ti.
64Paṭhamaṁ.
