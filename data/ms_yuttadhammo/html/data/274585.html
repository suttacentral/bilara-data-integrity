1.13.5 Dukkhatāsutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Esanāvagga
Dukkhatāsutta
165.
318“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, dukkhatā. Katamā tisso? Dukkhadukkhatā, saṅkhāradukkhatā, vipariṇāmadukkhatā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso dukkhatā. Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṁ dukkhatānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe…  ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
319Pañcamaṁ.
