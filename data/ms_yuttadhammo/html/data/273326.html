1.2.2 Dhammakammadvādasaka
Kammakkhandhaka
Niyassakamma
Dhammakammadvādasaka
14.
80Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. Sammukhā kataṁ hoti, paṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, paṭiññāya kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. (1)
81Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. Āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, desanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, adesitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. (2)
82Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. Codetvā kataṁ hoti, sāretvā kataṁ hoti, āpattiṁ āropetvā kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. (3)
83Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  sammukhā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (4)
84Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  paṭipucchā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (5)
85Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  paṭiññāya kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (6)
86Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (7)
87Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  desanāgāminiyā āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (8)
88Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  adesitāya āpattiyā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (9)
89Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  codetvā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (10)
90Aparehipi, bhikkhave…pe…  sāretvā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave…pe… . (11)
91Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. Āpattiṁ āropetvā kataṁ hoti, dhammena kataṁ hoti, samaggena kataṁ hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ niyassakammaṁ dhammakammañca hoti, vinayakammañca, suvūpasantañca. (12)
92Dhammakammadvādasakaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
