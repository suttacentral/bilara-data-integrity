2271Sammattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sammattaniyato dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā— sammattaniyataṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe…. (1)
2272Aniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca aniyato dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā— aniyataṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe… paṭisandhikkhaṇe aniyataṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe… vatthuṁ paṭicca khandhā. (Sabbe paccayā iminā kāraṇena vitthāretabbā. Saṁkhittaṁ.)
