2.7.2 Dhammānupassīsutta
Dutiyapaṇṇāsaka
Sāmaññavagga
Dhammānupassīsutta
118.
768“Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo ajjhattaṁ kāye…pe…  bahiddhā kāye…pe…  ajjhattabahiddhā kāye…pe…  ajjhattaṁ vedanāsu…pe…  bahiddhā vedanāsu…pe…  ajjhattabahiddhā vedanāsu…pe…  ajjhattaṁ citte…pe…  bahiddhā citte…pe…  ajjhattabahiddhā citte…pe…  ajjhattaṁ dhammesu…pe…  bahiddhā dhammesu…pe…  ajjhattabahiddhā dhammesu dhammānupassī viharituṁ. Katame cha? Kammārāmataṁ, bhassārāmataṁ, niddārāmataṁ, saṅgaṇikārāmataṁ, indriyesu aguttadvārataṁ, bhojane amattaññutaṁ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme pahāya bhabbo ajjhattabahiddhā dhammesu dhammānupassī viharitun”ti.
769Dutiyaṁ.
