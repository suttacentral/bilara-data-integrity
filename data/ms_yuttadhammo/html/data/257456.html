45 Ajjhattikaduka
Ajjhattikaduka
64.
196Naajjhattikaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naajjhattiko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.
197Nabāhiraṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nabāhiro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṁ paṭicca ajjhattikaṁ kaṭattārūpaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
198Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe pañca…pe…  avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
