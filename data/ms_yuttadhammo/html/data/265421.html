3418Upādinnaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā upādinno dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  upādinnaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paccayā tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  vatthuṁ paccayā khandhā, cakkhāyatanaṁ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṁ…pe…  kāyāyatanaṁ…pe…  vatthuṁ paccayā upādinnā khandhā. (1)
3419Upādinnaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anupādinno dhammo uppajjati ārammaṇapaccayā—  vatthuṁ paccayā anupādinnā khandhā. (2)
