Micchattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Micchattaniyataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nasammattaniyato ca naaniyato ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā.
