23.6 Aṅkolapupphiyattheraapadāna
Ālambaṇadāyakavagga
Aṅkolapupphiyattheraapadāna
29.
2937
“Nārado iti me nāmaṁ,
kassapo iti maṁ vidū;
Addasaṁ samaṇānaggaṁ,
vipassiṁ devasakkataṁ.
30.
2938
Anubyañjanadharaṁ buddhaṁ,
āhutīnaṁ paṭiggahaṁ;
Aṅkolapupphaṁ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṁ.
31.
2939
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṁ pupphamabhipūjayiṁ;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
32.
2940
Catusattatito kappe,
romaso nāma khattiyo;
Āmukkamālābharaṇo,
sayoggabalavāhano.
33.
2941
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe…
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
2942Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā aṅkolapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2943Aṅkolapupphiyattherassāpadānaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.
