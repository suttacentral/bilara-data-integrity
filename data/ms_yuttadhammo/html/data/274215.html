4.1.7 Rūpasañcetanāsutta
Okkantasaṁyutta
Cakkhuvagga
Rūpasañcetanāsutta
308.
1055Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Rūpasañcetanā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddasañcetanā…  gandhasañcetanā…  rasasañcetanā…  phoṭṭhabbasañcetanā…  dhammasañcetanā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī…pe…  sambodhiparāyano’”ti.
1056Sattamaṁ.
