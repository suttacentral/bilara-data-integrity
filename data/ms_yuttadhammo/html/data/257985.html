7 Kusalattika, Dassanattika
Kusalattika, Dassanattika
11.
42Nakusalaṁ nadassanena pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā akusalo dassanena pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
43Nakusalaṁ nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā akusalo bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
44Nakusalaṁ nanevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
