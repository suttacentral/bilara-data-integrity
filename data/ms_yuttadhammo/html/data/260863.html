56.
2592Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā ekavīsa, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte sattarasa, aññamaññe sattarasa, nissaye sattarasa, upanissaye ekavīsa, āsevane nava, kamme sattarasa, āhāre sattarasa, indriye jhāne magge sampayutte sattarasa, atthiyā sattarasa, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate sattarasa. (Evaṁ gaṇetabbaṁ.)
