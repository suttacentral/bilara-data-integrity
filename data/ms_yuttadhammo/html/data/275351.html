4.1.11--15
Peyyāla
Kodhapeyyāla
183.
278“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato dukkhaṁ viharati. Katamehi dvīhi? Kodhena ca upanāhena ca…  makkhena ca paḷāsena ca…  issāya ca macchariyena ca…  māyāya ca sāṭheyyena ca…  ahirikena ca anottappena ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato dukkhaṁ viharati”.
