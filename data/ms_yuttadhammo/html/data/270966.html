14.2.2.10 Mahantaraduka
Sikkhāpadavibhaṅga
Pañhāpucchaka
Duka
Mahantaraduka
2114Sārammaṇā, no cittā, cetasikā, cittasampayuttā, cittasaṁsaṭṭhā, cittasamuṭṭhānā, cittasahabhuno, cittānuparivattino, cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānā, cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānasahabhuno, cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānānuparivattino, bāhirā, no upādā, anupādinnā.
