3.1.1 Sārajjasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Phāsuvihāravagga
Sārajjasutta
101.
608“Pañcime, bhikkhave, sekhavesārajjakaraṇā dhammā. Katame pañca? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu saddho hoti, sīlavā hoti, bahussuto hoti, āraddhavīriyo hoti, paññavā hoti.
609Yaṁ, bhikkhave, assaddhassa sārajjaṁ hoti, saddhassa taṁ sārajjaṁ na hoti. Tasmāyaṁ dhammo sekhavesārajjakaraṇo.
610Yaṁ, bhikkhave, dussīlassa sārajjaṁ hoti, sīlavato taṁ sārajjaṁ na hoti. Tasmāyaṁ dhammo sekhavesārajjakaraṇo.
611Yaṁ, bhikkhave, appassutassa sārajjaṁ hoti, bahussutassa taṁ sārajjaṁ na hoti. Tasmāyaṁ dhammo sekhavesārajjakaraṇo.
612Yaṁ, bhikkhave, kusītassa sārajjaṁ hoti, āraddhavīriyassa taṁ sārajjaṁ na hoti. Tasmāyaṁ dhammo sekhavesārajjakaraṇo.
613Yaṁ, bhikkhave, duppaññassa sārajjaṁ hoti, paññavato taṁ sārajjaṁ na hoti. Tasmāyaṁ dhammo sekhavesārajjakaraṇo. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca sekhavesārajjakaraṇā dhammā”ti.
614Paṭhamaṁ.
