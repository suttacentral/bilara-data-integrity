4.1.8 Rūpataṇhāsutta
Okkantasaṁyutta
Cakkhuvagga
Rūpataṇhāsutta
309.
1057Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Rūpataṇhā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddataṇhā…  gandhataṇhā…  rasataṇhā…  phoṭṭhabbataṇhā…  dhammataṇhā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī…pe…  sambodhiparāyano’”ti.
1058Aṭṭhamaṁ.
