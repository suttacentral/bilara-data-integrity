40.1.8.17 Thālakānisaṁsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṁsakathā
Thālakānisaṁsa
136.
4433
Thālake sugate datvā,
saṁghe gaṇavaruttame;
Tīṇānisaṁse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
137.
4434
Soṇṇamaye maṇimaye,
athopi phalikāmaye;
Lohitaṅgamaye ceva,
labhāmi thālake ahaṁ.
138.
4435
Asatthake phalamaye,
atho pokkharapattake;
Madhupānakasaṅkhe ca,
labhāmi thālake ahaṁ.
139.
4436
Vatte guṇe paṭipatti,
ācārakiriyāsu ca;
Ime guṇe paṭilabhe,
tassa nissandato ahaṁ.
