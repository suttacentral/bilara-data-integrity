1.3.1.1.1 Kusalapada
Nikkhepavāra
Pucchāvāra
Paccayānuloma
Ekamūlaka
Kusalapada
26.
60Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kusalo dhammo uppajjeyya hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca akusalo dhammo uppajjeyya hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato dhammo uppajjeyya hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṁ hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca akusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṁ hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kusalo ca akusalo ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṁ hetupaccayā. Siyā kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca kusalo ca akusalo ca abyākato ca dhammā uppajjeyyuṁ hetupaccayā.
