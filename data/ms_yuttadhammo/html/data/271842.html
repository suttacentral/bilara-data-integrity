1.31 Kasāhatavatthu
Mahākhandhaka
Kasāhatavatthu
94.
432Tena kho pana samayena aññataro puriso kasāhato katadaṇḍakammo bhikkhūsu pabbajito hoti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma samaṇā sakyaputtiyā kasāhataṁ katadaṇḍakammaṁ pabbājessantī”ti. Bhagavato etamatthaṁ ārocesuṁ. “Na, bhikkhave, kasāhato katadaṇḍakammo pabbājetabbo. Yo pabbājeyya, āpatti dukkaṭassā”ti.
