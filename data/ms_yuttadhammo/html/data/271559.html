1.1.1.5.1 Paccanīkānuloma
Mahāpaṇṇāsaka
Paṭhamavagga
Puggalakathā
Okāsasaccikaṭṭha
Paccanīkānuloma
14.
52× Puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Āmantā. Sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenāti? Na hevaṁ vattabbe.
53Ājānāhi niggahaṁ. Hañci puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena, tena vata re vattabbe—  “sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṁ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā.
54No ce pana vattabbe—  “sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā”ti. Yaṁ tattha vadesi—  “vattabbe kho—  ‘puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthena’, no ca vattabbe—  ‘sabbattha puggalo nupalabbhati saccikaṭṭhaparamatthenā’”ti micchā…pe… .
55Chaṭṭho niggaho.
