3.5.9 Anussaritabbasutta
Tatiyapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyavagga
Anussaritabbasutta
153.
1145“Anussaritabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na anussaritabbañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, na anussaritabbo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na anussaritabbo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāvimutti— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo”ti.
1146Navamaṁ.
