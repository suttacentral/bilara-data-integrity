2.3.2.10.5 Cittasaṁsaṭṭhaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittasaṁsaṭṭhaduka
1199.
1895Katame dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho— ime dhammā cittasaṁsaṭṭhā.
1200.
1896Katame dhammā cittavisaṁsaṭṭhā? Sabbañca rūpaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu— ime dhammā cittavisaṁsaṭṭhā. Cittaṁ na vattabbaṁ— cittena saṁsaṭṭhantipi, cittena visaṁsaṭṭhantipi.
