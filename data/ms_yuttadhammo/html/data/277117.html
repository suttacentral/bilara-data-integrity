1.1.2.9 Paṭhamadukkhuppādasutta
Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Yamakavagga
Paṭhamadukkhuppādasutta
21.
54“Yo, bhikkhave, cakkhussa uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo sotassa…pe…  yo ghānassa…  yo jivhāya…  yo kāyassa…  yo manassa uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo.
55Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, cakkhussa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo. Yo sotassa…  yo ghānassa…  yo jivhāya…  yo kāyassa…  yo manassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
56Navamaṁ.
