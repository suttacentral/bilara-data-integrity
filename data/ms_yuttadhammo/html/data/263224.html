2564Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggārammaṇassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo… indriyapaccayena paccayo… jhānapaccayena paccayo… maggapaccayena paccayo… sampayuttapaccayena paccayo… atthipaccayena paccayo (ime satta paccayā sattarasa pañhā hetusadisā)… natthipaccayena paccayo… vigatapaccayena paccayo (anantarasadisā)… avigatapaccayena paccayo.
