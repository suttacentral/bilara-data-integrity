9.2.1.5 Satipaṭṭhāna
Asaṅkhatasaṁyutta
Dutiyavagga
Asaṅkhatasutta
Satipaṭṭhāna
1700Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu vedanāsu vedanānupassī viharati…pe…  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu citte cittānupassī viharati…pe…  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati…pe…  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe… . (1.9--12)
