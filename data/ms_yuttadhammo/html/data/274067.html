6.4.2 Kusalamūlasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṁyutta
Catutthavagga
Kusalamūlasutta
181.
1086Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtassa pariyādiṇṇacittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa kusalamūlaṁ samucchedamagamā. Evaṁ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe…  sikkhitabban”ti.
1087Dutiyaṁ.
