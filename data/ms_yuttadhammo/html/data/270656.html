673Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṁ samaye rūpūpapattiyā maggaṁ bhāveti ajjhattaṁ arūpasaññī bahiddhā rūpāni passati appamāṇāni, tāni abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmīti vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṁ dandhābhiññaṁ parittaṁ appamāṇārammaṇaṁ, tasmiṁ samaye phasso hoti…pe…  avikkhepo hoti…pe…  ime dhammā kusalā.
