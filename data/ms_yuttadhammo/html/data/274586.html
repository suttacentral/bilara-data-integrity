1.13.6 Khilasutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Esanāvagga
Khilasutta
166.
320“Tayome, bhikkhave, khilā. Katame tayo? Rāgo khilo, doso khilo, moho khilo— ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo khilā. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṁ khilānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya…pe… ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.
321Chaṭṭhaṁ.
