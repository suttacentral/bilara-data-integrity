302.
1004Kusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa avigatapaccayena paccayo— kusalo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṁ khandhānaṁ avigatapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa avigatapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ avigatapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
1005(Yathā atthipaccayaṁ, evaṁ vitthāretabbaṁ.)
1006Pañhāvārassa vibhaṅgo.
