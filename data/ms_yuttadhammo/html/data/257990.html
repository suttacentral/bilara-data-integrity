12 Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
Kusalattika, Parittārammaṇattika
16.
57Nakusalaṁ naparittārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca abyākato parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
58Nakusalaṁ namahaggatārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā kusalo mahaggatārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
59Nakusalaṁ naappamāṇārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā kusalo appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
