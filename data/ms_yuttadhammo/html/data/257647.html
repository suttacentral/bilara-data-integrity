32 Āsavaduka, Kusalattika
Āsavaduka, Kusalattika
41.
119Noāsavaṁ kusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṁ…pe…  avigate ekaṁ.
120Āsavaṁ akusalaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
121Noāsavaṁ abyākataṁ dhammaṁ paccayā nanoāsavo naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā… . Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
