128.
226Hetu vipākadhammadhammo hetussa vipākadhammadhammassa hetupaccayena paccayo… tīṇi.
129.
227Hetu vipākadhammadhammo hetussa vipākadhammadhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… nava.
130.
228Hetu vipākadhammadhammo hetussa vipākadhammadhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati… nava.
131.
229Hetu vipākadhammadhammo hetussa vipākadhammadhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo…pe… upanissayapaccayena paccayo— ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo… nava… āsevanapaccayena paccayo… nava.

231Nahetu vipākadhammadhammo nahetussa vipākadhammadhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
133.
232Hetuyā tīṇi, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare nava, samanantare nava, sahajāte nava, aññamaññe nava, nissaye nava, upanissaye nava, āsevane nava, kamme tīṇi, āhāre tīṇi, indriye nava, jhāne tīṇi, magge nava, sampayutte nava, atthiyā nava, natthiyā nava, vigate nava, avigate nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ, anulomaṁ.)
