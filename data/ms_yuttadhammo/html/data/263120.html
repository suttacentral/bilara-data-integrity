49.
2012Parittaṁ dhammaṁ saṁsaṭṭho paritto dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  paritte khandhe saṁsaṭṭhā parittā cetanā. (1)
2013Mahaggataṁ dhammaṁ saṁsaṭṭho mahaggato dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe saṁsaṭṭhā mahaggatā cetanā. (1)
2014Appamāṇaṁ dhammaṁ saṁsaṭṭho appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  kusale appamāṇe khandhe saṁsaṭṭhā appamāṇā cetanā. (1)
