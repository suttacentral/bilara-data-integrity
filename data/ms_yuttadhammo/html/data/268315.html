4.4.9 Anussaritabbasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyamaggavagga
Anussaritabbasutta
197.
1379“Anussaritabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi nānussaritabbañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, nānussaritabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, nānussaritabbo dhammo.
1380Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo”ti.
1381Navamaṁ.
