Sandhitattheraapadāna

2858

saṁvirūḷhamhi pādape;

alabhiṁhaṁ patissato.

2859

yaṁ saññamalabhiṁ tadā;
Tassā saññāya vāhasā,
patto me āsavakkhayo.

2860

dhaniṭṭho nāma khattiyo;

cakkavattī mahabbalo.
