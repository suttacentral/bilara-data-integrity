34.
3012Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā anidassanaappaṭighā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ anidassanaappaṭighānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṁ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṁ vodānassa…  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṁ maggassa…  maggo phalassa…  phalaṁ phalassa…  anulomaṁ phalasamāpattiyā…  nirodhā vuṭṭhahantassa nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṁ phalasamāpattiyā anantarapaccayena paccayo.
