11 Kusalattika, Parittattika
Kusalattika, Parittattika
23.
60Abyākataṁ parittaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo naparitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
61Kusalaṁ mahaggataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo namahaggato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
62Kusalaṁ appamāṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca nakusalo naappamāṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha…pe…  vipāke tīṇi…pe…  avigate cha.
