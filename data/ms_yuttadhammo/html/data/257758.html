53 Sanidassanattika, Sanidassanaduka
Sanidassanattika, Sanidassanaduka
96.
237Sanidassanasappaṭigho nasanidassano dhammo nasanidassanasappaṭighassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi, upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.
238Anidassanasappaṭighaṁ anidassanaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca naanidassanasappaṭigho naanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe… avigate nava.
