38.
2396Sārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
2397Anārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  rūpajīvitindriyaṁ kaṭattārūpānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2398Anārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  cakkhundriyaṁ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṁ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
2399Sārammaṇo ca anārammaṇo ca dhammā sārammaṇassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  cakkhundriyañca cakkhuviññāṇañca cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṁ khandhānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo…pe…  kāyindriyañca kāyaviññāṇañca kāyaviññāṇasahagatānaṁ khandhānaṁ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
