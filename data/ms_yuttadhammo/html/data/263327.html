2876Ajjhattārammaṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā— ahetukaṁ ajjhattārammaṇaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe… ahetukapaṭisandhikkhaṇe ajjhattārammaṇaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe… dve khandhe…pe… vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe paṭicca vicikicchāsahagato uddhaccasahagato moho.
