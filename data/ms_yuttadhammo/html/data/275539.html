1.2.7 Paṭhamaagatisutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Caravagga
Paṭhamaagatisutta
17.
112“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, agatigamanāni. Katamāni cattāri? Chandāgatiṁ gacchati, dosāgatiṁ gacchati, mohāgatiṁ gacchati, bhayāgatiṁ gacchati— imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri agatigamanānīti.
113
Chandā dosā bhayā mohā,
yo dhammaṁ ativattati;
Nihīyati tassa yaso,
kāḷapakkheva candimā”ti.
114Sattamaṁ.
