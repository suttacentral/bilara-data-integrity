49.
1912Sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo—  sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto eko khandho dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sahajātapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā ekassa khandhassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto eko khandho dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sahajātapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā ekassa khandhassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
50.
1913Dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo—  dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto eko khandho dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sahajātapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā ekassa khandhassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (Dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttapaṭisandhi na labbhati.) (1)

Dve khandhā ekassa khandhassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto eko khandho dvinnaṁ khandhānaṁ sahajātapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā ekassa khandhassa sahajātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
