2.3.1.21 Ajjhattārammaṇattika
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Tikanikkhepa
Ajjhattārammaṇattika
1053.
1718Katame dhammā ajjhattārammaṇā? Ajjhatte dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā— ime dhammā ajjhattārammaṇā.
1054.
1719Katame dhammā bahiddhārammaṇā? Bahiddhā dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā— ime dhammā bahiddhārammaṇā.
1055.
1720Katame dhammā ajjhattabahiddhārammaṇā? Ajjhattabahiddhā dhamme ārabbha ye uppajjanti cittacetasikā dhammā— ime dhammā ajjhattabahiddhārammaṇā.
