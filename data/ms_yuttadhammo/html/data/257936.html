49 Āsavasāsavadukādi, Sanidassanattika
Āsavasāsavadukādi, Sanidassanattika
97.
257Naāsavañceva naanāsavañca nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.
258Naanāsavañceva nano ca āsavaṁ nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… vipāke ekaṁ…pe… avigate tīṇi.
259(Naāsavañceva naanāsavaṁ naanidassanasappaṭighamūlepi purimanayena tīṇiyeva.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe… aññamaññe ekaṁ…pe… vipāke ekaṁ…pe… avigate tīṇi.
260Naāsavañceva naanāsavañca naanidassanaappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sāsavo ceva no ca āsavo anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekaṁ, adhipatiyā ekaṁ…pe… avigate ekaṁ.
261(Āsavaāsavasampayuttaduke pañhā na labbhati.)
