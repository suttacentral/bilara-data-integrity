2.3.5 Aparihāniyasutta
Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta
Udāyivagga
Aparihāniyasutta
206.
474“Satta vo, bhikkhave, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi; taṁ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā? Yadidaṁ—  satta bojjhaṅgā. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe…  upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā”ti.
475Pañcamaṁ.
