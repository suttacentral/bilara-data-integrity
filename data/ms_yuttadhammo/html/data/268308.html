4.4.2 Kaṇhamaggasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Ariyamaggavagga
Kaṇhamaggasutta
190.
1358“Kaṇhamaggañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi sukkamaggañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe… katamo ca, bhikkhave, kaṇho maggo? Pāṇātipāto…pe… micchādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, kaṇho maggo.
1359Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukko maggo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe… sammādiṭṭhi— ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukko maggo”ti.
1360Dutiyaṁ.
