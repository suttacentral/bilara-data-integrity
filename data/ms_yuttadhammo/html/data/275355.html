Peyyāla
Kodhapeyyāla
187.
282“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye. Katamehi dvīhi? Kodhena ca upanāhena ca… makkhena ca paḷāsena ca… issāya ca macchariyena ca… māyāya ca sāṭheyyena ca… ahirikena ca anottappena ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye”.
