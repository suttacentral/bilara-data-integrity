6.2.10.4 Lokuttarakusalacitta
Paṭiccasamuppādavibhaṅga
Abhidhammabhājanīya
Kusalaniddesa
Lokuttarakusalacitta
304.
1219Katame dhammā kusalā? Yasmiṁ samaye lokuttaraṁ jhānaṁ bhāveti niyyānikaṁ apacayagāmiṁ diṭṭhigatānaṁ pahānāya paṭhamāya bhūmiyā pattiyā vivicceva kāmehi…pe…  paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharati dukkhapaṭipadaṁ dandhābhiññaṁ, tasmiṁ samaye kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro, saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṁ, viññāṇapaccayā nāmaṁ, nāmapaccayā chaṭṭhāyatanaṁ, chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso, phassapaccayā vedanā, vedanāpaccayā pasādo, pasādapaccayā adhimokkho, adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo, bhavapaccayā jāti, jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ. Evametesaṁ dhammānaṁ samudayo hoti.
305.
1220Tattha katame kusalamūlā? Alobho, adoso, amoho.
1221Tattha katamo alobho…pe…  adoso…pe…  amoho? Yā paññā pajānanā…pe…  amoho dhammavicayo sammādiṭṭhi dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo maggaṅgaṁ maggapariyāpannaṁ—  ayaṁ vuccati “amoho”. Ime vuccanti “kusalamūlā”.
1222Tattha katamo kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro? Yā cetanā sañcetanā sañcetayitattaṁ—  ayaṁ vuccati “kusalamūlapaccayā saṅkhāro” …pe…  ayaṁ vuccati “chaṭṭhāyatanapaccayā phasso”.
1223Tattha katamā phassapaccayā vedanā? Yaṁ cetasikaṁ sātaṁ cetasikaṁ sukhaṁ cetosamphassajaṁ sātaṁ sukhaṁ vedayitaṁ cetosamphassajā sātā sukhā vedanā—  ayaṁ vuccati “phassapaccayā vedanā”.
1224Tattha katamo vedanāpaccayā pasādo? Yā saddhā saddahanā okappanā abhippasādo—  ayaṁ vuccati “vedanāpaccayā pasādo”.
1225Tattha katamo pasādapaccayā adhimokkho? Yo cittassa adhimokkho adhimuccanā tadadhimuttatā—  ayaṁ vuccati “pasādapaccayā adhimokkho”.
1226Tattha katamo adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo? Ṭhapetvā adhimokkhaṁ, vedanākkhandho saññākkhandho saṅkhārakkhandho viññāṇakkhandho—  ayaṁ vuccati “adhimokkhapaccayā bhavo” …pe…  ayaṁ vuccati “jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ”.
1227Evametesaṁ dhammānaṁ samudayo hotīti. Evametesaṁ dhammānaṁ saṅgati hoti, samāgamo hoti, samodhānaṁ hoti, pātubhāvo hoti. Tena vuccati “evametesaṁ dhammānaṁ samudayo hotī”ti.
1228Kusalaniddeso.
