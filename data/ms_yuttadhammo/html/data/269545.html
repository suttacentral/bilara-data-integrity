1.4.4 Catutthasuttantaniddesa
Mahāvagga
Indriyakathā
Catutthasuttantaniddesa
198.
1073Purimanidānaṁ. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṁ, vīriyindriyaṁ, satindriyaṁ, samādhindriyaṁ, paññindriyaṁ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni. Imāni pañcindriyāni katihākārehi kenaṭṭhena daṭṭhabbāni? Imāni pañcindriyāni chahākārehi tenaṭṭhena daṭṭhabbāni—  ādhipateyyaṭṭhena ādivisodhanaṭṭhena adhimattaṭṭhena, adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena, pariyādānaṭṭhena, patiṭṭhāpakaṭṭhena”.
