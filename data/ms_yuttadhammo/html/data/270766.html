2.3.3.12 Sassatadiṭṭhiduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Suttantikadukanikkhepa
Sassatadiṭṭhiduka
1322.
2027Tattha katamā sassatadiṭṭhi? Sassato attā ca loko cāti, yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṁ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṁ vuccati sassatadiṭṭhi.
1323.
2028Tattha katamā ucchedadiṭṭhi? Ucchijjissati attā ca loko cāti, yā evarūpā diṭṭhi diṭṭhigataṁ…pe…  vipariyāsaggāho—  ayaṁ vuccati ucchedadiṭṭhi.
