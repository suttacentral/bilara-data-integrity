2.4.2.12.6 Kilesasaṁkiliṭṭhaduka
Niddesa
Aṭṭhakathākaṇḍa
Dukaatthuddhāra
Kilesagocchaka
Kilesasaṁkiliṭṭhaduka
1575.
2286Katame dhammā kilesā ceva saṁkiliṭṭhā ca? Teva kilesā kilesā ceva saṁkiliṭṭhā ca.
1576.
2287Katame dhammā saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā? Ṭhapetvā kilese avasesaṁ akusalaṁ—  ime dhammā saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā. Asaṁkiliṭṭhā dhammā na vattabbā—  kilesā ceva saṁkiliṭṭhā cātipi, saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesātipi.
