106.
180Hetu adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo hetussa adukkhamasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo… sahajātapaccayena paccayo… upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
107.
181Nahetuyā nava, naārammaṇe nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ, paccanīyaṁ.)
182Hetupaccayā naārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ, anulomapaccanīyaṁ.)
183Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe nava. (Saṁkhittaṁ, paccanīyānulomaṁ.)
184(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvārassa anulomampi paccanīyampi anulomapaccanīyampi paccanīyānulomampi gaṇitaṁ, evaṁ gaṇetabbaṁ.)
185Hetudukavedanāttikaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
