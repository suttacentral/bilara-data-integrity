3.1.16 Nahotitathāgatosutta
Diṭṭhisaṁyutta
Sotāpattivagga
Nahotitathāgatosutta
221.
967Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati— ‘na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti? “Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā…pe… niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.
968Soḷasamaṁ.
