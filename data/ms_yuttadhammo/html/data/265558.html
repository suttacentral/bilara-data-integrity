91.
4041Saṁkiliṭṭho ceva no ca kileso dhammo saṁkiliṭṭhassa ceva no ca kilesassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo— saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā cetanā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
4042Saṁkiliṭṭho ceva no ca kileso dhammo kilesassa ceva saṁkiliṭṭhassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo— saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā cetanā sampayuttakānaṁ kilesānaṁ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
4043Saṁkiliṭṭho ceva no ca kileso dhammo kilesassa ceva saṁkiliṭṭhassa ca saṁkiliṭṭhassa ceva no ca kilesassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo— saṁkiliṭṭhā ceva no ca kilesā cetanā sampayuttakānaṁ khandhānaṁ kilesānañca kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
