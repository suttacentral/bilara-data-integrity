4.1.2 Sukkapakkhanavaka
Samathakkhandhaka
Sammukhāvinaya
Sukkapakkhanavaka
188.
797Dhammavādī puggalo adhammavādiṁ puggalaṁ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhāhi, imaṁ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (1)
798Dhammavādī puggalo adhammavādī sambahule saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhatha, imaṁ rocethā’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (2)
799Dhammavādī puggalo adhammavādiṁ saṁghaṁ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhāhi, imaṁ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (3)
800Dhammavādī sambahulā adhammavādiṁ puggalaṁ saññāpenti nijjhāpenti pekkhenti anupekkhenti dassenti anudassenti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhāhi, imaṁ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (4)
801Dhammavādī sambahulā adhammavādī sambahule saññāpenti nijjhāpenti pekkhenti anupekkhenti dassenti anudassenti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhatha, imaṁ rocethā’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (5)
802Dhammavādī sambahulā adhammavādiṁ saṁghaṁ saññāpenti nijjhāpenti pekkhenti anupekkhenti dassenti anudassenti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhāhi, imaṁ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (6)
803Dhammavādī saṁgho adhammavādiṁ puggalaṁ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhāhi, imaṁ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (7)
804Dhammavādī saṁgho adhammavādī sambahule saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhatha, imaṁ rocethā’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayena. (8)
805Dhammavādī saṁgho adhammavādiṁ saṁghaṁ saññāpeti nijjhāpeti pekkheti anupekkheti dasseti anudasseti— ‘ayaṁ dhammo, ayaṁ vinayo, idaṁ satthusāsanaṁ, imaṁ gaṇhāhi, imaṁ rocehī’ti. Evañcetaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ vūpasammati, dhammena vūpasammati sammukhāvinayenā”ti. (9)
806Sukkapakkhanavakaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
