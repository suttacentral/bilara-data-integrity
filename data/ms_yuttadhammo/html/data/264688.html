Napacchājātādi
66.
179Sahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati napacchājātapaccayā…  naāsevanapaccayā…  nakammapaccayā—  sahetuke khandhe paṭicca sahetukā cetanā. (1)
180Ahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca ahetuko dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  ahetuke khandhe paṭicca ahetukā cetanā…  bāhiraṁ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṁ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṁ…pe… . (1)
181Ahetukaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagataṁ uddhaccasahagataṁ mohaṁ paṭicca sampayuttakā cetanā. (2)
182Sahetukañca ahetukañca dhammaṁ paṭicca sahetuko dhammo uppajjati nakammapaccayā—  vicikicchāsahagate uddhaccasahagate khandhe ca mohañca paṭicca sampayuttakā cetanā…  navipākapaccayā. (Paṭisandhi natthi.)
