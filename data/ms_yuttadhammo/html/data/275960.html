4.1.10 Duppaṭivinodayasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Saddhammavagga
Duppaṭivinodayasutta
160.
927“Pañcime, bhikkhave, uppannā duppaṭivinodayā. Katame pañca? Uppanno rāgo duppaṭivinodayo, uppanno doso duppaṭivinodayo, uppanno moho duppaṭivinodayo, uppannaṁ paṭibhānaṁ duppaṭivinodayaṁ, uppannaṁ gamikacittaṁ duppaṭivinodayaṁ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca uppannā duppaṭivinodayā”ti.
928Dasamaṁ.
929Saddhammavaggo paṭhamo.
930Tassuddānaṁ
931
Tayo sammattaniyāmā,
tayo saddhammasammosā;
Dukkathā ceva sārajjaṁ,
udāyidubbinodayāti.
