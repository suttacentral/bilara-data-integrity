8.2.14 Āsavakkhayasutta
Anuruddhasaṁyutta
Dutiyavagga
Āsavakkhayasutta
922.
1619“Imesañca panāhaṁ, āvuso, catunnaṁ satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā āsavānaṁ khayā anāsavaṁ cetovimuttiṁ paññāvimuttiṁ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṁ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharāmī”ti.
1620Cuddasamaṁ.
1621Dutiyo vaggo.
1622Tassuddānaṁ
1623
Mahābhiññaṁ iddhi dibbaṁ,
Cetopariyaṁ ṭhānaṁ kammaṁ;
Sabbatthadhātudhimutti,
Indriyaṁ jhānaṁ tisso vijjāti.
1624Anuruddhasaṁyuttaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.
