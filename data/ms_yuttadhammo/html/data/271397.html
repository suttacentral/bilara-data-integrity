2.3.2.10.2 Cittaduka
Niddesa
Nikkhepakaṇḍa
Dukanikkhepa
Mahantaraduka
Cittaduka
1193.
1889Katame dhammā cittā? Cakkhuviññāṇaṁ, sotaviññāṇaṁ, ghānaviññāṇaṁ, jivhāviññāṇaṁ, kāyaviññāṇaṁ, manodhātu, manoviññāṇadhātu—  ime dhammā cittā.
1194.
1890Katame dhammā no cittā? Vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, sabbañca rūpaṁ, asaṅkhatā ca dhātu—  ime dhammā no cittā.
