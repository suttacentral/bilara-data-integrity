5.1.8 Taṇhāsutta
Uppādasaṁyutta
Uppādavagga
Taṇhāsutta
319.
1083Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya uppādo ṭhiti…pe… jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo…pe… yo dhammataṇhāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya nirodho…pe… jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo…pe… yo dhammataṇhāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.
1084Aṭṭhamaṁ.
