Anidassanaappaṭighaṁ āsavañceva āsavasampayuttañca dhammaṁ paṭicca nasanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca naanidassanasappaṭigho naāsavo ceva naāsavavippayutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā.
