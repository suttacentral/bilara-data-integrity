2.1.6 Dutiyasīlasutta
Dukanipāta
Paṭhamavagga
Dutiyasīlasutta
33.
203Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ— 
204“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge. Katamehi dvīhi? Bhaddakena ca sīlena, bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge”ti. Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati— 
205
“Bhaddakena ca sīlena,
bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;
Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,
yo samannāgato naro;
Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,
saggaṁ so upapajjatī”ti.
206Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.
207Chaṭṭhaṁ.
