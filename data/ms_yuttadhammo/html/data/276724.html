2.4.9.5 Gandhavaṇṇakasikkhāpada
Bhikkhunivibhaṅga
Pācittiyakaṇḍa
Chattupāhanavagga
Gandhavaṇṇakasikkhāpada
1198.
3469Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.
3470Assosuṁ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṁ manussānaṁ ujjhāyantānaṁ khiyyantānaṁ vipācentānaṁ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā…pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissantī”ti…pe…  “saccaṁ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyantī”ti? “Saccaṁ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyissanti. Netaṁ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṁ vā pasādāya…pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṁ sikkhāpadaṁ uddisantu— 
1199.
3471“Yā pana bhikkhunī gandhavaṇṇakena nahāyeyya, pācittiyan”ti. (88:143)
1200.
3472Yā panāti yā yādisā…pe…  bhikkhunīti…pe…  ayaṁ imasmiṁ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
3473Gandho nāma yo koci gandho. Vaṇṇakaṁ nāma yaṁ kiñci vaṇṇakaṁ. Nahāyeyyāti nahāyati. Payoge dukkaṭaṁ, nahānapariyosāne āpatti pācittiyassa.
1201.
3474Anāpatti—  ābādhappaccayā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.
3475Pañcamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
