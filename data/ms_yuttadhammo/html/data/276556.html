1.7.3.5 Ukkuṭikasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Khambhakatavagga
Ukkuṭikasikkhāpada
600.
1805Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchanti…pe… .
1806“Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (25:170)
1807Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gantabbaṁ. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1808Anāpatti— asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.
1809Pañcamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
