6.
982Āsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  āsave paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (1)
983Noāsavaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  noāsave khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ; paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…  khandhe paṭicca vatthu, ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ…pe… . (Yāva asaññasattā.) (1)
984Āsavañca noāsavañca dhammaṁ paṭicca noāsavo dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā—  āsave ca sampayuttake ca khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) (1)
