6.3.9--17 Nigaṇṭhasuttādi
Chaṭṭhapaṇṇāsaka
Sikkhāpadapeyyāla
Nigaṇṭhasuttādi
294--302.
1403“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato nigaṇṭho… muṇḍasāvako… jaṭilako… paribbājako… māgaṇḍiko… tedaṇḍiko… āruddhako… gotamako… devadhammiko yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye. Katamehi pañcahi? Pāṇātipātī hoti, adinnādāyī hoti…pe… surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhāyī hoti. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato devadhammiko yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye”ti.
1404Sattarasamaṁ.
1405Sikkhāpadapeyyālaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
