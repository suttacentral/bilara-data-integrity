40.1.8.7 Nakhacchedanānisaṁsa
Pilindavacchavagga
Pilindavacchattheraapadāna
Dānānisaṁsakathā
Nakhacchedanānisaṁsa
112.
4409
Nakhacchedane sugate,
datvā saṁghe gaṇuttame;
Pañcānisaṁse anubhomi,
kammānucchavike mama.
113.
4410
Dāsidāse gavasse ca,
bhatake nāṭake bahū;
Nhāpite bhattake sūde,
sabbattheva labhāmahaṁ.
