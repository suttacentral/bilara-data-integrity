1.1.3 Ākaṅkhamānachakka
Kammakkhandhaka
Tajjanīyakamma
Ākaṅkhamānachakka
6.
36Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgatassa bhikkhuno, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. Bhaṇḍanakārako hoti kalahakārako vivādakārako bhassakārako saṁghe adhikaraṇakārako; bālo hoti abyatto āpattibahulo anapadāno; gihisaṁsaṭṭho viharati ananulomikehi gihisaṁsaggehi—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgatassa bhikkhuno, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. (1)
37Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgatassa bhikkhuno, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. Adhisīle sīlavipanno hoti, ajjhācāre ācāravipanno hoti, atidiṭṭhiyā diṭṭhivipanno hoti—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgatassa bhikkhuno, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. (2)
38Aparehipi, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgatassa bhikkhuno, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. Buddhassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, dhammassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, saṁghassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati—  imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgatassa bhikkhuno, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. (3)
39Tiṇṇaṁ, bhikkhave, bhikkhūnaṁ, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. Eko bhaṇḍanakārako hoti kalahakārako vivādakārako bhassakārako saṁghe adhikaraṇakārako; eko bālo hoti abyatto āpattibahulo anapadāno; eko gihisaṁsaṭṭho viharati ananulomikehi gihisaṁsaggehi—  imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. (4)
40Aparesampi, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. Eko adhisīle sīlavipanno hoti, eko ajjhācāre ācāravipanno hoti, eko atidiṭṭhiyā diṭṭhivipanno hoti—  imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. (5)
41Aparesampi, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. Eko buddhassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, eko dhammassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, eko saṁghassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati—  imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ, ākaṅkhamāno saṁgho, tajjanīyakammaṁ kareyya. (6)
42Ākaṅkhamānachakkaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
