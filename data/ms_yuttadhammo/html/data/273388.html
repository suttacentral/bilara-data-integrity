4.6.1 Adhammakammadvādasaka
Samathakkhandhaka
Tassapāpiyasikā
Adhammakammadvādasaka
208.
861Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgataṁ tassapāpiyasikākammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca. Asammukhā kataṁ hoti, appaṭipucchākataṁ hoti, appaṭiññāya kataṁ hoti…pe… adhammena kataṁ hoti, vaggena kataṁ hoti— imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhaṅgehi samannāgataṁ tassapāpiyasikākammaṁ adhammakammañca hoti, avinayakammañca, duvūpasantañca.
