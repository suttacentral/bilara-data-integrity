243.
915Sanidassanasappaṭigho nahetu dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa nahetussa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
916Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā nava, anantare ekaṁ, samanantare ekaṁ, sahajāte ekavīsa, aññamaññe cha, nissaye ekavīsa, upanissaye tīṇi, purejāte cha, pacchājāte satta, āsevane ekaṁ, kamme vipāke āhāre satta, indriye nava, jhāne magge satta, sampayutte ekaṁ, vippayutte aṭṭha…pe…  avigate pañcavīsa. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
917Nahetuyā pañcavīsa, naārammaṇe ekavīsa. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
918Ārammaṇapaccayā nahetuyā tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
919Nahetupaccayā ārammaṇe tīṇi. (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
920(Yathā kusalattike pañhāvāraṁ, evaṁ vitthāretabbaṁ.)
921Sanidassanasappaṭighattikahetudukaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
