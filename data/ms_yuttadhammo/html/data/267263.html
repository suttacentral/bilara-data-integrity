13.9 Gaṇṭhipupphiyattheraapadāna
Sereyyavagga
Gaṇṭhipupphiyattheraapadāna
91.
2160
“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,
vipassī dakkhiṇāraho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
ārāmā abhinikkhami.
92.
2161
Disvānahaṁ buddhaseṭṭhaṁ,
sabbaññuṁ tamanāsakaṁ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gaṇṭhipupphaṁ apūjayiṁ.
93.
2162
Tena cittappasādena,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
puna vandiṁ tathāgataṁ.
94.
2163
Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṁ pupphamabhiropayiṁ;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
95.
2164
Ekatālīsito kappe,
caraṇo nāma khattiyo;
Sattaratanasampanno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.
96.
2165
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
2166Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā gaṇṭhipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
2167Gaṇṭhipupphiyattherassāpadānaṁ navamaṁ.
