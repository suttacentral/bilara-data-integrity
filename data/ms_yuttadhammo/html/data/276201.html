1.1.5 Saṁkhittadhanasutta
Paṭhamapaṇṇāsaka
Dhanavagga
Saṁkhittadhanasutta
5.
25“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, dhanāni. Katamāni satta? Saddhādhanaṁ, sīladhanaṁ, hirīdhanaṁ, ottappadhanaṁ, sutadhanaṁ, cāgadhanaṁ, paññādhanaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta dhanānīti.
26
Saddhādhanaṁ sīladhanaṁ,
hirī ottappiyaṁ dhanaṁ;
Sutadhanañca cāgo ca,
paññā ve sattamaṁ dhanaṁ.
27
Yassa ete dhanā atthi,
itthiyā purisassa vā;
Adaliddoti taṁ āhu,
amoghaṁ tassa jīvitaṁ.
28
Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,
Pasādaṁ dhammadassanaṁ;
Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,
Saraṁ buddhāna sāsanan”ti.
29Pañcamaṁ.
