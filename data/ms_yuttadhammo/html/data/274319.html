13.1.35--40 Kallitamūlakaārammaṇasuttādichakka
Jhānasaṁyutta
Jhānavagga
Kallitamūlakaārammaṇasuttādichakka
696--701.
1326Sāvatthinidānaṁ… “samādhismiṁ kallitakusalo hoti, na samādhismiṁ ārammaṇakusalo… samādhismiṁ ārammaṇakusalo hoti, na samādhismiṁ kallitakusalo… neva samādhismiṁ kallitakusalo hoti, na ca samādhismiṁ ārammaṇakusalo… samādhismiṁ kallitakusalo ca hoti, samādhismiṁ ārammaṇakusalo ca. Tatra, bhikkhave, yvāyaṁ jhāyī…pe… uttamo ca pavaro cā”ti.
1327Pañcatiṁsatimaṁ.
1328(Purimamūlakāni viya yāva cattālīsamā kallitamūlakasappāyakārīsuttā cha suttāni pūretabbāni. Kallitamūlakaṁ.)
