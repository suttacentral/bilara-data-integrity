6.4.4 Sukkadhammasutta
Lābhasakkārasaṁyutta
Catutthavagga
Sukkadhammasutta
183.
1090Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko. Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūtassa pariyādiṇṇacittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa sukko dhammo samucchedamagamā. Evaṁ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko…pe… sikkhitabban”ti.
1091Catutthaṁ.
