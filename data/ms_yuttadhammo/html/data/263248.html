5.
2636Atīto dhammo paccuppannassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā atītā khandhā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ paccuppannānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. Anulomaṁ gotrabhussa…  anulomaṁ vodānassa…  gotrabhu maggassa…  vodānaṁ maggassa…  maggo phalassa…  phalaṁ phalassa…  anulomaṁ phalasamāpattiyā…  nirodhā vuṭṭhahantassa nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṁ phalasamāpattiyā anantarapaccayena paccayo.
