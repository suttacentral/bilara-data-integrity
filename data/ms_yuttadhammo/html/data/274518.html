1.4.2 Dutiyapaṭipattisutta
Maggasaṁyutta
Paṭipattivagga
Dutiyapaṭipattisutta
32.
119Sāvatthinidānaṁ. “Micchāpaṭipannañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipannañca. Taṁ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipanno? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco micchādiṭṭhiko hoti…pe…  micchāsamādhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipanno. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipanno? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco sammādiṭṭhiko hoti…pe…  sammāsamādhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipanno”ti.
120Dutiyaṁ.
