19.10 Sambahulasīlādisampannabhikkhuvatthu
Dhammaṭṭhavagga
Sambahulasīlādisampannabhikkhuvatthu
271.
292
Na sīlabbatamattena,
bāhusaccena vā pana;
Atha vā samādhilābhena,
vivittasayanena vā.
272.
293
Phusāmi nekkhammasukhaṁ,
aputhujjanasevitaṁ;
Bhikkhu vissāsamāpādi,
appatto āsavakkhayaṁ.
294Dhammaṭṭhavaggo ekūnavīsatimo.
