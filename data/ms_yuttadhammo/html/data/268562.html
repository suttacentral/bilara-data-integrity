“Yathā vanaṁ cittalataṁ pabhāsati,
Uyyānaseṭṭhaṁ tidasānamuttamaṁ;
Tathūpamaṁ tuyhamidaṁ vimānaṁ,
Obhāsayaṁ tiṭṭhati antalikkhe.

Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti.
