310.
4731Niyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā aniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati naārammaṇapaccayā… tīṇi.
4732Niyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā niyyāniko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā— niyyānike khandhe paccayā niyyānikādhipati. (1)
4733Aniyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā aniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā— aniyyānikaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paccayā…pe… (yāva asaññasattā) cakkhāyatanaṁ paccayā cakkhuviññāṇaṁ…pe… kāyāyatanaṁ paccayā kāyaviññāṇaṁ, vatthuṁ paccayā aniyyānikā khandhā. Aniyyānikaṁ dhammaṁ paccayā niyyāniko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā— vatthuṁ paccayā niyyānikādhipati. (2)
4734Niyyānikañca aniyyānikañca dhammaṁ paccayā niyyāniko dhammo uppajjati naadhipatipaccayā— niyyānike khandhe ca vatthuñca paccayā niyyānikādhipati. (1)
