4.3.5 Dhammasutta
Catutthapaṇṇāsaka
Sādhuvagga
Dhammasutta
182.
1333“Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca. Taṁ suṇātha…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo? Pāṇātipāto…pe…  micchādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo.
1334Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī…pe…  sammādiṭṭhi—  ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo”ti.
1335Pañcamaṁ.
