37.8 Ekacampakapupphiyattheraapadāna
Mandāravapupphiyavagga
Ekacampakapupphiyattheraapadāna
27.
4073
“Upasanto ca sambuddho,
vasatī pabbatantare;
Ekacampakamādāya,
upagacchiṁ naruttamaṁ.
28.
4074
Pasannacitto sumano,
paccekamunimuttamaṁ;
Ubhohatthehi paggayha,
pūjayiṁ aparājitaṁ.
29.
4075
Pañcasaṭṭhimhito kappe,
yaṁ pupphamabhipūjayiṁ;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
30.
4076
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe…
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
4077Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā ekacampakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
4078Ekacampakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṁ aṭṭhamaṁ.
