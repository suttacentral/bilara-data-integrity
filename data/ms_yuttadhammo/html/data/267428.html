30.4 Saddasaññakattheraapadāna
Citakapūjakavagga
Saddasaññakattheraapadāna
17.
3380
“Anuggatamhi ādicce,
panādo vipulo ahu;
Buddhaseṭṭhassa lokamhi,
pātubhāvo mahesino.
18.
3381
Ghosamassosahaṁ tattha,
na ca passāmi taṁ jinaṁ;
Maraṇañca anuppatto,
buddhasaññamanussariṁ.
19.
3382
Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṁ saññamalabhiṁ tadā;
Duggatiṁ nābhijānāmi,
buddhapūjāyidaṁ phalaṁ.
20.
3383
Paṭisambhidā catasso,
…pe… 
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ”.
3384Itthaṁ sudaṁ āyasmā saddasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
3385Saddasaññakattherassāpadānaṁ catutthaṁ.
