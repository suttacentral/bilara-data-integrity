Anantara
25.
1668Gantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā ganthā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ ganthānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (1)
1669Gantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā ganthā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ noganthānaṁ khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo; ganthā vuṭṭhānassa anantarapaccayena paccayo. (2)
1670Gantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo—  purimā purimā ganthā pacchimānaṁ pacchimānaṁ ganthānaṁ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṁ anantarapaccayena paccayo. (3)
26.
1671Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Dve āvajjanā kātabbā, paṭhamo natthi.)
1672Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā ganthassa dhammassa anantarapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Ekampi vuṭṭhānaṁ kātabbaṁ, majjhe.)
