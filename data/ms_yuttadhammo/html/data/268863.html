1.1.5 Mahāgovindacariya
Akittivagga
Dānapāramī
Mahāgovindacariya
37.
42
“Punāparaṁ yadā homi,
sattarājapurohito;
Pūjito naradevehi,
mahāgovindabrāhmaṇo.
38.
43
Tadāhaṁ sattarajjesu,
Yaṁ me āsi upāyanaṁ;
Tena demi mahādānaṁ,
Akkhobbhaṁ sāgarūpamaṁ.
39.
44
Na me dessaṁ dhanaṁ dhaññaṁ,
Napi natthi nicayo mayi;
Sabbaññutaṁ piyaṁ mayhaṁ,
Tasmā demi varaṁ dhanan”ti.
45Mahāgovindacariyaṁ pañcamaṁ.
