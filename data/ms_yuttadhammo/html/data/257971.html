29 Sanidassanattika, Cūḷantaraduka
Sanidassanattika, Cūḷantaraduka
63.
149Nasanidassanasappaṭigho nasappaccayo dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa sappaccayassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā upanissaye tīṇi.
64.
150Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nasanidassanaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho sanidassano dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca…pe…  avigate pañca.
151Naanidassanaappaṭigho nanasanidassano dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa nasanidassanassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.
65.
152Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nasappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho sappaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate nava.
153Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ naappaṭighaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho appaṭigho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
66.
154Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ narūpiṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho rūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
155Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ naarūpiṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassanaappaṭigho arūpī dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
67.
156Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nalokiyaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  satta.
157Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nalokuttaraṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
158Naanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nalokiyaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca anidassanasappaṭigho lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  satta. Hetuyā ekavīsa.
159Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nalokiyañca naanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nalokiyañca dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho lokiyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  satta. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa…pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
160Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nalokuttaraṁ dhammaṁ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho lokuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā tīṇi…pe…  avigate tīṇi.
68.
161Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nakenaci viññeyyaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho kenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañcatiṁsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe pannarasa…pe…  avigate pañcatiṁsa.
162Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṁ nanakenaci viññeyyaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho nakenaci viññeyyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṁkhittaṁ.) Hetuyā pañcatiṁsa, ārammaṇe tīṇi…pe…  aññamaññe pannarasa…pe…  avigate pañcatiṁsa.
