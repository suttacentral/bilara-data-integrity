9.2.1.4 Suññatasamādhi
Asaṅkhatasaṁyutta
Dutiyavagga
Asaṅkhatasutta
Suññatasamādhi
1699Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Suññato samādhi. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Animitto samādhi. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…  katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Appaṇihito samādhi. Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe… . (1.6--8)
