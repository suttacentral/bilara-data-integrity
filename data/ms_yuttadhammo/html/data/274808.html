4.7.5 Paṭhamaphalasutta
Indriyasaṁyutta
Bodhipakkhiyavagga
Paṭhamaphalasutta
535.
1238“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṁ…pe…  paññindriyaṁ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ indriyānaṁ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā dvinnaṁ phalānaṁ aññataraṁ phalaṁ pāṭikaṅkhaṁ—  diṭṭheva dhamme aññā, sati vā upādisese anāgāmitā”ti.
1239Pañcamaṁ.
