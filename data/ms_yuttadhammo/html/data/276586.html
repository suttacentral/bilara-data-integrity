1.7.6.5 Sāmisasikkhāpada
Mahāvibhaṅga
Sekhiyakaṇḍa
Surusuruvagga
Sāmisasikkhāpada
631.
1960Tena samayena buddho bhagavā bhaggesu viharati susumāragire bhesakaḷāvane migadāye. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhū kokanade pāsāde sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggaṇhanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma samaṇā sakyaputtiyā sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggahessanti, seyyathāpi gihī kāmabhogino”ti.
1961Assosuṁ kho bhikkhū tesaṁ manussānaṁ ujjhāyantānaṁ khiyyantānaṁ vipācentānaṁ. Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe… te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti— “kathañhi nāma bhikkhū sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggahessantī”ti…pe… “saccaṁ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhū sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggaṇhantī”ti? “Saccaṁ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā…pe… kathañhi nāma te, bhikkhave, moghapurisā sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggahessanti. Netaṁ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṁ vā pasādāya…pe… evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṁ sikkhāpadaṁ uddiseyyātha—
1962“Na sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggahessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (55:200)
1963Na sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālako paṭiggahetabbo. Yo anādariyaṁ paṭicca sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaṁ paṭiggaṇhāti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
1964Anāpatti— asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, “dhovissāmī”ti vā “dhovāpessāmī”ti vā paṭiggaṇhāti, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.
1965Pañcamasikkhāpadaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.
