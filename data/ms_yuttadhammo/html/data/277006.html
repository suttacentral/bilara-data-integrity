1.2.2.7 Anattaniyasutta
Khandhasaṁyutta
Majjhimapaṇṇāsaka
Arahantavagga
Anattaniyasutta
69.
364Sāvatthinidānaṁ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu…pe… ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca— “sādhu me, bhante, bhagavā saṁkhittena dhammaṁ desetu…pe… vihareyyan”ti. “Yaṁ kho, bhikkhu, anattaniyaṁ; tatra te chando pahātabbo”ti. “Aññātaṁ, bhagavā, aññātaṁ, sugatā”ti.
365“Yathā kathaṁ pana tvaṁ, bhikkhu, mayā saṁkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṁ ājānāsī”ti? “Rūpaṁ kho, bhante, anattaniyaṁ; tatra me chando pahātabbo. Vedanā… saññā… saṅkhārā… viññāṇaṁ anattaniyaṁ; tatra me chando pahātabbo. Imassa khvāhaṁ, bhante, bhagavatā saṁkhittena bhāsitassa evaṁ vitthārena atthaṁ ājānāmī”ti.
366“Sādhu sādhu, bhikkhu. Sādhu kho tvaṁ, bhikkhu, mayā saṁkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṁ ājānāsi. Rūpaṁ kho, bhikkhu, anattaniyaṁ; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Vedanā … saññā… saṅkhārā… viññāṇaṁ anattaniyaṁ; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Imassa kho, bhikkhu, mayā saṁkhittena bhāsitassa evaṁ vitthārena attho daṭṭhabbo”ti…pe… aññataro ca pana so bhikkhu arahataṁ ahosīti.
367Sattamaṁ.
