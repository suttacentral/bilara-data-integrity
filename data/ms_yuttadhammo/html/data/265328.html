3051Cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānañca nocittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānañca dhammaṁ saṁsaṭṭho cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā— cittasaṁsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṁ ekaṁ khandhañca cittañca saṁsaṭṭhā dve khandhā, dve khandhe…pe… paṭisandhikkhaṇe…pe…. (1) (Saṁkhittaṁ.)
