2.5.9 Sandhitattheragāthā
Dukanipāta
Pañcamavagga
Sandhitattheragāthā
217.
426
“Assatthe haritobhāse,
saṁvirūḷhamhi pādape;
Ekaṁ buddhagataṁ saññaṁ,
alabhitthaṁ patissato.
218.
427
Ekatiṁse ito kappe,
yaṁ saññamalabhiṁ tadā;
Tassā saññāya vāhasā,
patto me āsavakkhayo”ti.
428…  Sandhito thero… .
Vaggo pañcamo.
429Tassuddānaṁ
430
Kumārakassapo thero,
dhammapālo ca brahmāli;
Mogharājā visākho ca,
cūḷako ca anūpamo;
Vajjito sandhito thero,
kilesarajavāhanoti.
431Dukanipāto niṭṭhito.
432Tatruddānaṁ
433
Gāthādukanipātamhi,
navuti ceva aṭṭha ca;
Therā ekūnapaññāsaṁ,
bhāsitā nayakovidāti.
