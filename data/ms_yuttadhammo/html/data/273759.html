4.1.9 Paṭhamaāyusutta
Mārasaṁyutta
Paṭhamavagga
Paṭhamaāyusutta
145.
808Evaṁ me sutaṁ—  ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ. Bhagavā etadavoca— 
809“Appamidaṁ, bhikkhave, manussānaṁ āyu. Gamanīyo samparāyo, kattabbaṁ kusalaṁ, caritabbaṁ brahmacariyaṁ. Natthi jātassa amaraṇaṁ. Yo, bhikkhave, ciraṁ jīvati, so vassasataṁ appaṁ vā bhiyyo”ti.
810Atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi— 
811
“Dīghamāyu manussānaṁ,
na naṁ hīḷe suporiso;
Careyya khīramattova,
natthi maccussa āgamo”ti.
812
“Appamāyu manussānaṁ,
hīḷeyya naṁ suporiso;
Careyyādittasīsova,
natthi maccussa nāgamo”ti.
813Atha kho māro…pe…  tatthevantaradhāyīti.
