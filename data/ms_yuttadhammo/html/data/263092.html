6.
1884Parittaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  parittaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ, dve khandhe…pe…  ekaṁ mahābhūtaṁ paṭicca tayo mahābhūtā…pe…  mahābhūte paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ upādārūpaṁ. (1)
1885Mahaggataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca mahaggato dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggataṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1886Mahaggataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggate khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (2)
1887Mahaggataṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto ca mahaggato ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  mahaggataṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
1888Appamāṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca appamāṇo dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (1)
1889Appamāṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto dhammo uppajjati adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇe khandhe paṭicca cittasamuṭṭhānaṁ rūpaṁ. (2)
1890Appamāṇaṁ dhammaṁ paṭicca paritto ca appamāṇo ca dhammā uppajjanti adhipatipaccayā—  appamāṇaṁ ekaṁ khandhaṁ paṭicca tayo khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānañca rūpaṁ…pe…  dve khandhe…pe… . (3)
